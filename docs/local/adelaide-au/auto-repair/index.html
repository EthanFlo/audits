The three fixes on 80% of audited auto repair shop profiles

  • Your Google Business Profile Description is Selling You Short

    Many auto repair shops in Adelaide miss out by using a brief, generic business description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles are under 400. This is a lost opportunity to detail your specialist services like European car diagnostics, 4WD expertise, or specific vehicle makes you service. A comprehensive description builds immediate trust and relevance for local drivers searching for specialised help.

  • Missing Out on Key Service Searches

    Your primary category might be "Auto Repair Shop," but are you using secondary categories to capture specific service searches? Many Adelaide auto businesses neglect to add crucial categories like "Oil Change Service," "Brake Shop," or "Tyre Shop." Without these, Google struggles to connect your business with highly specific customer queries, causing you to lose visibility for common and profitable services in your local area.

  • Failing to Showcase Your Expertise Visually

    Customers value transparency and professionalism, especially when choosing an auto repair shop. Profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos often fail to convey this. Missing visuals include clear shots of your clean workshop, advanced diagnostic equipment, friendly mechanics at work, comfortable waiting area, and the exterior of your Adelaide shop. High-quality photos significantly boost engagement and build confidence before a customer even calls.
