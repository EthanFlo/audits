The three fixes on 80% of audited tattoo artist profiles

  • Your Google Business Profile Description is Too Short

    Many Ottawa tattoo artists use a generic, short business description, missing out on crucial keyword opportunities. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles barely scratch 400. This is valuable space to detail your signature styles, studio vibe, hygiene standards, and what makes your art unique, directly appealing to clients searching for specific experiences.

  • Missing Niche-Specific Secondary Categories

    You might be listed as a "Tattoo Shop," but are you also telling Google you're a "Fine Line Tattoo Artist," a "Traditional Tattoo Artist," or a "Cover-Up Tattoo Specialist?" Failing to add these secondary categories means you're invisible to potential clients in Ottawa searching for very specific styles, limiting your reach to highly targeted, ready-to-book customers.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Your Google Business Profile should be a visual portfolio. Beyond just finished tattoos, many artists miss uploading photos of their clean, inviting studio space, artists at work (with client permission), flash designs, or even the consultation process. These diverse images build trust, showcase your professional environment, and give potential clients a comprehensive feel for your Ottawa studio before they even step inside.
