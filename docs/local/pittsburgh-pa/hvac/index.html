The three fixes on 80% of audited hvac contractor profiles

  • Your Business Description Isn't Working Hard Enough

    Many Pittsburgh HVAC contractors leave their Google Business Profile description short, missing out on crucial keyword opportunities. Google allows up to 750 characters, but often we see profiles barely reaching 200. This means you're not detailing your 24/7 emergency service, the specific brands you repair, or unique offerings like indoor air quality solutions, which stops potential customers from understanding your full capabilities right away.

  • Crucial Secondary Categories Are Overlooked

    Beyond "HVAC Contractor," many businesses in Pittsburgh miss out on adding vital secondary categories. These tell Google exactly what specific services you offer, directly impacting which searches you appear for. Common culprits include failing to add "Air Conditioning Repair Service," "Furnace Repair Service," and "Duct Cleaning Service." Without them, you're invisible to customers searching for those exact needs.

  • Your Profile Lacks Trust-Building Visuals

    A surprising number of Pittsburgh HVAC profiles have fewer than 10 owner-uploaded photos, let alone the recommended 30+. People want to see who they're hiring. Missing are photos of your certified technicians in uniform, your branded service vehicles, completed installations, the different types of furnaces or AC units you work on, and even your friendly office staff. Visuals build trust and credibility before the first call.
