The three fixes on 80% of audited optometrist profiles

Business Info

  • ☐ Verify your exact business name matches official registration and signage for consistency.
  • ☐ Ensure your primary phone number is direct and monitored during business hours.
  • ☐ Confirm your website URL is current, functional, and links directly to your practice site.
  • ☐ Update your address for precise pin placement on Google Maps, correcting any minor errors.
  • ☐ Specify accurate operating hours, including any holiday closures or special clinic days.

Categories

  • ☐ Set "Optometrist" as your primary business category for maximum relevance.
  • ☐ Add secondary categories like "Eye Care Center" and "Contact Lens Supplier."
  • ☐ Include "Pediatric Optometrist" if you specialize in children's eye care.
  • ☐ Add "Eyeglass Store" if you dispense frames and lenses on-site.
  • ☐ Review all chosen categories annually to ensure they still accurately reflect services.

Photos

  • ☐ Upload a high-resolution exterior photo showcasing your practice's entrance clearly.
  • ☐ Add interior photos of your reception area, exam rooms, and optical dispensary.
  • ☐ Include professional headshots of all practicing optometrists and key staff members.
  • ☐ Post photos of specific equipment like OCT scanners or autorefractors.
  • ☐ Feature images demonstrating services, e.g., an optometrist performing an Eye Exam.

Services

  • ☐ List "Comprehensive Eye Exam" as a distinct service with a detailed description.
  • ☐ Create a dedicated service for "Contact Lens Fitting and Evaluation."
  • ☐ Add "Pediatric Eye Care" emphasizing your approach to children’s vision.
  • ☐ Detail other specialized services like "Dry Eye Management" or "Glaucoma Screening."
  • ☐ Ensure all services include relevant keywords patients use when searching.

Reviews

  • ☐ Respond promptly and professionally to all new patient reviews, both positive and negative.
  • ☐ Implement a consistent strategy to solicit new 5-star reviews from satisfied patients.
  • ☐ Address any negative feedback constructively, offering solutions or inviting private contact.
  • ☐ Encourage patients to mention specific services like "Eye Exam" or "Contact Lens Fitting" in reviews.
  • ☐ Avoid using template responses; personalize each review reply whenever possible.

Posts

  • ☐ Create a "What's New" post announcing seasonal promotions on frames or contact lenses.
  • ☐ Publish an "Event" post for upcoming patient education seminars on eye health.
  • ☐ Use an "Offer" post to highlight a discount on a second pair of glasses.
  • ☐ Share an "Update" post about new diagnostic technology or a new optometrist joining the practice.
  • ☐ Post consistently, at least once a week, to keep your profile active and engaging.

Why these matter specifically for optometrists in Checklist

Once you’ve diligently worked through all 30 points on this checklist, your work isn't done – it's just beginning. The immediate next step is to access your Google Business Profile Insights and establish a baseline for key metrics like searches, views, and calls. Wait a minimum of 14 days for Google's algorithms to fully process and reflect your comprehensive updates. After this period, revisit your Insights, compare them to your baseline, and then systematically repeat this checklist, identifying areas for further refinement and continuous improvement to maintain your competitive edge.
