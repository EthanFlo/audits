The three fixes on 80% of audited hvac contractor profiles

  • Incomplete Business Descriptions Limiting Reach

    Many Dublin HVAC contractors use less than half of the 750 characters Google allows for their business description. This often means missing crucial keywords like "boiler repair Dublin," "heat pump installation," or "emergency HVAC service." A concise, keyword-rich description helps Google understand your full range of services, improving your visibility for specific queries and differentiating you from competitors right across the city.

  • Neglecting Crucial Secondary Categories

    While "HVAC Contractor" is essential, many local profiles stop there. Google allows up to nine additional categories to specify your services. For Dublin HVAC businesses, I frequently find missing vital secondary categories like "Air Conditioning Repair Service," "Heating Contractor," and "Duct Cleaning Service." This omission means you’re not showing up for highly relevant, targeted searches that customers perform daily.

  • Insufficient Visual Content (Under 30 Photos)

    Customers actively seek visual reassurance, yet many HVAC profiles in Dublin have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. Missing are images of your team at work, branded vans across different Dublin neighbourhoods, completed installations, your office/showroom, and before-and-after shots of repairs or upgrades. These visuals build trust and help potential clients envision your professional service quality before they even call.
