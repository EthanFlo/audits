The three fixes on 80% of audited chiropractor profiles

  • Your Google Business Profile Description is Too Short

    Many Wellington chiropractors unknowingly limit their reach by using a brief, unoptimized business description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles I see barely scratch 200. This is a crucial missed opportunity to highlight your unique services – perhaps family wellness, sports injury recovery, or posture correction – and connect with potential patients searching for specific care in the Wellington region. A longer, keyword-rich description helps Google understand your specialisations better.

  • You're Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Simply listing "Chiropractor" as your primary category isn't enough in competitive Wellington. Google allows multiple secondary categories, yet many practices leave these blank. Imagine a patient searching for "Sports Chiropractor Wellington" or "Family Chiropractor NZ." Without these specific tags like "Sports Chiropractor," "Auto Accident Chiropractor," or "Family Chiropractor," your profile won't show up, giving your competitors a direct advantage for these niche searches and specific patient needs.

  • Not Enough High-Quality Owner Photos

    Your Google Business Profile is visually underperforming with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. Generic stock images or a handful of exterior shots don't tell your story effectively. Patients in Wellington want to see your friendly team, clean treatment rooms, advanced equipment, and even photos demonstrating specific techniques. High-quality images of your clinic's interior, reception, adjusting rooms, and your team in action build trust and give a clearer picture of what to expect, encouraging more visits.
