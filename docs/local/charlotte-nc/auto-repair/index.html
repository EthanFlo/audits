The three fixes on 80% of audited auto repair shop profiles

SECTION 2 — THREE FINDINGS

  • Your Google Business Description is Too Short

    Many Charlotte auto repair shops use less than 400 characters for their Google Business Profile description, missing a huge opportunity. Google allows 750 characters, letting you detail everything from specialized diagnostics to suspension work, engine repair, and even specific car brands you service. Expanding this space with keyword-rich details helps Google understand your full service offering, making your shop more relevant for a wider range of local search queries beyond just "auto repair near me."

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Service Categories

    Often, auto repair shops only list "Auto Repair Shop" as their primary category. This overlooks the power of secondary categories. Google offers highly specific options like "Oil Change Service," "Brake Shop," and "Tire Shop," among many others. Adding these tells Google exactly what services you excel at, helping your Charlotte shop appear in more targeted searches, such as when a driver specifically needs a "tire rotation" or "brake inspection" rather than a general repair.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Most auto repair profiles lack sufficient high-quality, owner-uploaded photos. Customers want to see your clean bays, your diagnostic equipment in action, your friendly team members, and even specific parts being repaired. Photos of your waiting area, certified technicians at work, or examples of brake pads and new tires installed can build trust and show professionalism. More photos mean more engagement and a stronger signal to Google that your Charlotte business is active and credible.
