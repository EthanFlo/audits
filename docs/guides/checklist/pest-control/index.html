30-point Google Business Profile checklist for pest controls

This 30-point Google Business Profile checklist is specifically designed for pest control companies seeking to dominate their local search results. Unlike generic SEO advice that often leaves businesses wondering where to start, this guide provides a precise, actionable sequence of steps. Each item focuses on practical optimizations that directly impact visibility for services like Termite Control, Rodent Removal, and Bed Bug Extermination.

Imagine a slow afternoon where your team isn't out on calls. This checklist offers the exact roadmap to leverage that time, ensuring every facet of your Google Business Profile is meticulously optimized. By methodically working through these 30 points, pest control businesses can significantly enhance their local search rankings, attract more high-quality leads, and ultimately grow their client base by making sure potential customers find *them* first.

The three fixes on 80% of audited pest control profiles

Business Info

  • ☐ Verify your business name, address, and phone (NAP) are 100% accurate and consistent across all platforms.
  • ☐ Ensure your primary and secondary business hours, including special holiday hours, are always up-to-date.
  • ☐ Write a compelling business description (up to 750 characters) highlighting your pest control expertise and service area.
  • ☐ Add your precise service area(s) by specific zip codes or cities, not just a broad radius.
  • ☐ Confirm your website link points to an active, mobile-friendly landing page relevant to pest control services.

Categories

  • ☐ Set "Pest control service" as your primary category to accurately represent your core business.
  • ☐ Add secondary categories like "Termite exterminator" or "Rodent exterminator" if they represent significant services.
  • ☐ Include "Wildlife control service" if you regularly handle nuisance animals beyond typical insect pests.
  • ☐ Ensure you have a relevant category for every major service offered, e.g., "Bed bug exterminator."
  • ☐ Review competitor categories to identify any relevant, high-performing options you might be missing.

Photos

  • ☐ Upload at least five high-resolution photos of your team actively performing pest control services.
  • ☐ Add clear, well-lit photos of your branded vehicles and equipment to build trust and professionalism.
  • ☐ Include a high-quality headshot of the business owner or manager to personalize your profile.
  • ☐ Showcase before-and-after photos of successful pest removal (e.g., cleaned-up rodent droppings, ant trails).
  • ☐ Post photos of your office exterior and interior, if applicable, for businesses with a physical location.

Services

  • ☐ List all individual pest control services with detailed descriptions, including Termite Control and Rodent Removal.
  • ☐ Create specific service entries for Bed Bug Extermination, Mosquito Treatment, and Cockroach Extermination.
  • ☐ Add unique descriptions for each service, outlining your methods, benefits, and local expertise.
  • ☐ Include pricing or a clear call to action for a quote within service descriptions where appropriate.
  • ☐ Ensure service areas are clearly defined for each offering, especially for specialized treatments.

Reviews

  • ☐ Implement a consistent strategy for actively requesting new reviews from satisfied pest control customers.
  • ☐ Respond promptly and professionally to every single review, positive or negative, within 24-48 hours.
  • ☐ Address negative reviews constructively, offering solutions and moving sensitive discussions offline.
  • ☐ Encourage customers to mention specific services like "termite inspection" or "bed bug treatment" in their reviews.
  • ☐ Analyze review trends to identify areas for service improvement and highlight your strengths in pest control.

Posts

  • ☐ Create at least one "What's New" post weekly about recent pest control jobs or seasonal tips.
  • ☐ Publish "Offer" posts for current promotions on services like rodent removal or termite prevention.
  • ☐ Share "Event" posts for any local community involvement or educational pest control seminars.
  • ☐ Use "Product" posts to highlight specific eco-friendly treatments or advanced pest control technologies.
  • ☐ Include relevant, geo-targeted keywords and a strong call-to-action in every post for maximum impact.

Why these matter specifically for pest controls in Checklist

After meticulously checking off all 30 points, the work isn't over—it's just beginning. The immediate next step is to monitor your Google Business Profile Insights over the next 14-30 days to track improvements in views, calls, website clicks, and direction requests. Pay close attention to search queries driving traffic to your profile, identifying new opportunities for service expansion or content creation around specific pest issues like "attic rodent removal" or "preventative termite treatment." After this initial measurement period, schedule a recurring monthly or quarterly review to repeat this checklist, ensuring your profile remains optimized, current, and competitively sharp against other pest control services in your area.
