The three fixes on 80% of audited chiropractor profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Edmonton chiropractors use only a sentence or two for their Google Business Profile description, leaving hundreds of characters unused. This misses a critical opportunity to elaborate on your unique approach, specialized techniques like spinal decompression or active release therapy, your patient focus (e.g., pediatric, geriatric, athletes), and what truly sets your clinic apart from others in the city. A detailed description helps potential patients understand your expertise before they even click to call.

  • Insufficient Service Categories

    Most chiropractic clinics default to just "Chiropractor" as their primary and often only category. This overlooks vital secondary categories that capture specific patient searches in Edmonton. We often find profiles missing crucial additions like "Sports Chiropractor," "Auto Accident Chiropractor," and "Family Chiropractor." These specialized categories significantly broaden your visibility to patients actively seeking help for particular conditions or demographics, making your clinic more discoverable.

  • Lack of Engaging Photos

    Profiles often fall short of the recommended 30+ owner-uploaded photos, hindering visual engagement. Clinics frequently lack high-quality images of their modern reception area, comfortable treatment rooms, or specialized equipment. Crucially, photos of the chiropractic team, smiling staff, patient success stories (with consent), and exterior shots (for easy clinic identification) are often missing. Visuals build trust and help patients feel comfortable before their first visit.
