The three fixes on 80% of audited moving company profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Sacramento moving companies miss a massive opportunity by leaving their Google Business Profile description at only a few hundred characters. Google allows 750 characters, yet most profiles I audit barely scratch 300. This space is crucial for detailing your specific services like residential moves, commercial relocations, packing services, or specialty item transport, and highlighting what makes your Sacramento team stand out. You are missing chances to include keywords customers search for.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Your primary category might be "Moving Company," but Google offers many highly relevant secondary categories that are frequently overlooked. During audits, I often find profiles that could greatly benefit from additional categories like "Long Distance Movers," "Office Relocation Service," or "Piano Mover." These precise additions signal to Google exactly what specialized services your Sacramento moving company provides, significantly improving visibility for niche searches.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A significant number of moving company profiles lack sufficient high-quality, owner-uploaded photos. Google recommends at least 30, but many Sacramento movers have fewer than 10. You are missing vital opportunities to showcase your clean, well-maintained trucks, professional crew in uniform, specialized equipment in action, or even your local office space. Visual content builds trust and helps customers envision your reliable service before they even call.
