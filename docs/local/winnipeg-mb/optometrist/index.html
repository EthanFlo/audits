The three fixes on 80% of audited optometrist profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many optometrists in Winnipeg underutilize the Google Business Profile description, often leaving it under 400 characters when 750 are available. This means you're missing a critical opportunity to detail your specialized services like dry eye treatment, advanced diagnostic technology, or your commitment to pediatric vision care. A concise yet comprehensive description is vital to differentiate your clinic and attract patients searching for specific eye care needs in the city.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    A common oversight is failing to add relevant secondary categories beyond just "Optometrist." These categories are crucial for discoverability when patients search for specific services. I often find clinics in Winnipeg are missing categories like "Eye Exam," "Contact Lens Fitting," or "Pediatric Eye Care." Adding these accurately helps Google understand the breadth of your services, connecting you with patients actively seeking those exact offerings.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Visual content is paramount, yet many optometrist profiles in Winnipeg lack a robust photo gallery. A profile with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos often means key visuals are missing. Patients want to see your modern exam rooms, friendly staff, the welcoming reception area, and even close-ups of specific diagnostic equipment. High-quality, diverse photos build trust and give potential patients a clear sense of your clinic's atmosphere before they even visit.
