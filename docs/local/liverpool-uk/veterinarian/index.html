The three fixes on 80% of audited veterinarian profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many Liverpool vet practices miss a crucial opportunity by using a Google Business Profile description under the recommended 750 characters. A short description often fails to highlight your unique services, such as advanced surgical procedures, specialist exotic pet care, or dedicated dental suites. Expanding this section allows you to clearly communicate your practice's full value and expertise, attracting pet owners specifically searching for those niche services in Liverpool.

  • Missing Key Service Categories

    Beyond the primary 'Veterinarian' category, many practices overlook adding secondary categories that significantly improve local search visibility. You might be missing out on clients searching specifically for an 'Emergency Vet', an 'Animal Hospital', or 'Pet Surgery'. Adding these precise categories ensures your practice appears for a broader range of high-intent searches, helping pet owners in areas like Aigburth or Anfield find your specialized care.

  • Lack of Engaging Visuals

    Google Business Profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos often struggle to build trust and connection with potential clients. Pet owners want to see more than just your logo; they want to see your friendly team, clean waiting areas, modern examination rooms, and perhaps even happy patient interactions. High-quality photos of your facilities, staff, and happy pets can make your Liverpool clinic feel welcoming and professional long before a client steps through the door.
