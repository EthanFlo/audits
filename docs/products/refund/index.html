How to request a refund

  1. Email florendo.ethan+flo@gmail.com within 14 days of purchase.
  2. Subject line: "Refund request"
  3. Include the email you used at purchase and the product name.
  4. You'll receive confirmation within 1 business day. Refund typically processes in 2-5 business days via your original payment method.

What happens after refund

  • The refund is processed to your original payment method by Gumroad or the marketplace used at checkout.
  • Your access to the product is revoked.
  • You must delete the product files from your devices.
  • The license terminates per the terms.

Why we offer this

Most refund-buyers tell us the product wasn't a fit for their workflow — not that it didn't work. We'd rather you find a system that fits than struggle with ours.

The 14-day window is real. We honor it without interrogation, save attempts, or "are you sure?" emails.

Edge cases

  • Marketplace purchases (Gumroad / Whop / Etsy): the marketplace's refund policy applies first. If they decline, contact us — we honor refunds even on marketplace purchases within the 14-day window.
  • After 14 days: refunds are at our discretion. Reach out and explain — we're a small operation and we treat each case on its merits.
  • Partial use: a refund is full, even if you've already used part of the product.
  • Multiple refund requests from same buyer: after 2 refunded purchases from the same email, we may decline future purchases. Not punitive — just realistic.

What's NOT refundable

  • Custom services or 1:1 calls (none offered yet, but if added in the future).
  • Founding-member purchases past 14 days (founding pricing was a discount in exchange for honest feedback; we honor refunds within 14 days, but not after).

Questions before you buy?

Email florendo.ethan+flo@gmail.com. We answer pre-purchase questions to help you decide if the product fits — that's better for both of us than a refund later.
