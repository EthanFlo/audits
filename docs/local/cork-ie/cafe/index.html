The three fixes on 80% of audited cafe profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many Cork cafe owners miss a huge opportunity by only using a fraction of the 750 characters Google allows for their business description. Often, I see profiles with a generic "We serve coffee and food" which tells potential customers almost nothing. A detailed description is your chance to highlight your unique blend, fresh local ingredients, cosy ambiance, or signature pastries to stand out in a crowded market.

  • Missing Secondary Categories

    While "Cafe" is your primary category, many profiles stop there. Google allows you to add several secondary categories, significantly broadening your reach. I often find cafes missing crucial additions like "Coffee Shop," "Brunch Restaurant," or "Bakery," which are exactly what people search for in Cork when planning their day. Adding these ensures you appear in more relevant local searches.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A picture is worth a thousand words, especially for cafes. Many profiles have less than 30 owner-uploaded photos, meaning potential customers can't truly experience your space. I often find profiles lacking inviting interior shots, close-ups of specific dishes like your famous scones, barista action, or vibrant outdoor seating areas, all vital for enticing those looking for a specific vibe or meal.
