The three fixes on 80% of audited landscaper profiles

  • Short-Changed Business Description

    Many Calgary landscaper profiles neglect the full 750 characters available for their business description, often stopping far short of the potential 400+ mark. This misses a vital chance to detail crucial services like bespoke patio installations, weekly lawn care packages, expert tree pruning, or even specialized snow removal for commercial properties. A comprehensive description allows your profile to rank for a wider array of specific customer searches, clearly communicating your full scope of work to potential clients.

  • Neglected Niche Categories

    A common oversight for landscapers in Calgary is failing to utilize secondary Google Business Profile categories. While "Landscaper" is essential, leaving out categories like "Lawn Care Service," "Hardscaping Contractor," or "Tree Service" significantly limits your reach. These specific categories help your business appear for highly targeted searches, ensuring you capture clients looking for precise services rather than a generic landscaping solution, maximizing your local search visibility.

  • Lackluster Photo Gallery

    Most landscaper profiles in Calgary have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, which is a significant missed opportunity. Your Google Business Profile should showcase a rich visual portfolio. This includes missing "before and after" shots of dramatic transformations, examples of various hardscaping projects (walkways, retaining walls), diverse garden designs, your team in action, or even specific seasonal work like immaculate spring clean-ups or winter pruning projects. Visuals build trust and demonstrate capability.

Why these matter specifically for landscapers in Calgary, AB

These seemingly small fixes matter immensely for Calgary landscapers due to our unique market. Calgary has a highly competitive landscaping sector, from established firms to independent contractors, all vying for local visibility. Furthermore, Calgary’s sprawling urban layout and distinct quadrants mean residents often search for landscapers "near me" or within specific neighbourhoods like "landscaping South Calgary" or "lawn care NW Calgary." An unoptimized profile, missing key categories or services, simply won't appear in these crucial hyper-local searches, effectively ceding local clients to competitors who have perfected their profile for precise geographic targeting.

Landscaping in Calgary is also incredibly seasonal and project-based. Homeowners intensely search for specific services like spring yard clean-up in April, patio installation contractors in June, or fall leaf removal in October. A fully optimized profile, detailing all your offerings and showcasing relevant photos, ensures you capture these timely, high-intent searches year-round, turning seasonal fluctuations into consistent client acquisition.

Finally, trust and visual proof are paramount for Calgary homeowners investing in their outdoor spaces. A Google Business Profile rich with numerous, high-quality photos — demonstrating your specific expertise in local plant choices, hardscape materials suitable for our climate, or unique garden designs — builds immediate credibility. This visual reassurance often compels clients to choose you over a competitor with a sparse or generic photo gallery, particularly when they can see examples relevant to Calgary's landscape.
