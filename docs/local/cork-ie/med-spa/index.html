The three fixes on 80% of audited med spa profiles

  • Your Business Description is Limiting Your Reach

    Many med spa profiles in Cork underutilize the Google Business Profile description. Often, I find descriptions are under 400 characters, significantly less than the 750 Google allows. This missed opportunity means you're not including crucial keywords for services like advanced facials, dermal fillers, or body contouring, preventing potential clients from finding your specific treatments when they search locally.

  • Overlooking Crucial Secondary Categories

    A significant number of Cork med spas only select one or two primary categories, failing to add vital secondary ones. Without categories like "Botox clinic," "Laser hair removal service," or "Microneedling service," Google struggles to understand the full breadth of your offerings. This directly impacts your visibility for specific, high-intent searches that potential clients in Cork are making.

  • Sparse Photo Gallery Deters Potential Clients

    A common issue is fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, leaving your profile looking incomplete. Med spas thrive on visual trust. I often see profiles missing high-quality images of inviting treatment rooms, professional staff, product displays, and crucially, tasteful before-and-after client results (with consent), all essential for showcasing the quality and atmosphere of your Cork establishment.
