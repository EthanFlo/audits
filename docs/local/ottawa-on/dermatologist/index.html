The three fixes on 80% of audited dermatologist profiles

  • Your Google Business Profile Description is Selling You Short

    Many Ottawa dermatologists leave valuable online real estate on the table by using a brief, unoptimized description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet I often see profiles with fewer than 400. This is a missed opportunity to showcase your specialties like advanced acne treatment, detailed mole checks, or specific cosmetic injectables. A comprehensive description helps Google understand your full range of expertise and matches you with relevant local patients actively searching for those precise services.

  • You're Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Beyond "Dermatologist," many profiles in Ottawa are failing to utilize important secondary categories. These tell Google exactly what specific services you offer, helping you appear for niche searches. I commonly see profiles omitting key categories like "Cosmetic Dermatology," "Skin Cancer Screening Service," or "Pediatric Dermatology." Without these, you might be overlooked by patients actively seeking these precise services, limiting your visibility and patient acquisition potential in the Ottawa area.

  • Your Profile Lacks Engaging Visuals

    First impressions matter, and a photo-poor Google Business Profile can deter potential patients. Many Ottawa dermatologists have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, missing opportunities to showcase their practice. Patients want to see your modern clinic, friendly staff, clean treatment rooms, and perhaps even appropriate before-and-after results (with consent). High-quality images of your waiting area, specialized equipment, and welcoming team build trust and professionalism, significantly improving your profile's appeal and conversion rates for local searches.
