The three fixes on 80% of audited law firm profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many Johannesburg law firms underuse their Google Business Profile description, often staying under 400 characters when Google allows 750. This is a critical missed opportunity to showcase your firm's unique specialisations, values, and key differentiators. A concise description fails to incorporate valuable keywords related to your practice areas—like 'criminal defence attorney Sandton' or 'divorce lawyer Roodepoort'—which drastically limits your profile's discoverability for specific, high-intent searches from local clients.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Your primary Google Business Profile category gets you in the game, but secondary categories are what win you specific clients. I often find law firms in Johannesburg only listing "Law Firm" as their sole category. This neglects vital specific categories like "Personal Injury Lawyer," "Family Law Attorney," or "Estate Planning Lawyer." Without these, you're invisible to potential clients searching for exact legal services, allowing competitors who have correctly categorised their profiles to dominate relevant local search results.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A surprising number of law firms have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, significantly impacting their visual appeal and trustworthiness. Potential clients want to see who they're entrusting with sensitive matters. Missing photos often include professional team headshots, welcoming office interiors and exteriors in Johannesburg, meeting rooms, relevant legal awards, and even candid shots of your team engaging with the local community. A lack of visual content makes your firm appear less established and less approachable compared to competitors.
