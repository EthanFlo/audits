The three fixes on 80% of audited roofing contractor profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Your Google Business Profile description is a prime piece of digital real estate, yet many Columbus roofing contractors leave it largely blank or under 400 characters. Google allows up to 750 characters to detail your services, service areas like Upper Arlington or Hilliard, and unique selling propositions. Missing this opportunity means you’re not communicating essential keywords or the full scope of your expertise, making it harder for potential customers to find your specific services.

  • Neglecting Secondary Categories

    While "Roofing Contractor" is your primary category, neglecting secondary categories significantly limits your discoverability. Many profiles miss out on vital specificity. Adding categories like "Roof Repair," "Emergency Roofing Service," and "Roof Inspection Service" tells Google exactly what services you offer beyond general roofing. This precise categorization ensures your business appears in searches for these high-intent, specific needs within the Columbus metro area.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A picture is worth a thousand words, especially for a roofing contractor. Many profiles have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, often lacking the visual proof customers seek. Missing are critical images such as before-and-after project photos, your professional team in action, branded service vehicles, and even satisfied customer testimonials. These visuals build trust and showcase your quality work to Columbus homeowners and businesses searching for a reliable roofer.
