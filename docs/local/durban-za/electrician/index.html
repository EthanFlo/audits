The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

  • Incomplete Business Description

    Many Durban electricians underutilise their Google Business Profile description, often leaving it under 400 characters when Google allows a generous 750. This means you're missing a crucial opportunity to detail your specialisations—like surge protection, geyser wiring, or smart home integrations—and explain why your service stands out to potential clients searching for specific expertise.

  • Missing Key Service Categories

    A frequent oversight is not adding secondary categories beyond just "Electrician." This limits your reach. For Durban-based electrical businesses, I often find crucial categories like "Emergency Electrician," "EV Charger Installation Service," and "Electrical Panel Upgrade Service" are absent. Adding these helps you appear for highly specific and lucrative searches, directly connecting you with customers needing those precise services.

  • Insufficient Visual Content

    Most electrician profiles I audit in Durban feature fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, if any. This leaves potential customers guessing about your professionalism and work quality. You're missing vital opportunities to showcase before-and-after project photos, your professional team in uniform, branded service vehicles, and even specific tools or equipment that demonstrate your capability and commitment.
