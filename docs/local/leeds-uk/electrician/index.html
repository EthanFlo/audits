The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

*

Your Business Description is Too Short

Many electricians in Leeds only use a brief snippet for their Google Business Profile description, missing out on valuable SEO space. Google allows up to 750 characters, but I frequently see profiles under 400. This is a missed opportunity to detail your specific services like PAT testing, fuse box upgrades, or emergency call-outs, crucial keywords that help Leeds residents find you for their exact needs.

*

Missing Key Secondary Categories

While "Electrician" is your primary category, neglecting secondary categories significantly limits your reach. I often find profiles missing specific service types that customers in areas like Headingley or Roundhay actively search for. Adding categories such as "Emergency Electrician," "EV Charger Installation Service," or "Electrical Panel Upgrade Service" ensures you appear for a wider, more targeted range of local searches.

*

Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

A bare profile with minimal photos looks less trustworthy and engaging to potential clients. I consistently see Leeds electrician profiles with fewer than 10 owner-uploaded images, when Google recommends 30+. You're missing chances to show high-quality photos of your team on jobs, your vans, safety procedures, completed installations, and even before-and-after shots that build confidence and highlight your expertise.
