The three fixes on 80% of audited physical therapist profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Brief

    Many physical therapist profiles in Houston underutilize the Google Business Profile description, often staying below 400 characters when 750 are allowed. This critical oversight means you’re missing prime real estate to highlight unique offerings like specialized orthopedic rehab, sports injury prevention, or post-operative recovery programs. A short description fails to convey your clinic's full scope of expertise and distinct value, leaving potential patients guessing about your specific services.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Relying solely on 'Physical Therapist' as your primary category severely limits how many relevant searches you appear for in Houston. Our audits frequently reveal profiles lacking crucial secondary categories. Adding specific options like 'Sports Medicine Clinic,' 'Orthopedic Physical Therapy,' or 'Chronic Pain Physical Therapy' significantly broadens your reach, ensuring patients seeking very specific treatments can find your specialized services more easily.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A common finding is physical therapist profiles with fewer than 30 high-quality, owner-uploaded photos. Patients crave visual cues to build trust before visiting a new clinic. We often see a lack of images showcasing your modern treatment rooms, specialized equipment, friendly staff interacting with patients, or even a welcoming reception area. These missing visuals prevent your profile from conveying professionalism, warmth, and the advanced care you offer.
