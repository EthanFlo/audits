The three fixes on 80% of audited plumber profiles

  • Shortchanged Business Description

    Many plumbers only use a generic, short description, drastically underutilizing the 750 characters Google allows. This vital space is perfect for detailing your specialized services like geyser repairs, burst pipe fixes, and comprehensive drain unblocking across Cape Town's diverse suburbs. A detailed description helps potential customers understand your full expertise and service areas, making you a far more attractive option than a competitor with a bare-bones profile.

  • Overlooked Secondary Categories

    Often, plumbers only select "Plumber" as their primary category, missing out on crucial specificity. An audit will identify and add vital secondary categories such as "Emergency Plumber," "Drain Cleaning Service," and "Water Heater Installation Service." This tells Google precisely what specific services you offer, connecting you with customers searching for those exact, high-intent services across the entire Cape Peninsula, significantly improving search relevance.

  • Insufficient Visual Portfolio

    Most plumber profiles lack a robust visual presence, often having fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. Customers want to see your work, your professional team, and your branded service vehicles. We'll identify missing photos such as compelling before-and-after shots of completed jobs (e.g., new geyser installations, neat pipe repairs), your plumbers in uniform, and your recognizable branded vans, building immense trust and showcasing professionalism to Cape Town residents.
