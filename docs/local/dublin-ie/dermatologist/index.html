The three fixes on 80% of audited dermatologist profiles

  • Your Google Business Profile Description is Too Short

    Many Dublin dermatologists miss a crucial opportunity by using under 400 of the 750 available characters in their business description. This often leads to omitting vital keywords like 'acne treatment Dublin', 'eczema specialist', or 'skin cancer screening'. Expanding this section with specific conditions, treatments, and your clinic's unique approach helps Google understand your expertise and match you with more targeted patient searches.

  • Overlooking Critical Secondary Service Categories

    Often, profiles only list 'Dermatologist' as the primary category. However, Google allows multiple secondary categories which significantly broaden your reach. You should include relevant ones like 'Cosmetic Dermatology', 'Skin Cancer Screening Service', and 'Pediatric Dermatology'. Adding these ensures your clinic appears for highly specific searches beyond just general dermatology, connecting you with patients needing particular care.

  • Lack of Diverse, High-Quality Clinic Photos

    Most profiles lack the recommended 30+ owner-uploaded photos. Patients want to see your modern waiting area, the clean and comfortable treatment rooms, headshots of your compassionate team, and specialized equipment you use. Pictures of your clinic's exterior, ensuring easy recognition, are also frequently missing. These visuals build trust and help patients in Dublin feel confident choosing your practice before they even call.
