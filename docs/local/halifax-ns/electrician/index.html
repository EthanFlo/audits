The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

  • Your Google Business Profile Description is Too Short

    Many electricians in Halifax miss out on valuable keyword opportunities by using a business description under 400 characters, despite Google allowing up to 750. This means you're likely not detailing specific services like residential wiring upgrades, commercial fit-outs, or generator installations. A concise description fails to communicate your expertise, service areas beyond Halifax, or what sets your electrical business apart from local competitors.

  • You're Not Utilizing Secondary Business Categories

    Limiting your profile to just "Electrician" means missing out on crucial niche visibility. Most Halifax electricians aren't maximizing their reach by adding highly relevant secondary categories. By including "Emergency Electrician," "EV Charging Station Supplier," or "Panel Upgrade Service," your business becomes discoverable for more specific, high-intent searches. This broadens your exposure significantly to customers seeking specialized electrical work.

  • Too Few Owner-Uploaded Photos on Your Profile

    A bare-bones photo gallery gives potential clients little confidence. Many electrician profiles in Halifax feature fewer than 30 high-quality, owner-uploaded images. You might be missing before-and-after shots of panel upgrades, photos of your team in uniform, branded service vehicles, or safety practices in action. Customers want visual proof of professionalism, the scope of your work, and trust signals before they call for a quote.
