The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

Incomplete Business Description

Many Columbus electricians limit their Google Business Profile description to just a few sentences. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles I see utilize less than half. This means you're missing crucial opportunities to include keywords for services like "panel upgrade," "residential wiring," or "commercial electrical repair," making it harder for the right customers to find your specialized offerings in their search across the city.

Overlooked Secondary Categories

Beyond the primary "Electrician" category, many profiles neglect vital secondary options. These categories tell Google exactly what services you offer, significantly boosting your visibility for niche searches. I often find profiles missing categories like "Emergency electrician," "Electric vehicle charging station installation service," and "Electrical panel upgrade service," directly impacting their reach for these high-demand services across Columbus neighborhoods.

Insufficient Visuals and Project Photos

Trust is crucial for electrical work. Profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos often fail to showcase competence. Missing are "before and after" shots of successful panel upgrades, images of professional technicians in uniform on job sites, clear pictures of your service vehicles, or even your well-maintained shop exterior. These visuals build confidence and help potential clients in Columbus choose you over competitors.
