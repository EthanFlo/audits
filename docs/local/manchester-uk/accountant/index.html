The three fixes on 80% of audited accountant profiles

  • Business Description Underutilised

    Many Manchester accountants use less than 400 of the 750 characters Google allows for their business description. This critical space is often wasted with generic statements instead of detailing specialist services like R&D tax credits, payroll management, or specific industry expertise for local businesses in Spinningfields or the Northern Quarter. A comprehensive description is vital for matching your firm with precise client needs and boosting visibility.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Often, firms only select "Accountant" as their primary category, ignoring valuable secondary options. For accountants in Manchester, critical missing categories frequently include: Tax Preparation, Bookkeeping, and Payroll Service. Adding these distinct services significantly broadens the types of searches your Google Business Profile can rank for, helping you capture a wider range of local clients seeking specific financial help.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A surprising number of accountant profiles in Manchester lack sufficient owner-uploaded photos. We often find profiles with fewer than 10, when Google recommends 30+. Clients want to see your modern office interior, professional team headshots, the exterior of your building, or even local Manchester landmarks near your firm. High-quality, diverse images build trust and significantly increase engagement on your listing.
