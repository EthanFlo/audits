The three fixes on 80% of audited hvac contractor profiles

  • 1. Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many HVAC contractors in Singapore miss a huge opportunity by only writing a short, basic business description. Google allows up to 750 characters, but most profiles use less than 400. This space is crucial to highlight your unique selling points, specialist services like eco-friendly AC installations, emergency repairs, or specific brands you service, giving potential customers a much clearer picture of what makes your Singapore-based HVAC business the right choice.

  • 2. Overlooking Vital Secondary Categories

    Beyond your primary "HVAC Contractor" category, many Singaporean businesses fail to add other critical secondary categories. Neglecting these limits your visibility for specific high-value searches. Common missing categories for HVAC professionals include "Air Conditioning Repair Service," "Furnace Repair Service," and "Duct Cleaning Service." Properly categorizing ensures your profile appears for the full range of services you offer, directly connecting you with customers searching for those exact needs.

  • 3. Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A typical HVAC contractor's Google Business Profile in Singapore often has fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, hindering customer engagement. You're missing out on showcasing before-and-after shots of AC installations, your team in branded uniforms on job sites, well-maintained service vehicles, or high-quality images of specific equipment you service. Visual content builds trust and professionalism, helping customers visualize your expertise and the quality of your work before they even pick up the phone.

In Singapore's highly urbanized and densely populated environment, competition among HVAC contractors is incredibly fierce. Singapore's tech-savvy population relies heavily on online information to make quick decisions. A robust, image-rich Google Business Profile that clearly articulates your offerings builds immediate trust and credibility, directly influencing their decision to call you over a competitor with a sparse or outdated listing.
