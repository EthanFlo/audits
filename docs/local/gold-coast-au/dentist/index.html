The three fixes on 80% of audited dentist profiles

Underutilised Business Description

Many Gold Coast dental practices I audit have business descriptions that barely scratch the surface, often under 400 characters, despite Google allowing up to 750. This missed opportunity means you're not fully communicating your unique patient experience, specialty treatments, or practice philosophy. A well-crafted description uses keywords to attract the right patients and sets you apart from the competition, clearly outlining what makes your clinic the best choice on the Gold Coast.

Missing Secondary Categories

A common oversight for dentists in Gold Coast, AU is failing to utilise secondary Google Business Profile categories. While "Dentist" is essential, many profiles stop there. I frequently find practices missing crucial descriptive categories like "Cosmetic Dentist," "Pediatric Dentist," and "Emergency Dental Service." These specific categories are vital for appearing in targeted searches, directly connecting your services with patients actively looking for them.

Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

Most Gold Coast dental profiles I review fall short of Google's recommendation of at least 30 owner-uploaded photos. Clinics often lack images showcasing their warm reception areas, modern treatment rooms, and smiling staff. Crucially, before-and-after anonymised patient smiles or educational content visuals are also frequently absent. These photos build trust and give potential patients a visual sense of your practice before they even call.
