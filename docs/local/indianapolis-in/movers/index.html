SECTION 2 — THREE FINDINGS

  • Insufficient Business Description

    Many Indianapolis moving companies use a generic or too-short business description, missing out on valuable keywords and details. Google allows up to 750 characters, but I frequently find profiles under 400. This often means neglecting to list your full service area within Indianapolis and its surrounding suburbs, highlight unique specialties like antique moving or packing services, or emphasize your dedication to customer satisfaction and experienced local crews.

  • Overlooked Secondary Categories

    Your primary category might be "Moving Company," but Google offers additional categories that greatly improve visibility for specific services. A common finding is that crucial secondary categories are entirely missing. For Indianapolis movers, adding categories like "Long Distance Movers," "Office Relocation Service," or even "Piano Moving Service" can help you appear in highly targeted searches that your basic competitors might miss completely, expanding your reach to specific client needs.

  • Scarcity of Engaging Photos

    Google Business Profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos often fail to capture customer interest and trust. I see many Indianapolis moving companies with only a logo and one or two pictures. Potential clients want to see your clean, branded trucks, your professional team in action, well-packed items, and smiling customer testimonials. A lack of diverse, high-quality images translates to missed opportunities to showcase professionalism and build instant credibility.
