The three fixes on 80% of audited moving company profiles

  • Incomplete Business Description

    Many Durban moving companies underutilise the 750-character limit for their Google Business Profile description, often sticking to less than 400 characters. This means you're missing a vital opportunity to highlight your unique selling points, the specific areas you serve across Durban, your specialisations like fragile item transport, or your commitment to customer service. A detailed description helps both Google and potential clients understand the full scope of your reliable moving operations, which is crucial in a competitive market.

  • Missing Secondary Categories

    Your primary category might be "Moving Company," but Google allows for up to nine secondary categories. I often find Durban movers are missing out on these. For instance, you could add "Long-Distance Movers" if you serve beyond KwaZulu-Natal, "Office Movers" for commercial relocations, or "Furniture Movers" to capture highly specific local searches. These additional categories broaden your reach significantly, ensuring you appear in more diverse and relevant search results across the city's various suburbs and commercial hubs.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Your Google Business Profile should showcase your Durban moving business visually, yet many profiles have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. This often means crucial images are missing. Think photos of your branded trucks navigating Durban's streets, your professional team in action (wearing uniforms), secure packing processes, or even successful unpacking at a new home. High-quality, diverse photos build trust and professionalism, allowing potential clients to visualise your service and choose you over competitors with sparse or generic imagery.
