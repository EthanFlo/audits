The three fixes on 80% of audited dentist profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Indianapolis dental practices use less than half of the 750 characters Google allows for their business description. This valuable space is often filled with generic phrases or just a few sentences, missing a huge opportunity to highlight your unique selling points. You could be showcasing specialized services like Invisalign, advanced pain management, or your commitment to patient comfort – details that genuinely resonate with prospective patients searching for a dentist in central Indiana.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Your Google Business Profile should accurately reflect all the services you offer. We frequently find dental profiles in Indianapolis only using "Dentist" as their primary category, neglecting crucial secondary categories that boost visibility. Adding specific services like "Cosmetic Dentist," "Pediatric Dentist," and "Emergency Dental Service" ensures your practice appears in more targeted local searches, connecting you with patients actively seeking those particular treatments.

  • Scarcity of Engaging Photos

    Most dental profiles in Indianapolis fall short of the recommended 30+ owner-uploaded photos. Patients want to see your office environment, your friendly staff, and even a glimpse of the technology you use before they visit. Missing photos of your modern treatment rooms, patient waiting areas, welcoming reception, and professional headshots of your team creates a less inviting and trustworthy first impression online.
