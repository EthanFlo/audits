The three fixes on 80% of audited auto repair shop profiles

  • Business Description Underutilised

    Many Auckland auto repair shops neglect to fully leverage the 750-character limit for their Google Business Profile description, often using less than 400 characters. This missed opportunity means you're not detailing your unique selling points like WOF inspections, specific European car expertise, diagnostic capabilities, or even your team's certifications. A concise but comprehensive description helps you stand out from the competition and informs potential customers exactly what services you excel at.

  • Missing Secondary Categories

    While "Auto repair shop" is a vital primary category, many profiles miss crucial secondary categories. These tell Google (and customers) more about your specialities. Common missing categories for Auckland mechanics include "Oil change service," "Brake shop," and "Tire shop." Without these, you might not appear in searches for specific services, losing out on highly targeted local inquiries from drivers needing precise work done on their vehicles.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A Google Business Profile with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos often signals an inactive or less trustworthy business. Auckland customers want to see inside your workshop: clean bays, diagnostic equipment in action, a friendly reception area, and close-ups of specific repairs being performed. Crucially, photos of your team members build trust and demonstrate the professional, local service you provide, which is often missing from under-optimised profiles.
