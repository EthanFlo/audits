The three fixes on 80% of audited dentist profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many Melbourne dental practices fail to leverage the full 750 characters Google provides for their business description, often settling for a brief 200-300 character summary. This is a missed opportunity to detail your unique selling points, advanced treatments, patient-centric approach, and the specific areas of dentistry you excel in. A comprehensive description helps Google understand your services better and potential patients connect with your practice.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Your primary Google Business Profile category is likely "Dentist," which is correct, but insufficient. Most practices overlook adding vital secondary categories that signal specific services to local patients searching for them. Common missing categories include "Cosmetic Dentist," "Pediatric Dentist," and "Emergency Dental Service." Adding these accurately broadens your reach, ensuring you appear in searches for these specialised, high-intent terms within Melbourne.

  • Scarcity of Engaging Visual Content

    An alarming number of dentist profiles in Melbourne feature fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, often including only a basic exterior shot. This neglects the power of visual storytelling. Patients want to see your modern facilities, friendly reception area, comfortable treatment rooms, and professional team members. High-quality photos of your equipment, patient smiles, and practice environment build trust and make your listing significantly more appealing.
