The three fixes on 80% of audited dentist profiles

  • 1. Underutilised Business Description

    Many Johannesburg dental practices are missing out by having a Google Business Profile description under 400 characters. Google allows 750, a valuable opportunity to elaborate on your unique services, patient-first approach, and specific treatments offered. A concise, compelling description reassures potential patients browsing local listings, communicating your expertise and specialities effectively. This overlooked detail significantly impacts your ability to convert searches into appointments.

  • 2. Incomplete Service Categories

    I frequently discover Johannesburg dental profiles only listing "Dentist" as their primary category, neglecting crucial secondary categories. This severely limits how Google understands and presents your full range of services. To truly stand out, profiles should include specific categories like "Cosmetic Dentist," "Pediatric Dentist," and "Emergency Dental Service." Adding these accurately tells Google exactly what you offer, ensuring you appear for highly specific and valuable patient searches.

  • 3. Insufficient Visual Content

    Many Johannesburg dentists undershoot the visual potential of their Google Business Profile, often having fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. Potential patients want to see your practice. Key missing images include modern treatment rooms, a welcoming reception, friendly staff, specific dental equipment, and clear exterior shots for easy navigation. A visually rich profile builds crucial trust, showcases your professional environment, and significantly aids in converting online searches into real-world appointments.
