The three fixes on 80% of audited real estate agent profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many Christchurch real estate agents leave their business description under 400 characters, losing valuable SEO potential. You're missing a chance to highlight your local market expertise, specific suburbs like Rolleston or Cashmere, and the unique property types you handle. This crucial section should be a keyword-rich narrative, telling potential buyers and sellers exactly why you’re the best choice in Canterbury, rather than a generic overview.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Most Christchurch real estate agent profiles use only the primary 'Real Estate Agent' category, missing vital secondary ones. This oversight makes you invisible to specific client searches. Frequently, profiles are missing "Buyer's Agent," "Seller's Agent," and "Property Manager." These categories are crucial for defining your full service offering and attracting clients with precise needs, ensuring your profile appears for more targeted searches across the Christchurch region.

  • Scarcity of Engaging Photos

    Often, real estate agent profiles have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, a significant missed opportunity. Beyond headshots and office exteriors, potential clients in Christchurch want to see high-quality images of your team in action, community involvement, local Christchurch landmarks you serve, successful property closings, and even engaging videos. A robust visual library builds trust, showcases your local presence, and gives a more complete, inviting picture of your professional service.
