Why cadence matters

  • Google's algorithm specifically tracks upload pattern, not just total photo count
  • Bursts (e.g. 50 photos in a day, then nothing for a year) underperform steady cadence (2-5 per week)
  • Owner-uploaded photos with geolocation stamps rank higher than customer-uploaded photos (customer uploads rarely carry geolocation data)
  • 80 photos is a common plateau above which returns diminish; aim for 60-100 in your first 90 days

What to photograph

  1. Storefront / exterior (3-5 angles, different times of day)
  2. Interior (work areas, reception, equipment)
  3. Team members (5-10 individual + group)
  4. Every service you offer (one representative photo per service)
  5. Before/after or work-in-progress (if applicable to your business)
  6. Signage, logos, awards, licenses
