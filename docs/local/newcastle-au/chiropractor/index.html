The three fixes on 80% of audited chiropractor profiles

  • Missed Opportunity: Your Business Description Is Too Short

    Many chiropractors in Newcastle only write a brief sentence or two, missing out on valuable space. Google allows up to 750 characters, and you're likely using less than half. This is prime real estate to articulate your unique approach to spinal health, explain your passion for helping Newcastle residents with their chronic pain, or highlight specialised techniques like Gonstead or Diversified. A detailed description helps patients understand your clinic's philosophy and why you're the right choice for their specific needs, boosting relevance in local searches.

  • Incomplete Categorisation: Missing Key Secondary Services

    Often, chiropractors correctly list "Chiropractor" as their primary category but stop there. This overlooks crucial secondary categories that broaden your visibility. For a Newcastle practice, adding specific services like "Sports Chiropractor," "Auto Accident Chiropractor," or "Family Chiropractor" can significantly improve how you appear for niche searches. These categories tell Google exactly what specialised care you offer beyond general adjustments, connecting you with patients actively seeking specific solutions after an injury or for their children's spinal health.

  • Scarcity of Visuals: Not Enough Engaging Clinic Photos

    A common audit finding is fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, leaving your Google Business Profile feeling sparse and uninviting. Patients want to see your modern adjustment rooms, your friendly reception area, and even photos of your team interacting professionally. Missing are images showcasing your clinic's welcoming atmosphere, equipment like traction tables, or patient education materials. Visuals build trust and help potential patients in Newcastle feel comfortable before they even step through your door, making them more likely to choose you.
