The three fixes on 80% of audited hvac contractor profiles

Short Description Limiting Your Story

Many Las Vegas HVAC contractors underutilize their Google Business Profile description, often staying below Google's 750-character limit. A brief description fails to capture the full scope of your specialized services, like emergency AC repair, complex furnace installations, or preventative maintenance plans specific to the desert climate. You're missing valuable opportunities to include high-intent keywords and differentiate your expertise from local competitors.

Overlooking Crucial Secondary Categories

Relying solely on "HVAC Contractor" as your primary category is a common pitfall. To truly dominate local search in Las Vegas, you need to leverage secondary categories. Specific, relevant examples include: "AC Repair Service," "Furnace Repair Service," and "Duct Cleaning Service." These categories signal to Google exactly what services you offer, connecting you with more precise customer searches beyond just general HVAC needs.

Scarcity of Service-Specific Photos

Profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos often miss out on crucial customer engagement. Las Vegas homeowners want to see your professionalism and quality of work. Missing photos include your team in action (installing new units, performing repairs), technicians wearing uniforms, clean service vehicles, and before-and-after shots of completed jobs. Visual proof builds trust and showcases your expertise far better than words alone.
