The three fixes on 80% of audited locksmith profiles

  • Your Google Business Profile Description is Too Short

    Many Birmingham locksmiths leave their business description barely scratching the surface, often under 400 characters, missing a golden opportunity. Google allows up to 750 characters, space you should use to detail your emergency services, auto locksmith expertise, secure safe opening, and specific service areas within Birmingham. A comprehensive description signals to Google and customers that you are a serious, full-service provider, improving relevance for diverse search queries. This isn't just text; it’s prime real estate for conveying value.

  • Missing Vital Secondary Service Categories

    Your primary category might be "Locksmith," but many profiles neglect secondary categories that are crucial for capturing specific searches. I frequently find locksmiths in Birmingham missing critical additions like "Emergency Locksmith," "Auto Locksmith," or "Safe Opening Service." These specific categories directly map to what customers are typing into Google during urgent situations. Adding them dramatically expands the range of keywords your profile can rank for, directly connecting you with high-intent local searchers.

  • Lack of High-Quality, Relevant Photos

    A surprising number of locksmith profiles in Birmingham have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, often featuring just a logo. You're missing out on showcasing your professionalism and trustworthiness. Customers want to see your team, your branded service van, various types of locks you repair or install, and even before-and-after shots of your work. High-quality images of your tools, your shop front, or even satisfied customer interactions build credibility and engage potential clients far more effectively.
