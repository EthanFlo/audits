The three fixes on 80% of audited cafe profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many Perth cafes leave their Google Business Profile description severely underutilised, often with fewer than 400 characters when Google allows up to 750. This is a missed opportunity to truly tell your cafe's story. Potential customers are looking for what makes your place unique: do you offer artisanal flat whites, vegan-friendly pastries, a dog-friendly outdoor area, or locally sourced ingredients? A comprehensive description helps Google understand your offerings better and draws in the right clientele, especially in a competitive market.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Often, cafes only select 'Cafe' as their primary Google Business Profile category, neglecting valuable secondary categories. This limits your visibility when customers search for specific experiences. You could be missing out on searches for 'Brunch Spot', 'Coffee Shop', or even 'Bakery' if you produce your own goods. By adding relevant secondary categories like these, you expand your reach, ensuring your cafe appears for a wider range of high-intent searches from Perth locals seeking their next favourite spot.

  • A Dearth of Engaging Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Many cafe Google Business Profiles suffer from a lack of high-quality, owner-uploaded images, often hovering well below the recommended 30-plus. These aren't just pretty pictures; they're vital selling tools. Are you showcasing your perfectly poured latte art, the inviting interior ambiance, your mouth-watering avocado smash, or the friendly faces of your baristas? Missing these visual cues means potential customers might scroll past your listing, unable to truly visualise the unique experience your Perth cafe offers.

Why these matter specifically for cafes in Perth, AU

Perth's cafe scene is incredibly vibrant and competitive, from the bustling CBD to charming neighbourhood hubs like Leederville, Fremantle, and Mount Lawley. With new cafes consistently opening and established favourites vying for attention, simply existing isn't enough. Your Google Business Profile needs to be perfectly optimised to stand out amongst the dozens, if not hundreds, of other options potential customers are seeing. A poorly optimised profile can easily get lost in the noise, regardless of how good your coffee is.

Perth's spread-out metropolitan area means many locals rely on their phones for quick, hyper-local searches, especially during their morning commute or lunch break. People aren't driving across the city on a whim for coffee; they're searching "cafe near me" or "best coffee {suburb name}". If your profile isn't fully detailed with compelling descriptions and categories, you miss those crucial impulse decisions from customers looking for convenience and quality, right when they need it most.

Perth customers, like most, are increasingly visual. Before committing to a visit, they're scrutinising photos for ambiance, menu items, and overall vibe. If your cafe's Google Business Profile lacks diverse, high-quality images of your space, food, and drinks, they're likely to choose a competitor who has visually showcased their offering. In a city where cafes often become a 'third place' outside home and work, establishing that connection visually is paramount to attracting regular patronage.
