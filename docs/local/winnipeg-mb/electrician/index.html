The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Winnipeg electricians miss a critical opportunity by keeping their Google Business Profile description under 400 characters, despite Google allowing up to 750. This means you're not fully detailing key services like emergency repairs, commercial wiring, panel upgrades, or EV charger installations. A short description fails to capture specific search queries from potential clients, leaving them to guess if you offer the exact electrical solution they need.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Your main category might be "Electrician," but are you leveraging secondary categories to truly dominate local searches in Winnipeg? I often find profiles missing vital specific classifications. By adding categories like "Emergency Electrician," "EV Charger Installation Service," and "Electrical Panel Upgrade Service," you signal to Google and potential customers exactly what specialized services you offer, significantly boosting your visibility for those niche, high-value searches.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A picture is worth a thousand words, especially when customers are seeking a trusted electrician. Many Winnipeg electricians have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, missing opportunities to showcase their professionalism. You're likely missing images of completed projects (before/after), your team at work, well-maintained service vehicles, safety practices, and even simple headshots. These visuals build trust and help customers visualize your quality of work.
