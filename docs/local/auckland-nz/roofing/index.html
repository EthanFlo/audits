The three fixes on 80% of audited roofing contractor profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many Auckland roofing contractors miss a key opportunity by using a business description under 400 characters, despite Google allowing 750. This means you're not fully detailing your expertise in metal roofing, tile repairs, or spouting services, nor are you mentioning specific Auckland suburbs you service. A short description fails to tell Google's algorithm what you truly offer, diminishing your chances of appearing for relevant local searches and showcasing your full capabilities to potential customers needing roof work.

  • Incomplete Category Selection

    Beyond just 'Roofing contractor,' many profiles in Auckland overlook vital secondary categories that signal their full range of services to Google. Without adding 'Roof Repair Service,' 'Emergency Roofing Service,' and 'Roof Inspection Service,' your business might not appear for customers specifically searching for those needs. This limits your visibility for critical, high-intent local searches and allows competitors who have optimised these categories to capture those urgent Auckland leads first.

  • Lack of Visual Proof (Photos)

    Most Auckland roofing contractors have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, a significant oversight when visuals build trust. Profiles often lack crucial images like high-quality 'before and after' shots of completed re-roofs, team photos showing local faces, or diverse project examples across different Auckland suburbs. Missing these detailed visuals means potential clients can't easily see the quality of your work or your team's professionalism, often leading them to choose a competitor with a more visually rich and transparent profile.

This isn't just about general visibility; it's about connecting your specific solutions to urgent local problems in a crowded market where others are already optimised.

  • For roofing contractors, commute patterns are a crucial consideration. Auckland's sprawling geography and traffic mean customers typically seek roofers within a reasonable travel distance or who clearly service their specific suburb. By not fully detailing your service areas within your description or lacking geo-tagged photos from jobs in Albany, Ponsonby, or Papakura, you're missing opportunities to appear in localised searches that respect these geographical boundaries. A homeowner isn't just looking for 'a roofer'; they're looking for 'a roofer near me in Parnell'.

  • Auckland homeowners often search for roofing services with a high degree of visual expectation and trust. Before and after photos, combined with team images, provide tangible proof of quality and professionalism. Given the significant investment in a new roof or major repair, customers want to see the quality of work. Without a robust photo gallery showcasing local Auckland projects perhaps a newly installed Colorsteel roof in Remuera or a tile repair in Henderson you're failing to build critical pre-purchase confidence, making it easier for customers to choose a competitor who visibly demonstrates their expertise.

  • The nature of roofing issues, from storm damage to aging infrastructure, often involves urgency. Auckland experiences its share of intense weather, leading to immediate repair needs. When a roof is leaking, customers aren't browsing; they're acting fast. If your profile doesn't explicitly state 'Emergency Roofing Service' or showcase quick turnaround project photos, you risk being overlooked by those desperate for immediate assistance.
