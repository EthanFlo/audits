The three fixes on 80% of audited chiropractor profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many chiropractor profiles I review in Leeds are missing out by using a brief, generic business description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most practices use less than 400. This is a missed opportunity to articulate your unique approach, specific conditions you treat (like sciatica, migraines, or posture correction), and the patient experience you offer in Leeds. A detailed description helps patients connect with your brand and understand your value.

  • Neglecting Niche Specializations

    Often, chiropractors in Leeds only list 'Chiropractor' as their primary category. However, Google allows multiple secondary categories, which are vital for attracting specific patient segments. By not adding relevant options like 'Sports Chiropractor,' 'Paediatric Chiropractor,' or 'Posture Correction Service,' you're missing out on highly targeted searches from potential patients looking for specialist care. Broadening your categories dramatically improves your discoverability for key services.

  • Lack of Visual Trust-Building

    First impressions are critical, especially in healthcare. Many Leeds chiropractic practices have very few owner-uploaded photos, often fewer than 10, when Google recommends 30+. Patients want to see your friendly team, clean treatment rooms, welcoming reception area, and even exterior shots of your clinic. High-quality visuals build trust, professionalism, and provide a vital glimpse into your practice, making potential patients more comfortable before they even make contact.
