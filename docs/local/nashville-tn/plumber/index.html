The three fixes on 80% of audited plumber profiles

*

Underutilized Business Description

Many plumbing businesses in Nashville only use a short, generic description on their Google Business Profile. Google allows up to 750 characters, but most plumbers are barely using half of that. This missed opportunity means you're not detailing your specific services like water heater repair, drain cleaning, or emergency plumbing, nor are you leveraging important keywords that local customers in Nashville are searching for. *

Missing Key Secondary Categories

A common audit finding is that plumbers are only listed under the primary "Plumber" category. To capture a wider range of localized searches, your profile needs specific secondary categories. I frequently find missing crucial ones like "Emergency Plumber," "Drain Cleaning Service," and "Water Heater Installation Service," which directly match what Nashville residents search for when they need help. *

Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

Most plumbing profiles lack a comprehensive visual representation of their business. Google Business Profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos are missing out. This includes high-quality images of your service vehicles, uniformed technicians at work (with client permission), examples of completed repairs, your tools, and your team, all of which build trust and demonstrate professionalism to potential Nashville clients.
