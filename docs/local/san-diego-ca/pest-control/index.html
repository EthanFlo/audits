The three fixes on 80% of audited pest control profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many San Diego pest control companies leave their Google Business Profile description short, often under 400 characters, missing a huge opportunity to rank for vital keywords. Google allows up to 750 characters. This prime space should detail your unique services like termite inspections, ant colony elimination, eco-friendly options, and emergency services, all tailored to San Diego homes and businesses. A well-crafted description tells both Google and potential customers exactly what you offer, setting you apart from local competitors.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Beyond the primary 'Pest Control Service' category, many San Diego businesses neglect to add crucial secondary categories. This severely limits their visibility for specific, high-intent searches. You should be using categories like 'Termite Control', 'Rodent Exterminator', and 'Bed Bug Exterminator'. Each added category expands your reach and signals to Google that you are a comprehensive solution provider, connecting you with customers searching for precise pest solutions across San Diego neighborhoods.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Most pest control profiles in San Diego fall short of Google's recommendation of 30+ owner-uploaded photos. Often, I find profiles with just a logo and a couple of generic exterior shots. You're missing critical visual opportunities: images of your team in branded uniforms, before-and-after pest treatment photos, service vehicles, your equipment in action, and even photos of your satisfied San Diego clients (with permission!). High-quality, relevant photos build trust and showcase professionalism.
