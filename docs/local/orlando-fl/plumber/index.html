The three fixes on 80% of audited plumber profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Orlando plumber profiles I review use a generic, short description, often under 400 characters, when Google allows up to 750. This means you're missing a critical opportunity to highlight your specialized services like trenchless sewer repair, commercial plumbing, or eco-friendly water heater installations. A detailed description helps both Google and potential customers understand the full scope of your expertise, making your profile more appealing and relevant for diverse search queries across Central Florida.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Your primary category might be "Plumber," but a common oversight is neglecting secondary categories that drastically improve discoverability. For Orlando plumbers, I frequently find missing vital categories like "Emergency Plumber," "Drain Cleaning Service," and "Water Heater Installation Service." Adding these tells Google exactly what specialized jobs you do, ensuring you appear for highly specific, high-intent searches from customers in urgent need.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Most plumber profiles lack enough owner-uploaded photos, often fewer than the recommended 30. Google prioritizes active, visually rich profiles. Your profile is likely missing images of your friendly crew in uniform, branded service vans, specialized equipment in action, or before-and-after photos of completed jobs in Orlando homes. These visuals build trust, showcase professionalism, and give customers a clear idea of your expertise before they even call.
