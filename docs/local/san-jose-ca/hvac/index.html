The three fixes on 80% of audited hvac contractor profiles

  • Incomplete Business Description

    Many San Jose HVAC contractors unknowingly limit their reach with short Google Business Profile descriptions. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles are under 400. This means you're missing a critical opportunity to detail your specialized services—like smart thermostat installation or commercial HVAC maintenance—and showcase what makes your San Jose business the top choice for local homeowners.

  • Missing Secondary Service Categories

    A common audit finding is the absence of crucial secondary categories beyond just "HVAC Contractor." To truly stand out in San Jose, your profile should explicitly list services like "AC Repair Service," "Furnace Installation Service," and "Duct Cleaning Service." This granular detail helps Google match your business with highly specific local searches, preventing potential customers from choosing a competitor for their exact need.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Your Google Business Profile should be a visual showcase of your professionalism and expertise, but many San Jose HVAC profiles have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. Missing are crucial images of your team in uniform, branded service vehicles, before-and-after shots of successful installations or repairs, and even your well-maintained equipment. These visuals build trust and help local customers envision choosing your service.
