The three fixes on 80% of audited auto repair shop profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many auto repair shops in Glasgow miss the opportunity to use their full 750-character Google Business Profile description. Often, I find profiles with descriptions under 400 characters. This means you're not showcasing your full range of services – from routine servicing and diagnostic checks to specialist engine work or exhaust repairs – or highlighting what makes your Glasgow shop unique, like experienced technicians or customer service philosophy.

  • Overlooking Key Service Categories

    A common audit finding is auto repair shops only using 'Auto Repair Shop' as their primary category. Google allows several secondary categories that vastly improve search visibility. Many Glasgow shops are missing vital categories like 'Oil Change Service', 'Brake Shop', or 'Tire Shop'. Without these, you're invisible to customers specifically searching for those essential services, even if you offer them daily.

  • Scant Visuals of Your Workshop

    Customers want to see where their vehicle will be serviced. Many profiles I audit for Glasgow auto repair shops have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. This often means missing shots of your clean workshop, the diagnostic equipment in use, the various services being performed (like tyre fitting or engine repairs), your friendly team, or the comfortable customer waiting area. Visuals build trust and demonstrate professionalism.
