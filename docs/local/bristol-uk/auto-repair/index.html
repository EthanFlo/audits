The three fixes on 80% of audited auto repair shop profiles

  • Insufficient Business Description

    Many auto repair shops in Bristol miss out by having a business description under 400 characters, when Google allows up to 750. This means you’re failing to communicate crucial details like your specialisations – perhaps you’re experts in electric vehicle servicing, classic car restoration, or specific diagnostics for European models. A comprehensive description allows you to stand out and directly address the needs of potential customers before they even visit your website.

  • Missed Service Categories

    Your primary Google Business Profile category might be "Auto Repair Shop," which is correct, but failing to add relevant secondary categories significantly limits your visibility. I frequently find Bristol garages are missing vital additions like "Oil Change Service," "Brake Shop," and "Tire Shop." These specific categories directly match common customer searches, ensuring your garage appears for highly targeted queries, rather than just generic auto repair needs.

  • Lacking Visuals

    Most auto repair profiles I audit have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, which is a major missed opportunity. Customers want to see inside your workshop, the professionalism of your team, and the quality of your equipment. Missing photos often include shots of your clean reception area, diagnostic tools in action, specific bays for MOTs or servicing, examples of repairs, or even exterior shots that help people find your specific location in busy Bristol neighbourhoods.
