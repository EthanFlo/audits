The three fixes on 80% of audited moving company profiles

  • 1. Incomplete Business Description

    Many Cape Town moving companies underutilize their Google Business Profile description. We frequently find listings with less than 400 characters, despite Google allowing up to 750. This means you're missing a prime opportunity to clearly detail your expertise in local furniture removals, office relocations, or specialised transport services to potential clients. A short, generic description fails to convey your unique selling points, experience, and the specific areas you serve across the Cape Peninsula, making it harder for customers to choose you.

  • 2. Missing Specific Secondary Categories

    Google Business Profile allows multiple categories beyond just "Movers." Many Cape Town moving companies only select one, severely limiting their search visibility. We commonly find profiles missing crucial secondary categories like "Long Distance Movers," "Office Relocation Service," or "Piano Movers." Without these, your business won't appear for highly targeted searches, effectively hiding your full range of services from potential customers actively seeking specialised moving solutions across Cape Town.

  • 3. Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A strong Google Business Profile features at least 30 high-quality, owner-uploaded photos, yet many Cape Town moving companies fall short. We often see profiles with minimal images or only stock photos. Crucially missing are real pictures of your friendly moving crew in uniform, your well-maintained fleet of trucks, inside a clean moving truck, and staff carefully packing items. These authentic visuals build trust, demonstrate professionalism, and show potential clients the reliable service they can expect from your moving company, essential for local bookings.
