The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

  • An Underutilized Business Description (Under 400 Characters)

    Many Salt Lake City electricians use a bare-bones description, missing out on over 350 valuable characters Google allows. This often means profiles lack crucial keywords for services like "electrical panel upgrades," "EV charger installation," "emergency repairs," or specific "smart home wiring." A detailed description tells both Google and potential customers exactly what services you offer, significantly improving your relevance for various search queries beyond just "electrician near me."

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Your primary category is "Electrician," which is a good start. However, many profiles stop there, leaving valuable secondary categories unchecked. For Salt Lake City electricians, this means missing opportunities to rank for highly specific searches. I frequently find profiles that should be using "Emergency Electrician," "EV Charging Station Installation Service," or "Electrical Panel Upgrade Service." These categories directly connect you with customers searching for specialized services, giving you a competitive edge.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Many electrician profiles in Salt Lake City fall short on visual content, often having fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. This commonly means missing critical visual proof of your work. Customers are looking for images of recent projects like finished EV charger installations, updated electrical panels, before-and-after renovation wiring, your team in action, branded service vehicles, and even your professional office space. These photos build trust and showcase your expertise.
