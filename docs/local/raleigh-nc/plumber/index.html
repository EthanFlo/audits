The three fixes on 80% of audited plumber profiles

  • Business Description is Under 400 Characters

    Many Raleigh plumbers miss a massive opportunity by using an insufficient description on their Google Business Profile. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet I frequently find profiles with less than 200, barely outlining vital services like water heater repair, drain clearing, or emergency leak detection. This underutilization prevents Google and potential customers from understanding your full expertise, significantly hindering your visibility for crucial, high-intent searches.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    It's common to see Raleigh plumber profiles that only list "Plumber" as their sole category. This oversight means you're invisible to specific, urgent searches. Google permits up to ten categories, and adding relevant secondary ones like "Emergency Plumber," "Drain Cleaning Service," and "Water Heater Repair Service" is essential. Without them, you're not showing up for customers actively searching for precise solutions, giving competitors a significant advantage in a competitive market.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Another frequently overlooked area for Raleigh plumbers is the lack of owner-uploaded, high-quality photos. Profiles with fewer than 30 images often struggle to build trust and connection. Customers want to see your team, branded vans, before-and-after shots of completed jobs like a new water heater installation or a repaired pipe, and even your professional tools. These visuals provide critical social proof and a sense of your service quality, making your profile more appealing than a bare one.
