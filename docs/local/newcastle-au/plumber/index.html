The three fixes on 80% of audited plumber profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Newcastle plumbers miss a huge opportunity by leaving their Google Business Profile description under 400 characters, despite Google allowing 750. This means you're not showcasing key services like hot water system installation, gas fitting, or leak detection. A well-optimized description, rich with local keywords, tells potential customers exactly what you offer before they even click, directly impacting who gets the call.

  • Missing Critical Secondary Categories

    A common oversight is failing to add essential secondary categories beyond "Plumber." For Newcastle, this means you might be invisible for specific, high-intent searches. I often find profiles lacking categories like "Emergency Plumber," "Drain Cleaning Service," or "Water Heater Installation Service." These categories are vital for matching your business with customers searching for precise solutions, not just general plumbing work.

  • Scarcity of High-Quality Photos

    Many plumbing profiles in Newcastle fall short of the recommended 30+ owner-uploaded photos. This leaves potential clients guessing about your professionalism and team. Missing photos include your team in uniform, before-and-after shots of completed jobs, clearly branded vehicles, your service area, or even your friendly office staff. Visual trust is key for customers choosing a local tradesperson.
