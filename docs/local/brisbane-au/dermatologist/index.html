The three fixes on 80% of audited dermatologist profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many Brisbane dermatologists only use a fraction of the 750 characters available for their Google Business Profile description. This common oversight means missing vital opportunities to highlight specialised services like advanced mole mapping, paediatric dermatology, or cosmetic skin treatments. A short, generic description fails to differentiate your practice, making it harder for potential patients searching for specific care in Brisbane to choose you over a competitor.

  • Missing Key Service Categories

    Another frequent issue is relying solely on the primary "Dermatologist" category. Google allows secondary categories that significantly expand your reach. I often find practices missing crucial additions like 'Cosmetic Dermatology', 'Skin Cancer Clinic', or 'Paediatric Dermatologist'. Without these, your practice won't appear for highly specific, high-intent searches from Brisbane residents seeking particular types of skin care.

  • Lack of Engaging Visuals

    Profiles often fall short on owner-uploaded photos, usually fewer than 30. This means potential patients don't see your welcoming reception area, modern treatment rooms, or friendly staff. High-quality images of your clinic's interior, exterior, and team are vital for building trust and professionalism with local Brisbane residents, helping them visualise their visit before booking.
