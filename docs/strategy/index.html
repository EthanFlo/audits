Porter's Five Forces

Industry is LOW-barrier, HIGH-rivalry. Winners are those with unique volume + specific service offering + automated delivery. Our automation is the moat.

Threat Of New Entrants

HIGH

Why: GBP audit services have near-zero barriers to entry. Any freelancer can start doing audits. Differentiation is service quality, speed, and pricing model (flat fee vs retainer).

Our play: Defensible moat = speed (2h audit), price transparency ($287 flat), refund guarantee, proof of volume (250+ audits). Hard to replicate in week 1.

Bargaining Power Of Buyers

HIGH

Why: Buyers have many free alternatives (YouTube tutorials, Upwork competitors, DIY). Switching cost is zero.

Our play: Lower buyer friction with free tripwire audit, then upsell implementation. Compete on specificity and speed rather than price.

Bargaining Power Of Suppliers

LOW

Why: Suppliers = Google Cloud (Gemini API), Gmail, GitHub Pages, Cloudflare. All have free tiers. Switching to Anthropic/Groq/OpenAI is a one-line env-var change.

Our play: Best-of-Breed architecture means no supplier lock-in. LLM_PROVIDER env var supports fallback chain.

Threat Of Substitutes

MODERATE

Why: Substitutes = do-it-yourself guides, agency retainers, cheap overseas freelancers. DIY is free but slow; agencies are 10× more expensive; freelancers vary in quality.

Our play: Position as 'faster than agencies, cheaper than retainers, sharper than random freelancers'. The flat-fee + refund guarantee is the anti-substitute weapon.

Competitive Rivalry

HIGH

Why: Upwork and Fiverr have hundreds of 'GBP optimization' listings. SEO is crowded. Local SEO agencies are everywhere.

Our play: Win on (1) discovery surface (1,600+ SEO pages) (2) speed (sub-2h free audit) (3) transparency (public refund policy + pricing ladder on every page).

Porter's Generic Strategies

✓ Cost Leadership

Free-tier infrastructure, zero SaaS spend beyond $20/mo Gemini Pro, Best-of-Breed with custom glue. Our cost per audit trends to $0 (Gemini tokens only).

✓ Differentiation

Unique positioning: 2-hour turnaround, flat fee, 48h refund guarantee. No agency can match this speed; no freelancer can match the volume.

✓ Focus Niche

Narrow focus: Google Business Profile audits for local service businesses. Not trying to do general SEO, websites, ads.

We're pursuing all three, which Porter warns against ('stuck in the middle'). But the hybrid works for us because the automation layer collapses the cost-differentiation tradeoff — we can be cheap AND fast AND specialized because the marginal cost per audit is near zero.

Recommendation: Current positioning works for < $100K revenue. Beyond that, pick one primary strategy for defensibility.
