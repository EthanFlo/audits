Best Google Business categories for a dermatologist

Optimizing your Google Business Profile (GBP) is paramount for local search visibility, and selecting the correct categories is the most critical step. Google uses these categories to understand what services your business offers, matching you with potential patients searching for specific treatments or conditions. Think of each category as a unique ranking lane; if you don't declare a lane, you won't appear in searches for it.

Most dermatologists typically set only "Dermatologist" as their primary category, leaving up to nine valuable secondary category "lanes" completely empty. By strategically utilizing all available category slots, you can significantly broaden your reach, capturing a wider spectrum of searches ranging from general skincare to highly specialized treatments like Mohs surgery or pediatric dermatology, thereby outperforming competitors who overlook this vital optimization.

The three fixes on 80% of audited dermatologist profiles

### Dermatologist This is the unequivocal primary category because it is the most direct, universally understood, and frequently searched term for the core services provided by such a practice. While options like "Skin care clinic" or "Medical clinic" exist, they are too broad and dilute the specific expertise of a dermatologist. Choosing "Dermatologist" ensures your practice ranks highest for the most relevant and high-intent searches from patients explicitly seeking a skin specialist, establishing your authority from the outset.

Why these matter specifically for dermatologists in Categories

  1. Skin care clinic: Unlocks searches for general skin health, preventative care, acne management, and anti-aging services.
  2. Medical spa: Captures patients looking for cosmetic procedures such as Botox, dermal fillers, advanced facials, and non-surgical aesthetic treatments.
  3. Cosmetic surgery clinic: Attracts individuals seeking surgical aesthetic procedures like eyelid lifts, liposuction, or specific excisions if offered by the practice.
  4. Laser hair removal service: Targets patients actively searching for permanent or semi-permanent hair reduction solutions.
  5. Tattoo removal service: Connects with individuals interested in professional laser-based removal of unwanted tattoos.
  6. Acne treatment service: Reaches those specifically looking for professional diagnosis, medication, and management plans for acne and related skin conditions.
  7. Skin cancer treatment center: Ensures visibility for patients needing screening, diagnosis, and various forms of treatment for skin cancers.
  8. Mohs surgery clinic: Caters to individuals specifically seeking the highly specialized and precise Mohs micrographic surgery for skin cancer removal.
  9. Pediatric dermatology clinic: Attracts parents searching for specialized care for skin conditions affecting infants, children, and adolescents.
