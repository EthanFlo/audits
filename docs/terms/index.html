Scope

Flo Marketing provides Google Business Profile (GBP) audits and implementation services. The scope of each engagement is:

  • Audit ($47 tier or free): a one-page written report identifying three specific issues on your current GBP, delivered within 2 hours of receiving your URL.
  • Implementation ($287 tier): all three audit findings implemented directly on your GBP via manager-invite access, within 24 hours of receiving that access.
  • Quarterly ($998 tier): 15-factor audit plus 60 days of rank tracking.
  • Done-for-you ($2,998 tier): full buildout plus 30 days of management.
  • Maintenance ($134/mo): ongoing monthly optimization, cancelable anytime.

Free audit: no money changes hands, so no refund is applicable. You can always decline to act on the recommendations.
