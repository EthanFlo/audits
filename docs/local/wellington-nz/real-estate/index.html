The three fixes on 80% of audited real estate agent profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many Wellington real estate agents leave their Google Business Profile description brief, often under 400 characters, missing out on valuable space. Google allows up to 750 characters, giving you ample room to detail your specialisations – perhaps high-value properties in Khandallah, first-home buyers in Johnsonville, or apartment sales in Te Aro. A comprehensive description signals expertise to both Google and potential clients actively searching for specific real estate services in the capital.

  • Key Secondary Categories Are Missing

    Google Business Profile allows you to list up to nine categories, yet many Wellington real estate agents only use 'Real Estate Agency' as their primary. This overlooks crucial secondary categories that significantly boost your local search visibility. For example, 'Buyer's Agent,' 'Seller's Agent,' and 'Property Management Company' clearly tell Google and potential clients the full scope of your services, helping you appear in more targeted searches beyond just a general agency.

  • Not Enough High-Quality Owner Photos

    A visually engaging Google Business Profile attracts more clicks, yet many Wellington real estate agents upload fewer than 30 owner photos. This means missed opportunities to showcase professionalism and local expertise. We often find profiles lacking updated professional headshots, team photos, images of their Wellington office, recently sold properties, successful open home scenes, or compelling local area shots that resonate with potential buyers and sellers in specific suburbs like Karori or Mount Victoria.
