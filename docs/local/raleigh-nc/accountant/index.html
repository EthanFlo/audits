The three fixes on 80% of audited accountant profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Raleigh accountants miss a critical opportunity by leaving their Google Business Profile description well under the 750-character limit. We often find profiles with only a brief, generic statement. This prime real estate should be used to detail your specialized services like tax planning, payroll, QuickBooks setup, and small business consulting, ensuring you capture every relevant keyword a potential client might use to find a local expert.

  • Missing Secondary Categories

    Your primary category might be "Accountant," but neglecting secondary categories severely limits your reach. We frequently see profiles that don't leverage the full scope of services they offer. For Raleigh accountants, adding crucial categories such as "Tax Preparation," "Bookkeeping," and "Small Business CPA" immediately expands your visibility for specific, high-intent searches, ensuring you appear for clients needing precise solutions.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Visuals build trust, yet many accountant profiles in Raleigh have a sparse photo gallery. A profile with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded images usually lacks key elements. We often find missing photos of your professional office interior, your dedicated team at work, client meeting spaces, and even exterior shots of your Raleigh building, all of which help prospective clients feel more comfortable and familiar with your firm before they even call.
