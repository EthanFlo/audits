The three fixes on 80% of audited pest control profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many Christchurch pest control businesses use a Google Business Profile description that's far too short, often under 400 characters when Google allows up to 750. This means you're missing a critical opportunity to include important keywords like "termite inspection," "spider control Christchurch," or "wasp nest removal." A brief description fails to explain your specialist services, service area within Canterbury, or unique selling points, leaving potential customers to guess what you offer instead of being persuaded.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Often, I find pest control profiles only use the primary "Pest Control Service" category. However, Google allows multiple secondary categories, which are vital for niche visibility. You're likely missing out by not including highly relevant categories such as "Termite Control," "Rodent Control," or "Wasp Nest Removal Service." These specific categories directly map to what customers are searching for, helping your business appear for more targeted and high-intent local queries.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Many local pest control businesses have very few, if any, owner-uploaded photos on their Google Business Profile, falling well short of the ideal 30+ mark. This means crucial visual trust signals are absent. You're missing images of your uniformed technicians in action, branded vehicles, examples of pest damage (before/after), your office exterior, and even photos of your team. Visuals build trust and demonstrate professionalism far more effectively than text alone.
