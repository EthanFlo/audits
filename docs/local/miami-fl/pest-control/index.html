The three fixes on 80% of audited pest control profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Miami pest control profiles only use a fraction of the available space for their business description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet I frequently see profiles with less than 400. This is a missed opportunity to showcase your specialties like mosquito control, rodent exclusion, or eco-friendly treatments, directly telling potential customers in Brickell or Wynwood why you're the best choice.

  • Missing Secondary Categories

    Your primary category is "Pest Control Service," but are you utilizing the vital secondary categories? I often find profiles missing crucial additions. Correctly adding categories like "Termite Control Service," "Rodent Removal Service," or "Bed Bug Exterminator" drastically improves visibility for specific, high-intent searches. These detailed categories guide Google to match you with the precise needs of Miami homeowners and businesses.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A picture is worth a thousand clicks, especially in a visual market like Miami. I frequently see pest control profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. You’re missing opportunities to display your professional technicians, clean vehicles, successful treatment results (before/after), common local pests you handle, and your team interacting with satisfied clients. Visuals build trust and engagement far more effectively than text alone.
