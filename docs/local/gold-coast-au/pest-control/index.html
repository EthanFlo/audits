The three fixes on 80% of audited pest control profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many Gold Coast pest control businesses miss a huge opportunity by keeping their Google Business Profile description brief, often under 400 characters. Google allows up to 750! This crucial space is perfect for detailing your specific services like termite inspections, spider treatments, or rodent eradication, and highlighting your local expertise in the Gold Coast climate. A detailed description helps Google and potential customers understand exactly what you offer, leading to better matching and visibility.

  • Neglecting Secondary Categories

    A common oversight for pest control profiles is only using the primary "Pest Control Service" category. Gold Coast businesses often fail to add vital secondary categories such as "Termite Control", "Rodent Exterminator", or "Bed Bug Exterminator". These specific categories tell Google and users exactly what services you specialise in, greatly improving your chances of appearing in searches for those particular, high-value pest issues. Don't leave these vital signals unaddressed!

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Profiles frequently lack a robust gallery of owner-uploaded photos, often fewer than 30. For pest control, this means missing crucial visual proof of your work. Potential clients want to see images of your team in action (safely!), common pests you treat, vehicles, and even before-and-after shots of successful eradications. These authentic photos build trust and demonstrate professionalism far more effectively than stock images or a sparse gallery, making your profile more appealing.
