The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

  • Incomplete Business Description

    Many Miami electricians underestimate their Google Business Profile description. Often, I find profiles using fewer than 400 of the available 750 characters. This means missing out on crucial keywords and details about your specialized services like generator installations, smart home wiring, or commercial build-outs. A full, keyword-rich description tells Google exactly what you do, helping you rank for more relevant local searches.

  • Missing Critical Secondary Categories

    Your primary category might be 'Electrician,' but many profiles fail to utilize vital secondary categories that capture specific client needs. I frequently see profiles missing important additions like 'Emergency Electrician,' 'EV Charging Station Installation Service,' or 'Electric Panel Upgrading Service.' These granular categories are crucial for homeowners and businesses in Miami searching for highly specific electrical solutions.

  • Lack of Owner-Uploaded Visual Content

    Google allows up to 30 owner-uploaded photos before they start displaying user photos prominently, yet most electrician profiles have far fewer. I often find missing images of your licensed technicians in uniform, completed residential panel upgrades, commercial wiring projects, company vehicles, or even your Miami office front. These photos build trust, showcase your professionalism, and help potential clients visualize your expertise.
