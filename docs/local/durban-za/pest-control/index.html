The three fixes on 80% of audited pest control profiles

  • Incomplete Business Descriptions

    Many Durban pest control profiles only use a fraction of the available 750 characters for their business description. This is a missed opportunity to highlight your specific services like termite inspections, rodent baiting, or fumigation for Durban homes and businesses. A concise, keyword-rich description explains exactly what you offer, setting you apart from competitors and reassuring potential customers you're the right choice.

  • Overlooking Specific Service Categories

    I often find profiles missing crucial secondary categories. While "Pest Control Service" is essential, Google allows more. You should include specific service categories like "Termite Control," "Rodent Control," and "Bed Bug Exterminator." Adding these helps Google understand the breadth of your expertise, making you visible for highly specific searches by Durban residents facing particular pest issues.

  • Insufficient Visual Proof of Work

    A common issue is a lack of owner-uploaded photos. Profiles often have fewer than 30, sometimes none beyond the logo. Customers want to see your team in action, branded vehicles, before-and-after shots of treated areas, and photos of your Durban service area. Visuals build trust and demonstrate professionalism, showing you are active and reliable for local pest issues.
