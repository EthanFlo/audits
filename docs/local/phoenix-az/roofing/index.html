The three fixes on 80% of audited roofing contractor profiles

  • Your Google Business Profile Description is Too Short

    Many Phoenix roofing contractors leave their Google Business Profile description bare, often falling well short of Google's 750-character limit. This crucial space is often underutilized, missing opportunities to detail your expertise in tile and flat roof repairs, storm damage restoration, or your specific service areas like Scottsdale, Mesa, or Chandler. A robust description tells both Google and potential customers exactly what you offer, setting you apart from the competition.

  • You're Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Beyond the primary 'Roofing Contractor' category, many profiles neglect vital secondary options. For Phoenix roofers, this often means missing out on targeted searches for services like "Roof Repair," "Emergency Roofing Service," and "Roof Inspection Service." Each missing category is a missed opportunity for your business to appear for highly specific, high-intent searches from customers needing immediate help with their roofs, directly impacting your lead flow.

  • Your Profile is Missing Key Project Photos

    A visual void plagues many local roofing profiles. You might have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, losing out on showcasing your work. Potential clients want to see clear before-and-after shots of tile roof replacements, shingle repairs, crew members safely on job sites, and finished commercial projects across Phoenix. These images build trust and prove your capability, helping clients choose you over the competition based on tangible evidence.
