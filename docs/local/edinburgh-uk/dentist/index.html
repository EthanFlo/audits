The three fixes on 80% of audited dentist profiles

*

Underutilised Business Description

Many Edinburgh dental practices limit their Google Business Profile description to a few sentences, often falling well short of the 750-character limit. This means missing a crucial opportunity to detail your specialisations, patient care philosophy, or unique practice environment. A concise, keyword-rich description helps potential patients understand your full range of services, from preventative care to advanced cosmetic procedures, directly influencing their decision to choose you.

*

Missing Secondary Categories

A common oversight for dentists is neglecting to add secondary categories beyond just "Dentist". Google allows multiple classifications, significantly broadening your reach. Practices often miss categories like "Cosmetic Dentist", "Pediatric Dentist", or "Emergency Dental Service". Properly categorising ensures you appear in searches for specific treatments or urgent needs, directly connecting you with patients looking for precise services you offer in Edinburgh.

*

Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

Most dental profiles in Edinburgh fall short of the optimal number of owner-uploaded photos, often featuring only a handful of generic images. Patients want to see your clean, modern reception area, comfortable treatment rooms, friendly staff, and the accessible features of your practice. High-quality visuals of your team in action, specific equipment, and patient waiting areas build trust and convey professionalism, differentiating you from competitors.
