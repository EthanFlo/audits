The three fixes on 80% of audited veterinarian profiles

  • Your Google Business Profile Description is Underselling Your Practice

    Many Las Vegas veterinarians unknowingly use a business description that’s too brief, often under 400 characters, when Google allows 750. This means missing a crucial opportunity to highlight your unique compassionate approach, specialized equipment like digital radiography, or specific services such as acupuncture and dental cleanings, which truly set your clinic apart from others in the valley.

  • You're Missing Out on Critical Secondary Categories

    Beyond "Veterinarian," many profiles fail to leverage vital secondary categories that help Google understand the full scope of your services. Failing to include specific categories like "Emergency Vet," "Animal Hospital," or "Pet Surgery" can prevent your clinic from appearing in search results for highly targeted and profitable patient needs, especially crucial in a busy city like Las Vegas.

  • Your Profile Lacks Sufficient High-Quality, Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A Google Business Profile with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos often looks less inviting and trustworthy to potential clients. We frequently find profiles missing engaging photos of your skilled team in action, the pristine exam rooms, comfortable waiting areas, state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment, or even happy pets interacting with staff, which build vital trust before a visit.
