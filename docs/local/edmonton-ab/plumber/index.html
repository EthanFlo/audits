The three fixes on 80% of audited plumber profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many Edmonton plumbers only use a few lines in their Google Business Profile description, missing out on valuable space. Google allows up to 750 characters, but I often see descriptions barely hitting 300-400 characters. This means you're not fully explaining your expertise, your service area within Edmonton, or what makes your plumbing business unique to potential customers searching for urgent repairs or new installations.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Often, plumbers only select "Plumber" as their primary category. This is a missed opportunity. To capture more specific searches, you need robust secondary categories. I frequently find profiles missing vital additions like "Emergency Plumber," "Drain Cleaning Service," and "Water Heater Installation Service." These specific categories help Google understand your diverse offerings and match you with very specific customer needs across Edmonton.

  • Not Enough Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A common oversight is having fewer than 30 high-quality, owner-uploaded photos. Customers want to see who they're hiring. Profiles often lack images of your skilled technicians in action, completed repair work (before/after), your service vehicles, or even photos of your team. Visuals build trust and demonstrate professionalism, significantly impacting a customer's decision to choose your Edmonton plumbing business over a competitor's.

Why these matter specifically for plumbers in Edmonton, AB

Edmonton's plumbing market is highly competitive, especially in established neighbourhoods like Old Strathcona or rapidly growing areas like the southwest. When a pipe bursts in Sherwood Park or a furnace stops working in St. Albert, customers are often looking for the *closest available* qualified plumber. A well-optimized profile, showing comprehensive services and specific location relevance, ensures you appear in these urgent, geo-specific searches, regardless of where they are in the metro area.

Plumbers are frequently called for emergencies. People aren't browsing casually; they're often in crisis. They need immediate trust signals. A complete, photo-rich, and accurately categorized Google Business Profile acts as a credible online storefront. It quickly answers their questions, builds confidence, and encourages them to call you instead of scrolling past to the next listing.
