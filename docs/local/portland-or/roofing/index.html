The three fixes on 80% of audited roofing contractor profiles

  • Generic Business Description Holding You Back

    Many Portland roofing contractors often settle for a short, uninformative business description, missing out on valuable SEO real estate. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles barely scratch 400. This is a missed opportunity to showcase your specific services like storm damage repair, cedar shake expertise, or gutter installation, and highlight your Portland-area focus, failing to attract the right local customers searching for detailed services.

  • Neglecting Crucial Secondary Categories

    Your primary Google Business Profile category might be 'Roofing Contractor,' but neglecting secondary categories is a common oversight. Many profiles miss adding vital service-specific options like 'Roof Repair,' 'Emergency Roofing,' or 'Roof Inspection.' In a competitive market like Portland, this omission severely limits your visibility for specific, high-intent searches, letting competitors with more comprehensive profiles steal valuable leads who are looking for those exact services.

  • Scarcity of High-Quality Owner Photos

    A robust Google Business Profile needs visual evidence of your work, yet many Portland roofing contractors have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. This often means missing before-and-after shots of roof replacements, team photos showcasing your local crew, images of completed repair jobs in specific Portland neighborhoods, or even pictures of your clean, branded work vehicles. These visuals build trust and show off your expertise, crucial for convincing local clients.
