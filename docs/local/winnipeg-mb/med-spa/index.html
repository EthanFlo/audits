The three fixes on 80% of audited med spa profiles

  • Business Description Under 400 Characters

    Many med spas in Winnipeg overlook the full potential of their Google Business Profile description. Google allows 750 characters, but I often find profiles using less than 400. This is a missed opportunity to showcase your unique approach to client care, detail specific treatments like advanced facials or body contouring, and explain what sets your Winnipeg med spa apart from others in the city, impacting your ability to capture relevant search traffic.

  • Missing Secondary Categories

    It's common to see med spas list 'Med Spa' as their primary category and stop there. But Google Business Profile allows for multiple secondary categories, which significantly boosts visibility for specific services. I consistently find Winnipeg med spa profiles missing crucial secondary categories like 'Botox clinic', 'Laser hair removal service', and 'Microneedling service', preventing them from showing up in highly targeted searches by local clients.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Visuals are vital for a med spa, yet many profiles fall short of the optimal 30+ owner-uploaded photos. I often see profiles missing high-quality images of clean treatment rooms, the welcoming reception area, professional staff in action (with client permission), or detailed close-ups of advanced equipment. Potential Winnipeg clients want to see the environment and quality they can expect before ever visiting your location.
