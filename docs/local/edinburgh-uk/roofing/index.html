The three fixes on 80% of audited roofing contractor profiles

  • Limited Business Description Hindering Visibility

    Many Edinburgh roofing contractors use a short, generic business description that barely scrapes 400 characters, leaving hundreds of valuable characters unused. Google allows up to 750, providing ample space to detail your specialisations like slate repair, flat roof installations, or guttering services. A sparse description often fails to include crucial keywords local customers are searching for, meaning your comprehensive services aren't being fully showcased to potential clients looking for skilled roofers in the area.

  • Crucial Secondary Categories Are Missing

    A common audit finding is the omission of vital secondary categories beyond just "Roofing Contractor". While "Roofing Contractor" is primary, Google allows multiple categories that significantly broaden your reach. We often see profiles missing essential choices like "Roof Repair", "Emergency Roofing Service", and "Roof Inspection Service". Without these, you're not appearing in searches from customers specifically needing urgent fixes or proactive checks, narrowing your potential client base in Edinburgh considerably.

  • Scarcity of Engaging Project Photos

    Many roofing profiles feature fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, often just a logo or a single completed job. This is a huge missed opportunity to build trust and showcase expertise. What's often missing are high-quality images of your team at work, before-and-after shots of challenging repairs, diverse completed projects (slate, tile, flat roofs), and even photos of your vans and tools. Visual proof of your craftsmanship and professionalism is critical for attracting new clients.
