The three fixes on 80% of audited pest control profiles

  • Short-changed Business Description

    Many Pittsburgh pest control profiles underutilize their Google Business Profile description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet I frequently see businesses using less than 400. This is a massive missed opportunity to highlight unique selling points like "eco-friendly solutions for Squirrel Hill homes," "certified termite specialists," or "emergency wasp nest removal in the South Hills." A robust description helps you rank for specific services and stand out from competitors.

  • Overlooking Key Service Categories

    A common oversight is neglecting secondary categories beyond the primary "Pest Control Service." For Pittsburgh pest control businesses, this means missing out on targeted searches. I often find profiles that could greatly benefit from adding categories like "Termite Exterminator," "Rodent Control Service," and "Bed Bug Exterminator." Specifying these crucial services directly tells Google and potential customers exactly what specialized problems you solve.

  • Scarcity of Trust-Building Visuals

    More often than not, pest control profiles in Pittsburgh have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. Customers want to see who they're hiring. Missing are images of your professional team in uniform, branded service vehicles, technicians safely at work (e.g., inspecting a foundation), or even subtle "before/after" shots. These visuals build trust and convey professionalism, crucial for a local service business homeowners invite into their property.
