The three fixes on 80% of audited physical therapist profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many physical therapy profiles in Vancouver, BC, underutilize the Google Business Profile description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most clinics use less than 400. This is a missed opportunity to showcase your clinic's unique specializations like specific treatment modalities, patient-focused approach, or advanced equipment. A detailed description helps potential patients understand your value beyond a simple service listing, differentiating you from other Vancouver clinics and improving keyword relevance.

  • Missing Key Secondary Service Categories

    Simply listing "Physical Therapist" as your primary category isn't enough in a market like Vancouver. Many profiles fail to add crucial secondary categories that capture specific patient searches. Examples include "Sports Therapy Clinic," "Post-Surgery Rehabilitation," or "Pediatric Physical Therapy." Without these, you miss out on patients actively seeking specialized care, making it harder for Google to connect your targeted services with local search queries.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Google Business Profiles with extensive, high-quality photos signal an active, trustworthy business. Many Vancouver physical therapists neglect to upload a comprehensive suite of images. I often find profiles with fewer than 30 photos. Patients want to see your modern treatment rooms, a welcoming reception area, photos of your team, and even therapists demonstrating exercises or using specialized equipment. This visual content builds trust and shows off your clinic's professional environment.
