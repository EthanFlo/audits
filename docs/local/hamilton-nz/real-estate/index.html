The three fixes on 80% of audited real estate agent profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many real estate agents in Hamilton only use a few sentences in their Google Business Profile description. Google allows up to 750 characters, but most use under 400. This is a missed opportunity to showcase your specialisations—whether it’s residential sales, rural properties, or specific Hamilton suburbs like Rototuna or Melville. A detailed description helps potential clients understand your unique value proposition before they even click.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Your primary category is likely "Real Estate Agent," which is good, but many profiles neglect secondary categories. This significantly limits your visibility for specific searches. For Hamilton real estate agents, essential secondary categories to consider adding include "Buyer's Agent," "Seller's Agent," and "Property Manager." Adding these tells Google exactly what services you offer, matching you with more precise client searches.

  • Too Few Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A surprising number of Hamilton real estate agents have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. Your profile should be a visual representation of your business and expertise. Missing photos often include agent headshots, team photos, your office interior, local landmarks relevant to listings, and engaging images of different Hamilton property types. Rich visuals build trust and help prospects connect with your brand before they meet you.
