The three fixes on 80% of audited med spa profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description (Under 400 Characters)

    Many Ottawa med spas are leaving valuable space blank in their Google Business Profile description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles I audit barely use half. This means missing critical opportunities to highlight signature treatments like advanced facials, laser hair removal expertise, or specialized anti-aging solutions that set your med spa apart in a competitive market, reducing your visibility for key local searches.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Your med spa likely offers a diverse range of services, but often profiles only list "Medical Spa" as the sole category. I frequently find vital secondary categories like "Skin Care Clinic," "Laser Hair Removal Service," or "Electrolysis Service" are completely omitted. This oversight prevents your business from appearing in highly specific searches where local clients are actively looking for the exact treatments you provide, directing them to competitors instead.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    In a visual industry like med spas, high-quality, abundant photos are essential. I consistently find profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded images. This means prospective Ottawa clients aren't seeing your inviting reception area, spotless treatment rooms, the friendly faces of your aestheticians, or compelling before-and-after results for popular treatments. A lack of visual trust signals often causes potential clients to scroll past to a more visually engaging competitor.
