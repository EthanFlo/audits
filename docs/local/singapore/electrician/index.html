The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many electrician profiles in Singapore only scratch the surface with their business description, leaving it well under Google's 750-character limit. This is a crucial missed opportunity to highlight your BCA license, specific expertise in HDB or condo electrical systems, 24/7 emergency services, or your service areas like Jurong East or Tampines. A short description means Google doesn't fully grasp the breadth of your offerings, leading to lower relevance in detailed local searches.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Most electricians correctly list "Electrician" as their primary category, but then fail to add critical secondary categories. Google allows up to nine. Common omissions include "Emergency Electrician," "EV Charging Station Installation Service," or "Electrical Panel Upgrade Service." Without these specific tags, your business won't appear for high-intent searches from customers actively looking for these specialized services, handing those leads directly to more thoroughly categorized competitors.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Your Google Business Profile likely suffers from a severe lack of owner-uploaded, high-quality images. Customers want to see your professional team in uniform, branded service vehicles, and clear before-and-after photos of completed projects—think new light installations, faulty switch replacements, or upgraded distribution boards. Without visual evidence of your work quality, local expertise, and the faces behind your business, potential clients often overlook your listing for those with a more vibrant and trustworthy photo gallery.
