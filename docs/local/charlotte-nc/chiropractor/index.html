The three fixes on 80% of audited chiropractor profiles

  • Your Google Business Description is Too Short

    Many Charlotte chiropractors use a business description that barely scratches the surface, often falling well short of Google's 750-character limit. This crucial space is often underutilized, missing vital keywords and specific service mentions like prenatal care, scoliosis treatment, or posture correction. A detailed description tells potential patients exactly what makes your Charlotte practice unique and helps Google understand your specializations.

  • Neglecting Secondary Categories

    Optimizing your Google Business Profile goes beyond the primary "Chiropractor" category. I frequently find Charlotte practices missing key secondary categories that would help them rank for more specific patient searches. Adding categories like "Sports Chiropractor," "Auto Accident Chiropractor," and "Family Chiropractor" significantly expands your visibility, ensuring you appear for patients seeking specialized care beyond general adjustments.

  • Lack of High-Quality Photos

    First impressions matter, and a profile with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos tells an incomplete story. Many Charlotte chiropractor profiles lack images showcasing their inviting waiting areas, modern treatment rooms, friendly staff, or specialized equipment like spinal decompression tables. High-quality visuals build trust and help potential patients visualize their experience at your clinic, making them more likely to choose you.
