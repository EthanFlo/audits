The three fixes on 80% of audited moving company profiles

  • Your Google Business Description is Too Short

    Many San Jose moving companies underutilize the crucial 750-character business description field, often settling for less than 400 characters. This missed opportunity means you're not showcasing key services like residential moves, commercial relocation, packing services, or specialty item transport. A robust description helps Google understand your full service range and connects you with potential clients searching for specific moving needs in the San Jose area.

  • You're Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Beyond the primary "Moving Company" category, many San Jose movers overlook vital secondary categories. For instance, adding "Long Distance Movers," "Office Relocation Service," or "Piano Mover" significantly broadens your reach. These specific categories tell Google exactly what specialized services you offer, ensuring your profile appears for highly targeted searches from customers needing those precise moving solutions across Silicon Valley and beyond.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Your Google Business Profile should be a visual showcase, yet many San Jose moving companies have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. You're likely missing images of your uniformed team in action, branded trucks on local San Jose streets, safe packing techniques, successful deliveries, or your storage facilities. High-quality photos build trust, demonstrate professionalism, and help potential clients visualize your service, significantly impacting their decision-making process.
