The three fixes on 80% of audited dermatologist profiles

Finding 1: Your Business Description is Too Short

Many Edmonton dermatologists underutilize the Google Business Profile description, often keeping it under 400 characters when 750 are allowed. This critical space is where you can detail your unique specialties, advanced technologies, and patient-first philosophy. A brief description leaves potential patients guessing about your expertise in areas like pediatric dermatology or advanced cosmetic procedures, making it harder for them to choose your clinic over a competitor who clearly outlines their services.

Finding 2: Crucial Secondary Categories Are Missing

Your Google Business Profile likely only lists "Dermatologist" as your primary category, neglecting vital secondary categories. For dermatologists in Edmonton, this means missing out on patients searching for specific services like "Cosmetic Dermatology," "Skin Cancer Screening," or "Pediatric Dermatology." Google uses these categories to match your clinic with highly specific search queries, and without them, you're invisible to a significant portion of your target audience who needs your specialized care.

Finding 3: Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

Many dermatology clinics have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, missing a huge opportunity to build trust and showcase their environment. Patients in Edmonton want to see your modern waiting area, state-of-the-art treatment rooms, and the friendly faces of your staff. High-quality photos of specialized equipment, accessible parking, and even educational posters about common skin conditions can significantly improve engagement and help potential patients feel more comfortable choosing your clinic before they even step through the door.
