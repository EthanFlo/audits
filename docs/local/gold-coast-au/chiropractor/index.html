The three fixes on 80% of audited chiropractor profiles

  • Description Too Short & Generic

    Many Gold Coast chiropractors only use a few sentences in their Google Business Profile description, missing out on valuable space. Google allows up to 750 characters, but I frequently find profiles with less than 400. This is a missed opportunity to showcase your unique approach, conditions you treat (like sciatica or headaches), or your commitment to patient wellness, making your profile less informative for potential patients searching in your area.

  • Crucial Secondary Categories Are Missing

    Your primary category is "Chiropractor," but neglecting secondary categories significantly limits your reach. I often see Gold Coast clinics missing vital classifications like "Sports Chiropractor" for athletes, "Auto Accident Chiropractor" for injury claims, or "Family Chiropractor" for paediatric and prenatal care. Adding these helps you appear in more niche, high-intent searches from specific patient groups looking for your specialized services.

  • Scarcity of Engaging Photos

    Google profiles with ample, high-quality images attract more clicks. I consistently find Gold Coast chiropractor profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, often missing key visuals. Patients want to see your modern clinic interior, welcoming reception area, the faces of your friendly team, and even educational shots of treatment rooms or equipment. This visual void makes your practice less inviting and trustworthy compared to competitors.
