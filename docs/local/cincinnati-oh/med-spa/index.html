The three fixes on 80% of audited med spa profiles

  • Your Med Spa's Description is Too Short

    Many Cincinnati med spas overlook the full potential of the Google Business Profile description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet I frequently find profiles utilizing less than half. This space is crucial for showcasing your unique selling points – perhaps your advanced laser technology, specialized aesthetic team, or unique client experience – making your med spa stand out from the competition in a crowded market.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Beyond "Medical Spa," Google Business Profile offers specific secondary categories that dramatically improve local search visibility for your services. Many Cincinnati med spas miss out by not including relevant options like "Skin care clinic," "Laser hair removal service," or "Botox clinic." These categories are vital because they directly align your profile with what potential clients are specifically searching for, boosting your chances of appearing in targeted local results.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A picture is worth a thousand words, especially for aesthetic services. I often find Cincinnati med spa profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, a significant missed opportunity. Clients want to see your inviting reception area, clean treatment rooms, advanced equipment, professional staff, and even before-and-after results (where permitted). High-quality, diverse imagery builds trust and helps differentiate your practice from others online.
