The three fixes on 80% of audited physical therapist profiles

  • Your Business Description is Selling You Short

    Many Brisbane physical therapy profiles use only a fraction of the 750 characters Google allows for the business description. This often results in a generic overview that fails to highlight your unique specialities, patient-centric approach, or the specific conditions you excel at treating. An underwritten description means you're missing a crucial opportunity to connect with potential patients looking for tailored care like sports injury recovery, chronic pain management, or post-surgical rehabilitation right here in Brisbane.

  • Missing Out on Key Service Categories

    A common audit finding is the absence of crucial secondary categories that precisely define your services beyond just "Physical Therapist." Your Google Business Profile should accurately reflect your diverse expertise. We frequently see profiles overlooking important options such as "Sports Physical Therapy," "Geriatric Physical Therapy," and "Orthopedic Physical Therapy," all of which can significantly broaden your reach to patients searching for those specific types of care in their local Brisbane area.

  • Your Visual Story is Incomplete

    Most physical therapy profiles in Brisbane have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, falling short of Google's recommendations for a comprehensive visual representation. This often means missing critical images of your clinic's inviting reception area, modern treatment rooms, specialized equipment, and even your friendly team in action (with consent). A lack of diverse, high-quality photos means potential patients can't visualise your practice, understand your environment, or build trust before their first visit.

Why these matter specifically for physical therapists in Brisbane, AU

In Brisbane's dense urban and suburban landscape, patients rarely commute across multiple suburbs for physical therapy unless absolutely necessary. Proximity is paramount. When a potential patient searches for "physical therapy near me," Google prioritises local, relevant businesses. If your profile is incomplete or inaccurate, it signals to Google that you might not be the most relevant option, pushing you down the local pack results, even if you're only a few blocks away.

The competition among physical therapists in Brisbane is robust, with clinics in every major hub from the CBD to Chermside, and out to Springfield. Each of these practices is vying for attention in the same geo-specific searches. Fully optimising your Google Business Profile isn't just about being visible; it’s about standing out in a crowded market. Every detail, from your service categories to your visual content, contributes to Google's understanding of your unique offerings and helps you rise above the competition.

Brisbane patients, particularly those seeking physical therapy, often have immediate needs, whether recovering from a sporting injury or managing chronic pain. They are looking for expertise and trustworthiness, and a well-optimised Google Business Profile acts as your digital shopfront and credibility badge. Detailed descriptions, specific service categories, and compelling photos directly address these patient concerns, building trust and encouraging direct engagement – whether that's a call, a website visit, or directions to your clinic.
