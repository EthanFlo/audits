The three fixes on 80% of audited law firm profiles

  • Your Business Description Is Too Brief

    Many Liverpool law firms only use a few sentences to describe their services, missing out on valuable space. Google allows up to 750 characters, a prime opportunity to detail your specialisms like property law, commercial litigation, or family law, and highlight what makes your firm unique. A longer, keyword-rich description helps potential clients understand your expertise before they even click.

  • Overlooking Key Secondary Categories

    Often, law firms only select "Law Firm" as their primary category, neglecting crucial secondary options. For a Liverpool-based practice, adding specific categories like "Personal Injury Lawyer," "Family Law Attorney," or "Estate Planning Lawyer" tells Google and clients exactly what services you offer, significantly improving visibility for niche searches and connecting you with the right local clients.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Most law firm profiles in Liverpool feature very few, if any, owner-uploaded photos. You are missing chances to showcase your team, office exterior and interior, client meeting spaces, and even local landmarks near your office. These images build trust and professionalism, helping clients visualize your firm and connect on a personal level before their initial consultation.
