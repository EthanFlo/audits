The three fixes on 80% of audited locksmith profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short and Generic

    Many Cork locksmiths miss a vital opportunity here. Google allows 750 characters, yet I frequently see profiles with descriptions barely scratching 200. This isn't just about character count; it's about context. A short description often fails to mention key services like emergency lockout assistance, uPVC door lock repairs, or even specific areas of Cork you serve, leaving potential customers guessing and often clicking away.

  • Neglecting Crucial Secondary Categories

    While 'Locksmith' is your primary category, many profiles stop there. Google allows several secondary categories, and neglecting these means missing out on targeted searches. I often find Cork locksmiths are missing 'Emergency Locksmith,' 'Auto Locksmith,' and 'Safe Opening Service' – all critical terms potential clients are searching for, directly impacting who appears in specific, urgent search results.

  • Lack of Engaging, Professional Photos

    Google Business Profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos often underperform. For Cork locksmiths, this means failing to showcase your expertise. I commonly see profiles missing images of specific lock types you service, your van clearly branded, staff in uniform, or even before/after shots of repairs. These visuals build trust and help customers immediately understand your professional capabilities.

In a city like Cork, where numerous skilled locksmiths operate, simply having a Google Business Profile isn't enough. The competition for top spots in Google Maps and local search results is intense. When your profile lacks crucial optimisation elements like a detailed description or specific categories, you're effectively handing business to competitors who have taken the time to fully complete their profiles. Being visible on page one isn't a luxury here; it's a necessity to capture attention amongst the crowd.

Cork customers needing a locksmith often have an urgent problem – a lockout, a broken key, or a security issue. Their search behavior is typically immediate and hyper-local. They're looking for the nearest, most trustworthy locksmith right now, often using terms like 'locksmith near me Cork' or 'emergency locksmith Cork city.' A poorly optimised profile won't show up when it matters most, leading to instant lost opportunities that won't come back.

Cork's unique geography, with its winding streets, specific suburbs, and surrounding county areas, means customers rely on accurate service area information. If your profile doesn't clearly articulate the specific areas you cover – whether it's Douglas, Ballincollig, Blackrock, or across the wider county – you risk appearing irrelevant to potential clients just outside a presumed radius. Maximising your categories and description helps Google understand your full reach and match you with customers throughout your operational zones.
