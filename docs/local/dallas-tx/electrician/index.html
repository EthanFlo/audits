The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short and Generic

    Many electricians in Dallas settle for a few sentences when Google allows up to 750 characters. A short description misses vital opportunities to highlight your Dallas service areas, specific expertise like commercial or residential, emergency services, and licensed qualifications. This lack of detail fails to inform potential customers quickly and concisely why they should choose your electrical company over the competition, hindering your visibility for nuanced local searches.

  • You’re Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    If "Electrician" is your only category, you're severely limiting your reach. Most Dallas customers search for specific needs. During our audits, we often find profiles lacking important secondary categories like "Emergency Electrician," "EV Charging Station Installation Service," or "Electrical Panel Upgrade Service." These specific categories are vital for matching your business to the precise services Dallas residents and businesses are actively searching for, directly impacting your local rankings.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Many electrician profiles are starved for visual content. Beyond a logo and a single storefront photo, we frequently see profiles with less than 10 owner-uploaded images. This means potential clients in Dallas aren't seeing your team in action, completed projects like a new wiring installation or a renovated electrical panel, your service vehicles, or even client testimonials. High-quality, diverse photos build trust and showcase your professionalism effectively.
