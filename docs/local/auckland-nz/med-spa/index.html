The three fixes on 80% of audited med spa profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many Auckland med spas leave their Google Business Profile description almost empty, or a short paragraph under 400 characters. Google allows up to 750 characters, which is a huge missed opportunity to showcase your unique treatments, your clinic's atmosphere, and what sets you apart. This prime real estate should be used to detail your signature anti-aging treatments, advanced skin rejuvenation options, and the expert care clients can expect, drawing in those specific searches.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Your primary category might be "Medical Spa," but neglecting secondary categories severely limits your reach. I often find profiles missing vital specific categories that potential clients are searching for directly. For Auckland med spas, I frequently see "Botox Clinic," "Laser Hair Removal Service," and "Microneedling Service" omitted. Adding these ensures you appear in more targeted local searches, connecting you with clients actively seeking those precise treatments.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A picture truly is worth a thousand words, especially for visual businesses like med spas. Most profiles I audit have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. This means missing crucial visual elements like high-quality interior shots of serene treatment rooms, inviting reception areas, 'before & after' treatment examples (where appropriate), detailed photos of advanced equipment, and professional staff headshots. These visuals build essential trust and help clients envision their luxurious experience before they even visit.
