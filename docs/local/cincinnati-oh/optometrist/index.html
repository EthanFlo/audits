The three fixes on 80% of audited optometrist profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Cincinnati optometrists stop at a short, basic description, missing out on over 350 characters Google allows. This often means vital keywords and unique selling points for your practice, like advanced diagnostic equipment or specific specialty lenses, aren't being highlighted. A detailed description helps potential patients understand your full scope of services before they even click your website.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Your Google Business Profile likely has "Optometrist" as its primary category, which is correct. However, we frequently find practices in Cincinnati are missing crucial secondary categories that help Google understand the breadth of your services. Key categories like "Eye Exam," "Contact Lens Fitting," and "Pediatric Eye Care" can significantly improve visibility for specific patient searches.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    We often discover optometrist profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. Patients want to see your modern waiting room, state-of-the-art exam equipment, friendly team members, and the actual frames you offer. Without these visual cues, your practice appears less inviting and trustworthy, missing a critical opportunity to build connection before a visit.
