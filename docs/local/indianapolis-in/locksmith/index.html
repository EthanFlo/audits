The three fixes on 80% of audited locksmith profiles

  • Your Google Business Profile Description is Too Short

    Many locksmiths in Indianapolis miss a crucial opportunity by having a business description under 400 characters, when Google allows up to 750. This isn't just about word count; it's about missing valuable keywords and service details that tell potential customers and Google exactly what you offer. A limited description means you're not fully showcasing your expertise in residential, commercial, or automotive locksmithing, hindering your visibility in critical local searches.

  • Neglecting Secondary Service Categories

    While 'Locksmith' is essential, relying solely on it severely limits your reach. Our audits frequently reveal that Indianapolis locksmiths are missing vital secondary categories that expand their audience. Optimizing your profile with specific categories like 'Emergency Locksmith,' 'Auto Locksmith,' and 'Safe Opening Service' can significantly improve your chances of appearing for a wider range of high-intent searches, capturing more specialized local needs.

  • Insufficient Visuals: Too Few Photos

    Google Business Profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos often struggle to engage potential customers and convey trustworthiness. For an Indianapolis locksmith, this means missing out on showcasing your professional vans, identifiable team members in uniform, examples of residential door lock installations, commercial access control systems, or even the tools of your trade. High-quality, relevant images build instant credibility and provide visual proof of your reliability and service range.
