The three fixes on 80% of audited dentist profiles

### Your Business Description is Too Short Many Charlotte dentists inadvertently limit their online reach with a brief, unoptimized Google Business Profile description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet I frequently find descriptions under 400 characters. This missed opportunity means you're not showcasing your unique selling points like advanced technology, patient comfort initiatives, or specific specialties such as Invisalign or sleep apnea treatment, which are crucial details local patients seek when choosing a new dental home. ### Missing Critical Secondary Categories I often see dentist profiles in Charlotte only using the primary "Dentist" category. However, to capture niche searches, you need robust secondary categories. Practices commonly miss critical ones like "Cosmetic Dentist," "Pediatric Dentist," and "Emergency Dental Service." Without these specific designations, you're invisible to patients explicitly searching for veneers, children's dentistry, or urgent care, letting competitors with more comprehensive categorizations grab that vital local traffic. ### Not Enough Owner-Uploaded Photos A common oversight for Charlotte dental practices is a sparse photo gallery. Google Business Profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos leave much to the imagination. Patients want to see your welcoming reception area, the friendly faces of your team, and modern treatment rooms. High-quality photos showcasing your equipment, patient testimonials (with consent), and even before-and-after smile transformations are crucial. These visuals build trust, helping prospective patients connect with your practice and visualize their experience before they even call.
