The three fixes on 80% of audited chiropractor profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many Minneapolis chiropractors use only a fraction of the 750 characters Google allows for their business description. This critical space is often wasted with generic phrases instead of a compelling narrative. We'll craft a rich, keyword-optimized description that highlights your unique approach to spinal health, pain relief, and wellness, ensuring potential patients understand exactly what sets your practice apart in Minneapolis.

  • Neglecting Secondary Categories

    Google Business Profile lets you specify multiple service categories, yet many chiropractor profiles only list "Chiropractor." This is a missed opportunity in a competitive market like Minneapolis. Properly utilizing secondary categories like "Sports Chiropractor," "Auto Accident Chiropractor," or "Family Chiropractor" helps you appear in more targeted searches, connecting you with patients actively seeking specialized care.

  • Lack of Engaging Owner-Uploaded Photos

    While patients might upload a few photos, many chiropractor profiles have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded images. This leaves out crucial visual elements that build trust and showcase your practice. We often find missing photos of your welcoming reception area, treatment rooms, team members, spinal health education materials, and even "before and after" examples (where appropriate and ethical) that could attract Minneapolis residents.
