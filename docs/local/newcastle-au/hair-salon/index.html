The three fixes on 80% of audited hair salon profiles

  • 1. Incomplete Business Description

    Many Newcastle hair salons leave their Google Business Profile description brief, often under 400 characters. Google allows up to 750 characters. This missed opportunity means you're not fully communicating your salon's unique style, services offered, your team's expertise, or what makes your salon the perfect choice for a new haircut, colour, or style. A detailed description helps clients truly connect with your brand before they even visit.

  • 2. Overlooking Secondary Categories

    Beyond the primary 'Hair Salon' category, many profiles miss crucial secondary categories. These tell Google exactly what specialised services you offer. I often find salons missing key options like 'Colorist', 'Men's Barber Shop', or 'Bridal Shop' which directly relate to high-value searches. Properly categorising helps you appear for highly specific client needs across Newcastle.

  • 3. Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A visually rich Google Business Profile makes a huge difference. I frequently see profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. This often means missing crucial images of your beautiful salon interior, your stylists in action, various 'before and after' client transformations, your product displays, and even high-quality team headshots. Fresh, diverse photos show off your expertise and environment, attracting more clients.
