The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

  • Your Business Description Sells You Short (Under 400 Characters)

    Many Hamilton electricians use a brief, generic sentence or two for their Google Business Profile description. Google allows up to 750 characters, a crucial space to detail your specializations like heat pump wiring, solar installations, or commercial fit-outs. A short description misses the chance to tell potential customers exactly what you excel at, leaving them to guess if you're the right fit for their specific electrical needs.

  • Missing Key Secondary Service Categories

    Your primary category might be 'Electrician', but without specific secondary categories, you're invisible for niche searches. We often find profiles missing vital classifications like 'Emergency Electrician', 'EV Charger Installation Service', or 'Electrical Panel Upgrade Service'. Adding these not only broadens your reach but also signals to Google precisely which specific, high-value services you offer to the Hamilton community.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A Google Business Profile with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos often signals a missed opportunity to build trust and showcase expertise. We typically see a lack of team photos in uniform, images of completed residential wiring projects, commercial fit-outs, or vans proudly displaying branding. Potential customers want to see your professionalism and the quality of your work before they pick up the phone.
