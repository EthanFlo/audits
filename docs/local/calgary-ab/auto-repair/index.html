The three fixes on 80% of audited auto repair shop profiles

  • Business Description Underutilized

    Many auto repair profiles in Calgary fall short by using a business description well under Google’s 750-character limit. This is a huge missed opportunity. Most shops only include a sentence or two, failing to detail their specific services, the types of vehicles they specialize in, or what makes them unique. A comprehensive description allows you to integrate important keywords and clearly convey your expertise, helping you stand out to customers searching for precise repair needs.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Your Google Business Profile should list more than just "Auto Repair Shop" as a category. Many profiles are missing crucial secondary categories that help Google understand the full scope of your services. For Calgary auto repair shops, it’s vital to include categories like 'Oil Change Service,' 'Brake Repair Shop,' and 'Tire Shop.' Adding these ensures you appear in more specific searches, directly connecting you with customers looking for those exact services.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A surprising number of auto repair shops have very few, or no, owner-uploaded photos on their Google Business Profile. Often, you'll see just a single exterior shot or a handful of customer-contributed images. Missing are vital photos of your clean service bays, skilled technicians at work, specific tools and diagnostic equipment, comfortable waiting areas, the front desk team, and examples of completed repairs. More photos build trust and showcase your professional environment.
