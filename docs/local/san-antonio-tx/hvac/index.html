SECTION 2 — THREE FINDINGS

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many HVAC contractor profiles in San Antonio miss a critical opportunity by having a business description under 400 characters, despite Google allowing 750. This means you are likely omitting key services like emergency AC repair, furnace installation, preventative maintenance plans, and even specific San Antonio service areas. A concise but comprehensive description packed with relevant keywords is essential for capturing varied customer searches.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    A common finding is that San Antonio HVAC contractors often only use "HVAC contractor" as their primary category, neglecting vital secondary categories. This severely limits visibility for specific customer needs. Adding categories like "AC Repair," "Furnace Installation Service," and "Duct Cleaning Service" helps Google understand your full range of expertise, matching your business with highly specific and intent-driven local searches.

  • Scarcity of Engaging Photos

    Many profiles feature fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, leaving a significant visual gap. Customers want to see who they’re hiring. Missing photos often include uniformed technicians on job sites, your fleet vehicles, clear before-and-after repair examples, specific HVAC equipment you service, or even team photos that build trust. These visuals boost engagement and credibility with potential clients in San Antonio.
