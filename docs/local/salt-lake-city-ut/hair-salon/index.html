The three fixes on 80% of audited hair salon profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many Salt Lake City hair salons miss out by having a bare-bones business description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles are under 400. This is prime real estate to highlight your unique selling points – perhaps your master colorists, expertise in extensions, or the relaxing, upscale atmosphere you offer. An incomplete description means potential clients don't get the full picture of your salon's specialty before moving on to a competitor.

  • You're Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Beyond the primary 'Hair Salon' category, many profiles overlook critical secondary categories that capture niche searches. Are you a renowned 'Hair Colorist' specializing in balayage or vivids? Do you have an expert 'Hair Extension Technician' on staff? Perhaps you also cater to men, qualifying as a 'Men's Hair Salon'. Omitting these tells Google you only do general hair services, preventing you from appearing for highly specific and profitable client searches in Salt Lake City.

  • Not Enough High-Quality Owner Photos

    Hair is a visual business, yet many Salt Lake City salons have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, if any at all. Clients want to see your work, your space, and your team. We often find profiles missing stunning before-and-after transformations, photos of happy clients, your talented stylists in action, and inviting interior shots of your salon. These visuals build trust and showcase your artistry far more effectively than text alone.
