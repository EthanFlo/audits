The three fixes on 80% of audited chiropractor profiles

  • Your Business Description Is Too Short

    I often find Sacramento chiropractic profiles only use a fraction of the 750 characters Google allows for the business description. This is a missed opportunity to showcase your unique approach, list specialized services like pediatric care or scoliosis treatment, and connect with potential patients on a deeper level. A detailed description helps searchers understand your practice beyond just your name and service list.

  • Overlooking Crucial Secondary Categories

    Many profiles only list "Chiropractor" as their primary category. However, Google lets you add several secondary ones that significantly boost visibility for specific searches. I frequently see practices missing key categories like "Sports Chiropractor," "Auto Accident Chiropractor," or "Family Chiropractor," which are vital for attracting specific patient demographics in Sacramento looking for specialized care.

  • Not Enough Engaging, Practice-Specific Photos

    A common audit discovery is profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. Generic stock images or just a few exterior shots don't cut it. Sacramento patients want to see photos of your welcoming waiting room, treatment rooms, friendly staff, smiling patients (with consent!), and even your adjusting tables. These visuals build trust and help people envision their visit to your practice.
