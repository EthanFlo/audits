The three fixes on 80% of audited cafe profiles

  • An Undersized Business Description

    Many Edmonton cafes underutilize their Google Business Profile description, often coming in under 400 characters when Google allows a generous 750. This means missing crucial opportunities to highlight your unique blend of coffee, signature brunch items, cozy ambiance, or special events. A short description fails to capture what makes your cafe a must-visit destination, leaving potential customers to guess and often move on to a competitor with a more compelling profile.

  • Neglecting Secondary Business Categories

    A common oversight is failing to add relevant secondary categories beyond just "Cafe." Google allows multiple categories, and neglecting these limits your reach significantly. For an Edmonton cafe, this often means missing out on being discovered by users searching for "Coffee Shop," "Brunch Restaurant," or "Bakery." Optimizing these categories ensures your business appears in a wider range of specific searches, directly connecting you with more interested customers looking for your exact offerings.

  • Too Few Owner-Uploaded Photos

    An engaging Google Business Profile should showcase your cafe's best assets, yet many Edmonton cafes have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. This often means missing vibrant shots of your latte art, freshly baked croissants, inviting indoor seating areas, outdoor patios, or even your friendly baristas in action. High-quality, diverse images are critical for attracting customers who browse visually, helping them envision themselves enjoying your space and compelling them to visit.

Why these matter specifically for cafes in Edmonton, AB

Because Edmonton's cafe scene is incredibly vibrant and competitive, standing out isn't just about great coffee; it's about being found first. With a high density of unique cafes, coffee shops, and bakeries clustered in areas like Old Strathcona, Whyte Avenue, and downtown, customers have ample choice. A perfectly optimized Google Business Profile is your digital storefront, ensuring you capture attention amidst the noise when locals are actively searching for their next coffee fix or brunch spot.

Commute patterns across Edmonton also play a significant role. Whether someone is driving from the west end to downtown for work, or walking through a neighbourhood looking for a quick break, they often pull out their phone to find "cafes near me." An incomplete or poorly managed profile means your cafe won't appear prominently in those crucial proximity searches, effectively making you invisible to people passing by who are ready to make a purchase.

Furthermore, Edmonton cafe customers are increasingly sophisticated in their search behaviour. They aren't just looking for "coffee"; they might be searching for "gluten-free pastries Edmonton," "dog-friendly patio cafe," or "best brunch southside." When your GBP accurately reflects your full offerings and ambiance through detailed descriptions, secondary categories, and compelling photos, you directly answer these specific queries, drawing in a highly qualified customer ready to visit your unique establishment.
