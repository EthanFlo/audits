The three fixes on 80% of audited roofing contractor profiles

  • Your Google Business Description is Too Short

    Many Manchester roofing contractors leave hundreds of valuable characters unused in their Google Business Profile description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet I often find profiles using less than 400. This is a missed opportunity to naturally weave in crucial keywords like "flat roof repair Manchester," "emergency roofing Altrincham," or "new roof installation Stockport," detailing your services and specific areas you cover, which helps Google understand and rank your business for diverse local searches.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Your primary category might be "Roofing Contractor," but without relevant secondary categories, you're invisible for more specific searches. I frequently uncover profiles missing vital additions like "Roof Repair Service," "Emergency Roofing Service," or "Roof Inspection Service." Adding these helps Google accurately classify your business for a wider range of services, ensuring you appear when a Manchester resident searches for precisely what you offer, not just general roofing.

  • A Scarcity of High-Quality Photos

    Many roofing contractor profiles feature fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, if any at all. This leaves customers guessing about your quality and expertise. I often see missing images of completed projects (before & afters are gold!), your team in branded uniforms, company vehicles, and different types of roofs you work on (slate, tile, flat). High-quality visuals build trust and showcase your work across Manchester, influencing potential clients to choose you.
