The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many electricians in Toronto only use a few sentences in their Google Business Profile description, missing a huge opportunity. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles barely hit 200. This section is crucial for detailing your specialized services like knob and tube remediation, commercial fit-outs, or smart home wiring. Maximize this space to tell potential Toronto clients exactly why you're their best choice and improve your keyword relevancy.

  • Missing Critical Secondary Categories

    Listing only "Electrician" as your primary category is leaving money on the table. Google allows you to add multiple secondary categories, which significantly impacts who sees your profile. For Toronto electricians, I often find missing crucial service categories like "Emergency Electrician," "EV Charging Station Installation Service," or "Electric Panel Upgrade Service." Adding these helps you rank for specific, high-intent searches.

  • Too Few Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Believe it or not, many Google Business Profiles for Toronto electricians have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. High-quality images build trust and show professionalism. I often see profiles missing essential visuals like uniformed team photos, vans with branding, before-and-after shots of panel upgrades, safety gear in use, or clear examples of their commercial and residential work. This visual content is vital for attracting local customers.
