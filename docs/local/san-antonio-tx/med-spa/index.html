The three fixes on 80% of audited med spa profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description (Under 400 Characters)

    Many San Antonio med spas leave significant marketing potential on the table by not fully using their Google Business Profile description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles we audit feature descriptions under 400. This often results in a generic overview that fails to highlight unique services like advanced skin rejuvenation, IV therapy, or body contouring that set your practice apart, missing a key opportunity to inform and attract local clients searching for specific treatments.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    A common oversight for med spas in San Antonio is neglecting to add specific secondary categories to their Google Business Profile. While "Medical Spa" is a good start, it is not enough. We frequently find profiles that miss vital service-specific categories like "Botox clinic," "Laser hair removal service," or "Microneedling service." Without these, your med spa won't appear in searches for these highly specific and profitable treatments, losing visibility to competitors who have correctly categorized their offerings.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Visual appeal is critical for San Antonio med spas, yet many profiles lack a comprehensive photo gallery. Our audits reveal a consistent pattern of fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. This means missing images of inviting waiting areas, modern treatment rooms, smiling staff, or even discreet before-and-after examples (with client permission). Without a rich visual presence, potential clients cannot fully envision their experience, making your med spa less appealing than visually richer local competitors.
