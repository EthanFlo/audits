GBP Business Hours

GBP Business Hours refers to the operating times a business publicly displays on its Google Business Profile.

This critical component of your online presence includes your standard weekly operating schedule, such as "Monday: 9 AM - 5 PM," but also encompasses special hours for holidays, temporary closures, or unique events. Maintaining accurate and up-to-date business hours on your Google Business Profile (GBP) is fundamental for effective local SEO and ensuring customers have reliable information.

The accuracy of your GBP Business Hours significantly impacts your visibility in local search results and directly affects customer trust. Google prioritizes businesses that provide comprehensive, verified information, rewarding them with better placement in Google Maps and local pack listings, especially for "open now" searches. When your hours are incorrect, potential customers might visit your location when you are closed, leading to frustration, negative reviews, and ultimately, lost business opportunities. Conversely, accurate hours ensure that when a customer searches for a service or product near them, and you are open, your business is prominently displayed and trusted.

Consider a busy cafe in Portland, Oregon. If a customer searches "coffee near me open now" at 6:30 AM, and the cafe's GBP accurately lists its opening time as 6:00 AM, that cafe will likely appear high in the search results, drawing in morning commuters. However, if the cafe closes early on a holiday, but forgets to update its "Special Hours" on GBP, customers who arrive expecting a warm drink will find the doors locked. This not only disappoints the customer but also damages the cafe's online reputation, as they might leave a one-star review citing the inaccurate information.

Common mistakes businesses make regarding GBP Business Hours include failing to update for holidays or special events, which can be particularly damaging. Some businesses also mistakenly set their hours too broadly, indicating they are open when staff may not be available for certain services, or too narrowly, missing out on potential customers who assume they are closed. Another frequent oversight is forgetting to revert "Special Hours" back to "Regular Hours" after a holiday, leading to prolonged misinformation. It's crucial to understand that GBP Business Hours are not a "set it and forget it" setting; they require ongoing management.

To maximize the effectiveness of your GBP Business Hours, follow these actionable steps:

  • Regularly Review: Make it a habit to check your listed hours at least monthly, especially before holiday seasons or local events.
  • Utilize Special Hours: Always update your "Special Hours" for holidays, temporary closures, or modified schedules well in advance.
  • Revert Special Hours: After a special period, ensure you revert your hours to your standard operating schedule.
  • Be Specific: If different departments or services have varied hours (e.g., delivery vs. in-store), use the "More hours" section to provide that detail.
  • Ensure Consistency: Verify that the hours listed on your GBP match those on your website, social media, and other online directories.
