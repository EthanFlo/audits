The three fixes on 80% of audited pest control profiles

Your Google Business Profile Description is Too Short

Many Johannesburg pest control businesses only use a fraction of the available 750 characters for their GBP description. This means you're missing a crucial opportunity to detail your specific services like termite fumigation, rodent exclusion, or eco-friendly bed bug treatments. A comprehensive description allows Google to better understand what you offer, matching your business with more relevant searches from potential clients across areas like Sandton, Roodepoort, or Soweto.

Neglecting Vital Secondary Service Categories

Often, pest control profiles are only listed under "Pest Control Service." However, Google allows multiple secondary categories, which are vital for niche visibility. Without adding specific categories like "Termite Control," "Rodent Exterminator," or "Bed Bug Exterminator," your business won't appear for these high-intent, specific searches. Failing to leverage these categories means your competitors who do will consistently outrank you for highly profitable local service queries in Johannesburg.

Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos and Visuals

A surprising number of pest control profiles in Johannesburg lack sufficient owner-uploaded photos, often fewer than 30. Google prioritizes businesses with rich visual content. You're likely missing crucial images like your uniformed technicians in action, clearly branded service vehicles, "before and after" shots of pest eradication, or even photos of your team. These visuals build trust and show professionalism, directly impacting customer engagement and click-through rates on your Google Business Profile.
