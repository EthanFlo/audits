The three fixes on 80% of audited chiropractor profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many Manchester chiropractors settle for a brief, generic business description on their Google Business Profile, often falling well short of the 750-character limit. This misses a crucial opportunity to showcase your specialisations, such as pediatric care, sports injury recovery, or advanced spinal adjustments. A detailed description, filled with relevant keywords, helps Google understand exactly who you are and what unique services you offer, connecting you with patients searching for those specific treatments.

  • Overlooking Crucial Secondary Categories

    A frequent oversight for Manchester chiropractic clinics is failing to utilise secondary categories beyond just "Chiropractor." Google allows multiple categories, and neglecting this means you're not showing up for specific patient searches. Key secondary categories like "Sports Chiropractor," "Auto Accident Chiropractor," or "Family Chiropractor" are essential. Adding these accurately broadens your reach, ensuring you connect with specific patient demographics looking for specialised care you provide.

  • Lack of Engaging Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Many chiropractic profiles in Manchester fall short of optimising their visual appeal, often having fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. This means missing out on chances to visually communicate your practice's professional, welcoming atmosphere. Patients want to see your modern treatment rooms, your friendly team, state-of-the-art equipment, and the comforting reception area. High-quality images build trust and help potential patients envision their visit, making your profile far more engaging than text alone.
