The three fixes on 80% of audited moving company profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many Perth moving companies use less than 400 characters for their Google Business Profile description, despite Google allowing up to 750. This is a missed opportunity to showcase your unique selling points, specific services like packing or storage, and the Perth suburbs you frequently serve. A short description often means vital keywords and compelling details that could attract local customers are simply not present, making your profile less appealing and discoverable.

  • Missing Secondary Categories

    Your primary category might be "Moving Company," but without relevant secondary categories, you're invisible for niche searches. I often find profiles lacking crucial additions like "Long Distance Movers," "Office Relocation Service," or "Piano Mover." Adding these specific categories significantly broadens your reach, allowing customers searching for specialised services within Perth to find your business, rather than defaulting to a competitor.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A typical moving company profile in Perth often has fewer than 10 owner-uploaded photos, falling short of the 30+ needed to truly stand out. What’s missing are images of your professional team in uniform, branded trucks on Perth streets, different types of moves (residential, office), safe packing techniques, and specialised equipment. High-quality, diverse photos build trust and give potential clients a visual understanding of your service excellence.
