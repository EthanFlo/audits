The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

Business Description Under 400 Characters

Many electrician profiles in Minneapolis often feature sparse business descriptions, failing to utilize the full 750 characters Google allows. This crucial space should detail your specialized services like commercial build-outs, residential panel upgrades, or smart home integrations. A short description misses the chance to include vital keywords and showcase your unique selling points, limiting your profile's ability to rank for specific, high-intent searches.

Missing Secondary Categories

Often, electricians only select "Electrician" as their primary Google Business Profile category. However, omitting relevant secondary categories significantly narrows your reach. Minneapolis customers search for specific needs. You should include categories like "Emergency Electrician," "EV Charging Station Installation Service," and "Lighting Fixture Store" if you offer these services, ensuring you appear for a wider array of customer queries beyond general electrical work.

Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

A Google Business Profile with minimal photos feels incomplete and untrustworthy. Electricians frequently miss opportunities to showcase their work, team, and professional setup. Missing photos typically include "before and after" shots of panel upgrades, team photos in uniform at Minneapolis job sites, pictures of your service vehicles, and high-quality images of specific installations like new wiring, outdoor lighting, or smart home device setups.
