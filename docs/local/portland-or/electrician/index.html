The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many electricians in Portland miss a crucial opportunity by using a bare-bones Google Business Profile description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles I audit are well under 400. This space is vital for incorporating local keywords like "Portland panel upgrades" or "emergency electrician Beaverton," describing your unique services, and building trust. A robust description signals relevance to Google and instills confidence in potential clients looking for comprehensive electrical solutions.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Your primary category is "Electrician," which is a great start. However, a significant number of Portland electrician profiles overlook adding crucial secondary categories. These specialized tags, such as "Emergency Electrician," "EV Charger Installation Service," or "Electrical Panel Store," are what help you appear for highly specific, high-intent searches. Neglecting them means you're invisible to customers actively seeking those precise services, handing those leads directly to competitors.

  • Not Enough High-Quality Owner Photos

    While customer-uploaded photos are helpful, your Google Business Profile likely has too few owner-uploaded, high-quality images. Many profiles I see have under 10 photos, far short of the recommended 30+. You're missing crucial visuals like before-and-after shots of panel upgrades, professional photos of your team on job sites, your branded service vans, or detailed close-ups of EV charger installations. These impactful images build trust and visually demonstrate your expertise to potential clients.
