The three fixes on 80% of audited hair salon profiles

  • 1. Underutilised Business Description

    Many Birmingham hair salons only use a fraction of the 750 characters available for their Google Business Profile description. This often leaves out crucial details like unique services, specialities (e.g., sustainable colour), the salon's welcoming atmosphere, or its precise location advantages. A full, keyword-rich description helps potential clients understand exactly what you offer and why they should choose you over competitors.

  • 2. Missing Specific Secondary Categories

    Beyond "Hair Salon," Google allows multiple secondary categories, yet many Birmingham businesses miss this opportunity to capture niche searches. I often find profiles lacking important descriptors like "Color Specialist," "Men's Barber," or "Bridal Hair Salon." Adding these ensures you appear in searches for specific services, broadening your reach beyond generic terms and connecting you with highly targeted clients.

  • 3. Too Few Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A Google Business Profile with fewer than 30 high-quality, owner-uploaded photos often underperforms. Clients want to see your work! This usually means missing vibrant before-and-after transformations, detailed shots of your salon's inviting interior, photos of your friendly team in action, or creative examples of different hairstyles and colours. Visuals are critical for attracting new clients to your Birmingham hair salon.
