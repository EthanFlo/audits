The three fixes on 80% of audited veterinarian profiles

Your Business Description is Too Short

Many Birmingham vets unintentionally limit their visibility by underutilising the Google Business Profile description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles I audit are well under 400. This is a crucial missed opportunity to highlight your unique services, such as emergency care, specific surgical expertise, or your commitment to compassionate patient communication, all while strategically weaving in local keywords that pet owners in Selly Oak or Harborne might use.

You're Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

Google Business Profile lets you add multiple categories beyond just "Veterinarian." Many practices in Birmingham overlook this, missing out on vital search traffic. Expanding your categories is key for pet owners searching for specific services. I often find practices haven't added 'Animal Hospital,' 'Emergency Veterinarian,' or 'Pet Surgery' which directly impacts who sees your profile when searching for urgent or specialised care in areas like Solihull or Erdington.

Too Few, Or The Wrong Kind, Of Photos

A Google Business Profile with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded, high-quality photos significantly underperforms. Pet owners searching for a vet in Birmingham want to see more than just your logo. They're looking for comforting images of your reception area, your friendly vet nurses interacting with animals, state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment, or even behind-the-scenes glimpses of a clean operating theatre. These visuals build trust and show prospective clients the true welcoming nature of your practice.
