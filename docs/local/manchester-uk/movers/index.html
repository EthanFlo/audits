The three fixes on 80% of audited moving company profiles

  • Your Google Business Description is Too Short

    Many moving companies in Manchester only use a fraction of the 750 characters Google allows for their business description. This is a missed opportunity to detail your full range of services, highlight your Manchester-specific expertise, and include keywords vital for local searches. An under-optimised description leaves potential customers in Bury or Oldham guessing about your capabilities instead of confidently choosing your services.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Often, moving company profiles default to just "Moving Company." However, Google offers many relevant secondary categories that can dramatically improve your visibility for specific services. Neglecting categories like "Long Distance Movers," "Office Relocation Service," or "Piano Movers" means you're missing out on customers actively searching for these precise needs, especially critical in a diverse city like Manchester.

  • Not Enough Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Profiles frequently have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, relying too heavily on customer submissions. Missing photos of your team in uniform, well-maintained removal vans with your branding, your professional packing process, or successful moves in key Manchester neighbourhoods like Didsbury or Chorlton, fails to build trust and visually showcase your operational quality to potential clients.

Why these matter specifically for moving companys in Manchester, UK

These fixes matter more for moving companies in Manchester, UK, due to several unique local factors. Firstly, the city's dense competition means every single search advantage counts. Manchester is a hub, and customers have many choices; a perfectly optimised Google Business Profile pushes you ahead of rivals who haven't paid attention to these details, securing those crucial first-page positions.

Secondly, Manchester's diverse commute patterns and geographical spread across its boroughs impact search behaviour. People moving across the M60 or from the city centre to a quieter suburb like Sale or Bolton are specifically searching for local, reliable services. A profile that accurately lists all services and is rich with relevant keywords and categories directly answers these location-specific queries, bypassing competitors stuck in generic categories.

Finally, customer search behaviour for moving companies is often urgent and highly localized. When someone in Stockport or Salford needs a mover, they're typically looking for immediate, trustworthy, and proximate options. A complete business description, robust secondary categories, and compelling photos not only improve your ranking but also build instant credibility, leading directly to higher click-through rates and, ultimately, more bookings from across Greater Manchester.
