The three fixes on 80% of audited locksmith profiles

  • Your Google Business Profile Description is Too Short

    Many locksmiths don't realize Google Business Profile allows up to 750 characters for their business description. I often find Calgary locksmiths using just a few short sentences, missing a huge opportunity. This limited text fails to showcase your full range of services, like automotive key cutting, residential rekeying, or commercial access control, and doesn't mention crucial details such as 24/7 emergency service, which Google uses to understand your business better.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Google Business Profile allows you to specify multiple categories beyond just 'Locksmith.' I consistently see Calgary locksmiths missing out on vital secondary categories that help them appear for highly specific searches. Adding categories like 'Emergency Locksmith,' 'Auto Locksmith,' and 'Safe Opening Service' can significantly improve your visibility. This ensures your profile matches exactly what potential customers are searching for, rather than just a broad 'locksmith' term.

  • Not Enough Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Most locksmith profiles I audit in Calgary have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, if any at all. This is a missed opportunity to build trust and show professionalism. You should be showcasing high-quality images of your service vehicles, technicians in uniform, your retail storefront (if applicable), specific tools being used, and examples of completed work like new lock installations or safe repairs. Visual content dramatically boosts engagement and informs potential customers.
