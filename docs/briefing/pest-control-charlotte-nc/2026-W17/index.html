Question we kept hearing

SECTION 2 — ONE COMMON QUESTION ANSWERED: Q: I see some competitors adding "Charlotte" or "Pest Control" to their official GBP business name. Does this help with rankings?

A: Be careful here. While it *can* sometimes temporarily boost visibility, Google explicitly states your GBP name should exactly match your legal business name. Adding keywords violates their guidelines and can lead to your listing being suspended or losing features. Focus on legitimate SEO tactics like gathering reviews, accurate service descriptions, and regular posts instead. It's safer and more sustainable for long-term visibility.
