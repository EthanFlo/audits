30-point Google Business Profile checklist for med spas

This 30-point Google Business Profile checklist for med spas offers a precise, actionable roadmap to enhance your online visibility and attract more clients. Unlike general advice that can feel overwhelming, a checklist provides specific steps, ensuring no critical element is overlooked. Think of this as your med spa's essential afternoon project – a dedicated hour or two to systematically optimize your profile, ensuring every facet accurately reflects your services and appeals directly to prospective clients searching for aesthetic treatments.

Working through this checklist will transform your Google Business Profile from a passive listing into an active client acquisition tool. By meticulously addressing each point, you'll improve your local search ranking, build trust with potential clients through detailed information and compelling visuals, and directly showcase your expertise in areas like Botox, laser hair removal, and advanced skincare. This isn't just about presence; it's about strategic optimization designed to convert searches into booked appointments for your med spa.

The three fixes on 80% of audited med spa profiles

Business Info

  • ☐ Verify your med spa's exact business name matches official registration documents.
  • ☐ Update precise street address and specify your exact service area if applicable.
  • ☐ Confirm the accurate, direct phone number for client inquiries and bookings.
  • ☐ Set consistent operating hours, including any special holiday schedules or extended evening availability.
  • ☐ Add your primary website link, ideally pointing to a booking page or service menu.

Categories

  • ☐ Select "Medical Spa" as your primary and most relevant business category.
  • ☐ Add "Laser Hair Removal Service" to attract clients specifically seeking this treatment.
  • ☐ Include "Skin Care Clinic" or "Dermatologist" if relevant to your service offerings.
  • ☐ Specify "Botox Clinic" or "Cosmetic Surgeon" if these are core specialized services.
  • ☐ Review all chosen categories to ensure maximum relevance and comprehensive search visibility.

Photos

  • ☐ Upload a high-resolution exterior photo showcasing your med spa's inviting entrance.
  • ☐ Feature professional interior photos of your reception area and clean, modern treatment rooms.
  • ☐ Add compelling before-and-after client photos for services like microneedling (with consent).
  • ☐ Include professional headshots of your friendly staff, building trust and personal connection.
  • ☐ Showcase photos of specialized equipment used for treatments such as laser hair removal.

Services

  • ☐ List "Botox & Dermal Fillers" with clear descriptions, benefits, and competitive pricing.
  • ☐ Detail "Laser Hair Removal" by body area, mentioning technology used and package options.
  • ☐ Add "Microneedling" and "Chemical Peels," explaining their benefits for skin rejuvenation.
  • ☐ Include specialized facials, body contouring, and other unique aesthetic treatments offered.
  • ☐ Ensure all service descriptions are keyword-rich, benefit-oriented, and client-focused.

Reviews

  • ☐ Actively encourage satisfied med spa clients to leave authentic Google reviews after their visit.
  • ☐ Respond promptly, professionally, and courteously to all new client reviews received.
  • ☐ Address any negative reviews constructively, offering solutions or apologies where appropriate.
  • ☐ Create and share a direct Google review link for easy client access in follow-up communications.
  • ☐ Monitor review patterns to identify service strengths and areas for potential improvement.

Posts

  • ☐ Publish weekly "Offer" posts highlighting special discounts on popular med spa treatments.
  • ☐ Create "What's New" posts announcing new services, product lines, or staff additions.
  • ☐ Share "Event" posts for open houses, educational seminars, or VIP client evenings.
  • ☐ Use "Product" posts to showcase popular skincare products or post-treatment care items.
  • ☐ Post FAQs or educational content about Botox safety, laser hair removal prep, or microneedling benefits.

Why these matter specifically for med spas in Checklist

Once all 30 points on this Google Business Profile checklist are meticulously checked off, your work isn't entirely done; it's time for evaluation and iteration. Begin by monitoring your performance through Google Business Profile Insights over the next 14 days, looking for improvements in views, calls, website clicks, and direction requests. Give the algorithm time to process your updates and reflect them in search results. After this initial two-week period, revisit your profile, review your insights, and prepare to repeat this optimization process, perhaps focusing on areas that show less improvement or introducing new content based on trending client interests.
