The three fixes on 80% of audited chiropractor profiles

  • Your Google Business Profile Description is Too Short

    Many Victoria chiropractors leave valuable space on the table, often writing a description under 400 characters when Google allows 750. This means you're missing opportunities to highlight your unique approach to patient care, mention specialized techniques like spinal decompression or Active Release Technique, and clearly communicate your clinic's philosophy, leaving potential patients with an incomplete picture of your expertise and offerings.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Are you only listed as a "Chiropractor"? Google Business Profile allows for multiple categories. Many profiles I review in Victoria are missing crucial secondary categories like "Sports Chiropractor," "Auto Accident Chiropractor," or "Family Chiropractor." Without these specific tags, you're invisible to local patients searching for specialized care, funneling those highly motivated searches straight to your competitors who have optimized their listings.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    First impressions are visual, and many chiropractor profiles have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. Patients want to see your modern waiting area, the welcoming faces of your team, specific treatment rooms, and even before-and-after diagrams for common conditions. A lack of diverse, high-quality images makes your clinic feel less inviting and less professional compared to competitors who showcase their practice visually.
