Privacy Policy

What we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Last updated: 2026-05-02 · Plain-English summary first; full policy second.

Plain English

Flo Marketing is a one-person operation run by Ethan Florendo. We sell digital products. We collect the minimum data needed to deliver products and run the business. We don't sell your data. Ever.

  • What we collect: your email when you buy or contact support. Gumroad or the checkout platform handles your payment information directly — we never see your card.
  • What we don't collect: we don't track you across the web. No third-party trackers. No remarketing pixels.
  • What we share: nothing, except with our processors (Gumroad or marketplace checkout for payment) — and only what's needed to deliver service.
  • Your rights: request a copy of your data, request deletion, opt out of email at any time. Email florendo.ethan+flo@gmail.com.

Full policy

1. Who we are

Flo Marketing ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Ethan Florendo as a sole proprietor. Contact: florendo.ethan+flo@gmail.com.

2. What information we collect

  • Email address — when you purchase a product or email support, so we can deliver files and answer support requests.
  • Purchase records — product purchased, date, amount. Stored by Gumroad or the payment processor and our records.
  • Support correspondence — emails you send to our support address are retained for support purposes.
  • Anonymous usage data — basic page-view counts via privacy-respecting analytics (no cookies, no IP tracking, no cross-site tracking). If we add a different analytics provider in the future, this policy will be updated and you'll be notified.

3. What we don't collect

  • Credit card or bank information — handled directly by Gumroad or the checkout platform.
  • Cross-site browsing behavior — no remarketing pixels, no Facebook tracking, no Google Ads tracking.
  • Personal information beyond email and purchase record.

4. How we use your information

  • To deliver the product you purchased.
  • To send post-purchase support emails, receipts, access help, refund confirmations, or product updates.
  • To respond to your support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax records).

5. Who we share with

  • Gumroad — current product checkout and delivery. Gumroad Privacy Policy.
  • Whop / Etsy / Gumroad — if you buy through one of these marketplaces, that platform's privacy policy applies to the purchase data they hold.

We don't sell, rent, or share your data with anyone else.

6. Your rights

You can:

  • Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of non-essential product-update emails.
  • Request that we stop processing your data (subject to legal/tax retention requirements).

To exercise any of these rights, email florendo.ethan+flo@gmail.com. We respond within 5 business days.

7. Data retention

We keep purchase records for 7 years for tax purposes (US standard). Support correspondence is kept for 2 years.

8. International users

Flo Marketing is operated from the United States. By using our products, you consent to data being processed in the US. If you're in the EEA or UK, you have additional rights under GDPR — email us to exercise them.

9. Children's privacy

Our products are not designed for users under 16. We don't knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy materially, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top and notify customers when the change affects their product access or privacy rights.

11. Contact

Privacy questions, data requests, or concerns: florendo.ethan+flo@gmail.com.
