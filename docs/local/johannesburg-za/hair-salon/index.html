The three fixes on 80% of audited hair salon profiles

  • 1. Underutilised Business Description

    Many Johannesburg hair salons only use a fraction of the 750 characters available for their Google Business Profile description. This often results in a generic overview that fails to highlight your unique salon atmosphere, specialised services like balayage or extension work, or the distinct expertise of your stylists. You're missing a prime opportunity to connect with clients searching for specific styles or an exclusive salon experience in your area.

  • 2. Incomplete Service Categorisation

    A common oversight is failing to add relevant secondary categories beyond just "Hair Salon." Google allows multiple categories, yet many profiles miss out. For Johannesburg salons, this means you might not appear for highly specific searches. We frequently find profiles missing crucial categories such as "Color Specialist," "Men's Barber," and "Bridal Hair Stylist," limiting your visibility to potential clients looking for these exact services.

  • 3. Insufficient Photo Gallery

    A visually appealing Google Business Profile is critical for hair salons, but many have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. We often see profiles lacking compelling interior shots, exterior signage, behind-the-scenes team photos, or comprehensive "before and after" galleries showcasing specific styles like intricate braids, weaves, or vibrant colour transformations. Without these, potential clients can't visualise your work or salon's ambiance.
