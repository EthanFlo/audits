The three fixes on 80% of audited physical therapist profiles

  • Business Description Underutilised

    Many physical therapy profiles in Manchester only use a fraction of the 750 characters Google allows for the business description. This often leaves crucial details like specialist treatments (e.g., manual therapy, dry needling, sports injury rehab) or unique selling points unmentioned. A concise, keyword-rich description helps patients understand your full offering and connects with their specific needs, enhancing your visibility for relevant searches beyond just "physiotherapist Manchester".

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Too many Manchester physical therapists only list "Physical Therapist" as their primary category. This overlooks the opportunity to rank for highly specific services. Google allows up to nine secondary categories. I often find profiles missing vital options like "Sports Therapy," "Post-Surgery Rehabilitation Service," or "Pediatric Physical Therapy Clinic." Adding these ensures your clinic appears in searches for specialized care, attracting patients looking for very specific help.

  • Scarcity of Engaging Photos

    Google Business Profiles with under 30 owner-uploaded photos often fail to capture patient interest effectively. For a physical therapy clinic, this means missing images of your modern treatment rooms, specialist equipment like shockwave therapy machines, or your friendly team interacting with patients (with consent, of course). High-quality visuals build trust and give prospective Manchester clients a clear impression of your professional environment and the high standard of care they can expect.
