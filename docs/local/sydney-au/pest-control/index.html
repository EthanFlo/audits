The three fixes on 80% of audited pest control profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Google allows 750 characters for your business description, yet many pest control profiles barely use 200. This is a missed opportunity to showcase your unique expertise, whether it's eco-friendly treatments, specialisation in termites or rodents, or your specific service areas across Sydney. A longer, keyword-rich description helps Google understand and rank your business for more relevant searches, giving potential customers a clear picture of what you offer.

  • Missing Vital Secondary Categories

    While "Pest Control Service" is essential, relying solely on it severely limits your visibility. Your profile needs secondary categories to capture specific searches. During an audit, I often find businesses missing crucial additions like "Termite Control", "Rodent Exterminator", or "Bed Bug Control Service". Adding these ensures your business appears when Sydney residents search for very specific pest solutions, significantly broadening your reach and relevance.

  • Your Profile Lacks Sufficient Photos

    Many pest control businesses have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, hindering trust and engagement. Sydney customers want to see who they’re hiring. Critical photos often missing include before-and-after shots of pest treatments, clear images of your team and branded vehicles, and close-ups of specific pest evidence or equipment in action. These visuals build credibility, demonstrate professionalism, and help customers choose your service over competitors.
