The three fixes on 80% of audited real estate agent profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many Liverpool real estate agents miss a huge opportunity by only using a few sentences in their Google Business Profile description. Google allows 750 characters, giving you ample space to detail your specialisations – from first-time buyer support in Crosby to luxury property sales in Woolton, or letting services near the city centre. A comprehensive description builds trust and helps your profile rank for relevant, specific searches.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    An incomplete list of categories on your profile means you're not appearing in all relevant searches. Beyond the primary "Real estate agency," I often find agents in Liverpool are missing crucial secondary categories that perfectly describe their services. Adding categories like "Buyer's agent," "Seller's agent," or "Property manager" can significantly broaden your reach and help potential clients find your specific expertise.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    First impressions matter, especially in real estate. Many profiles I audit for Liverpool agents have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, often lacking crucial visual content. Clients want to see your team, your office exterior (especially if you have a high street presence), successful "sold" boards around Liverpool neighbourhoods, and even community involvement photos. High-quality, diverse images significantly boost engagement and trust.
