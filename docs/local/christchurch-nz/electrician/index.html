The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Christchurch electricians leave their Google Business Profile description far too brief, often under 400 characters. This wastes valuable space to showcase your specializations like solar panel wiring, emergency call-outs, or smart home installations. A comprehensive description, utilizing the full 750 characters, allows you to integrate relevant keywords and build immediate trust by detailing your expertise, service areas within Christchurch, and commitment to safety standards.

  • Missing Critical Secondary Categories

    Relying solely on "Electrician" as your primary category drastically limits visibility for niche services. I often find profiles lacking vital secondary categories. Adding specific options like "Emergency Electrician," "EV Charging Station Installation Service," and "Circuit Breaker Installation Service" directly tells Google what specific services you offer. This helps you appear in searches for those exact needs across Christchurch, attracting highly qualified local leads.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A Google Business Profile with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos misses a huge opportunity to build credibility. Many electricians neglect uploading images of their team in uniform, branded vans, toolkits, safety gear, or "before and after" shots of switchboard upgrades, new wiring installations, or lighting projects. High-quality, relevant photos convey professionalism and demonstrate your work, helping Christchurch customers feel more confident in choosing your services over a competitor.
