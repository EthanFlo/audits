The three fixes on 80% of audited dermatologist profiles

  • Your Business Description is Selling You Short

    Many Cincinnati dermatologists underutilize the Google Business Profile description, often sticking to a generic one-liner. With Google allowing up to 750 characters, a description under 400 characters misses a critical chance to detail your specialized services like advanced acne treatments, anti-aging solutions, or comprehensive skin cancer screenings. This prime real estate should communicate your unique value to potential patients searching specifically for your expertise.

  • Crucial Secondary Categories Are Absent

    Google Business Profile allows you to specify multiple categories, but many dermatology practices only list "Dermatologist." We frequently find critical secondary categories missing. Adding specific services like "Cosmetic Dermatology," "Skin Cancer Screening Service," and "Pediatric Dermatology" helps Google understand your full scope of practice. This ensures you appear in niche searches that your primary category alone might miss, directly connecting you with patients needing those specific services.

  • A Visual Story is Missing from Your Profile

    Profiles often lack a robust collection of owner-uploaded photos. We consistently see fewer than the recommended 30 images, leaving a blank canvas where trust and professionalism should be built. Missing are inviting photos of your waiting areas, clean treatment rooms, friendly staff, and professional doctor headshots. These visuals are vital for Cincinnati patients seeking a welcoming and expert environment before they even step into your practice.
