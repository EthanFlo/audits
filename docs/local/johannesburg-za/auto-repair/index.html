The three fixes on 80% of audited auto repair shop profiles

Your Business Description is Too Short and Generic

Many Johannesburg auto repair shops only use a few sentences in their Google Business Profile description, missing a huge opportunity to showcase their expertise. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles barely hit 200. This space is crucial for detailing your specialised services like diagnostics, engine overhauls, and preventative maintenance, or highlighting what sets your JHB shop apart, such as customer service awards or specific vehicle expertise. A detailed description helps both search engines and potential clients understand your full value.

Crucial Secondary Categories Are Often Unselected

Many auto repair profiles only list "Auto Repair Shop" as their primary category and stop there. This severely limits visibility for specific services that local drivers are searching for. For shops in Johannesburg, it's vital to add secondary categories such as "Oil Change Service," "Brake Shop," and "Tire Shop." These specific additions tell Google exactly what services you offer, allowing your business to appear in more targeted searches and capture customers looking for immediate, specific automotive help.

Your Profile Lacks Sufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

A picture is worth a thousand rands, especially for auto repair shops in Johannesburg. We often find profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, leaving potential customers guessing about your premises and capabilities. High-quality images of your workshop interior, diagnostic equipment in use, your friendly mechanics, a clean waiting area, and before-and-after repair shots build trust and transparency. More photos help customers visualise your professional environment and feel confident choosing your shop over a competitor.
