The three fixes on 80% of audited roofing contractor profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many roofing contractors use a brief, generic description that barely scratches the surface. Google allows 750 characters, yet most profiles only use a fraction. This means you are missing crucial opportunities to highlight your expertise in specific areas like tile, metal, or flat roof repairs, guttering, and servicing key Melbourne suburbs, making your profile less appealing and harder to find.

  • Incomplete Service Categories

    Google Business Profile offers multiple categories, but many roofing contractors only select "Roofer". You are missing out on vital search visibility by not utilising secondary categories like "Roof Repair Service", "Emergency Roofing Service", or "Roof Inspection Service". These specific categories ensure you show up when customers search for their exact needs, directly boosting your local relevance.

  • Insufficient Visual Proof

    A Google Business Profile with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos tells potential clients very little. They want to see proof of your work. Missing are clear before and after shots of repairs, full roof replacements, your crew in uniform on job sites, and branded vehicles. Visuals build trust and demonstrate your professionalism far more effectively.
