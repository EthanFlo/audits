The three fixes on 80% of audited real estate agent profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many Leeds real estate agents use only a few sentences to describe their business on Google Business Profile, missing a crucial opportunity. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most struggle to reach even 400. This space is perfect for detailing your unique selling points, the specific Leeds neighbourhoods you serve, your specialisms like first-time buyers or luxury properties, and your commitment to client satisfaction, making you stand out.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Often, real estate agents only select "Real Estate Agent" as their primary category. This limits your visibility significantly. For agents in Leeds, expanding your categories is vital. You should also consider "Buyer's Agent," "Seller's Agent," and "Property Management Company" to capture a wider range of local searches. This tells Google exactly what services you offer beyond just general agency work.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Your Google Business Profile should be a visual showcase, yet many agents have fewer than 10 photos. We consistently find Leeds agents missing crucial imagery. This includes professional headshots of your team, interior and exterior shots of your Leeds office, local area photos, and high-quality images of properties you have successfully sold or let, always with client consent. Visuals build trust and engagement.
