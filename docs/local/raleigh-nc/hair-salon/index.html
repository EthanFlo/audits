SECTION 2 — THREE FINDINGS

1. Your Business Description Sells You Short (Under 400 Characters)

Many Raleigh hair salons use a Google Business Profile description that barely scratches the surface, often staying under 400 of the allowed 750 characters. This leaves out crucial details like your specialty in balayage, expertise in natural hair, or your relaxing spa-like atmosphere. You're missing a key opportunity to inform potential clients about what makes your salon unique and why they should choose you over the competition right here in Raleigh.

2. Missing Critical Secondary Categories

Google Business Profile lets you add multiple categories beyond just "Hair Salon." Many Raleigh salons overlook secondary categories like "Hair Colorist," "Hair Extension Technician," or "Bridal Hair Stylist." Without these, you're invisible to clients specifically searching for these high-value services. Maximize your visibility by accurately reflecting your full range of expertise.

3. Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

A picture is worth a thousand bookings, yet many salon profiles have very few owner-uploaded photos. You're likely missing captivating shots of your stylists in action, stunning before-and-after transformations, your inviting salon interior, product displays, and happy clients (with permission). These visuals are essential for showing off your skills and attracting new clientele in Raleigh, NC.

Fixing these Google Business Profile issues isn't just good practice; it's essential for hair salons operating in Raleigh, NC, specifically. A well-optimized profile with detailed descriptions and relevant secondary categories ensures you appear for these nuanced, high-intent searches, directly matching your services to what Raleigh clients are actively seeking.
