Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-02

Who we are

Flo Marketing is a one-person consultancy run by Ethan Florendo from Vancouver, BC, Canada. Contact: florendo.ethan@gmail.com.

What we collect

Prospects (cold email recipients): business name, public email address, city, primary business category, website URL, Google Business Profile URL. All data is scraped from publicly available sources (Google Places, business directories, or the business's own website). We do not collect personal identifiers beyond what's listed on your public business profile.

Website visitors (ethanflo.github.io/audits/): we do not use analytics cookies or tracking pixels by default. If you submit the chat form, we collect the information you provide (business name, email, GBP URL). Your IP + user-agent are logged for security and rate-limit purposes only.

Customers (via Stripe / Upwork): payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe (buy.stripe.com) or Upwork. We never see or store your credit card details. We receive only your name, email, and purchase line items.

How we use it

Prospect data is used to send at most one or two cold outreach emails introducing our audit service. Each email includes a clear unsubscribe mechanism (reply STOP).

Visitor/customer data is used solely to deliver the audit and implementation you purchased, and to follow up on delivery quality.

Who we share with

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.

We share with necessary vendors only: Stripe (payment processing), Upwork (if you purchased via Upwork), Gmail (email delivery), Google Cloud / Gemini API (audit generation — your GBP URL and business name are sent as API input). No vendor receives more than what's required for their specific function.

How to opt out / delete data

Cold email: reply STOP to any message. Your email address is immediately added to our opt-out ledger, and you will not receive further emails from us.

Data deletion: email florendo.ethan@gmail.com with "Delete my data" in the subject. We will remove you from all prospect lists, opt-out ledgers, and active session databases within 7 business days.

Data access: email the same address with "Data access request" to receive a copy of what we hold about you.

Data retention

Prospect data: retained until you opt out, then kept in opt-out ledger only.

Customer data: retained for the duration of the engagement plus 7 years for tax/accounting purposes.

Chat sessions: retained for 90 days, then deleted.

Security

Our server runs on a personal Mac behind a Cloudflare tunnel. chat.db (containing session data) is local and never committed to git. Payment data is never seen by us (handled by Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure). All inter-service communication uses HTTPS.

Your rights under Canadian PIPEDA / EU GDPR / California CCPA

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. See the opt-out / deletion section above for how to exercise these rights. See also Data Subject Requests.

Changes

We update this policy when our practices change. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change. Material changes will be communicated to customers via email.
