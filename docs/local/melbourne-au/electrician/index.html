The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many Melbourne electricians miss out on crucial ranking signals by having a Google Business Profile description that's too short and generic. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles I review are under 400. This is a missed opportunity to include essential keywords like "emergency electrical repair Melbourne," "residential switchboard upgrade," or "commercial lighting installation," which helps Google understand your exact services and match you with local customers.

  • Missing Secondary Service Categories

    Your primary category might be "Electrician," but that's just the start. Many profiles are missing vital secondary categories that signal your specific expertise to Google and potential clients. I often find profiles lacking categories such as "Emergency Electrician," "EV Charging Station Installation," or "Electrical Panel Upgrades." Adding these ensures you appear in highly targeted searches, differentiating you from general electrical services in Melbourne.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Visual content is king, especially for local businesses. Most electrician profiles have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, which is a major gap. Customers want to see your work, your team, and your professionalism. Critical missing photos often include before-and-after project shots, your team safely at work, branded service vehicles, specific equipment in use, and clear images of your qualified Melbourne electricians.
