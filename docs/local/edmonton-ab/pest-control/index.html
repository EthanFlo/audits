The three fixes on 80% of audited pest control profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many Edmonton pest control companies underutilize their Google Business Profile description, often leaving it well under Google’s generous 750-character limit. This shortchanges your opportunity to showcase your specialties like spider control, wasp nest removal, or seasonal prevention services. A detailed, keyword-rich description helps both search engines and potential clients understand exactly what pest issues you solve, helping you stand out from the crowd.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Often, pest control businesses only select "Pest Control Service" as their primary category and overlook the power of secondary categories. For Edmonton-based companies, adding specific services like "Termite Control," "Rodent Exterminator," and "Bed Bug Exterminator" is vital. These precise categories tell Google exactly what specialized services you offer, significantly improving your visibility for specific, high-intent searches from residents dealing with these particular infestations.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A common audit finding is a scarcity of owner-uploaded photos. Instead of just a logo, your pest control profile should showcase high-quality images of your team in uniform, branded vehicles, before-and-after treatment areas (respecting privacy), and even specific pest identification photos. These visuals build trust and demonstrate professionalism to potential clients in Edmonton. Google prioritizes businesses with rich visual content, which helps you convert views into calls.
