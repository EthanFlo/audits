The three fixes on 80% of audited pest control profiles

  • Your Business Description is Selling You Short

    Many Cape Town pest control businesses miss out on valuable search visibility by having a short, generic Google Business Profile description. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most use less than 400. This is a missed opportunity to highlight your specific expertise in tackling common Cape Town pests like termites or rodents, to mention eco-friendly options, or emergency services that truly set you apart locally.

  • Neglecting Niche Service Categories

    Beyond the primary 'Pest Control Service' category, neglecting to add specific secondary categories severely limits your reach. For pest control in Cape Town, you should be utilizing categories like 'Termite Control', 'Rodent Control', and 'Bed Bug Exterminator'. These help Google understand the breadth of your services, ensuring your profile appears for highly specific searches from clients needing targeted solutions, rather than just general pest help.

  • Your Photo Gallery is Understocked

    A strong visual presence builds trust and showcases professionalism. Many Cape Town pest control profiles lack a robust gallery of at least 30 owner-uploaded photos. You should be including clear images of your uniformed team, branded vehicles, before-and-after treatment results (discreetly), specific tools, and shots of your office or service areas. These images reassure potential clients and highlight your local, professional presence effectively.
