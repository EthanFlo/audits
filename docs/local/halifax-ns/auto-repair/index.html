The three fixes on 80% of audited auto repair shop profiles

  • Business Description Under 400 Characters

    Many Halifax auto repair shops aren't using their full Google Business Profile description. Google allows 750 characters, yet most profiles I audit only use a fraction. This means you're missing a crucial chance to highlight your unique services, specialities like European car repair, or customer service ethos to potential clients actively searching for a reliable mechanic in Halifax.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Beyond "Auto Repair Shop," Google offers many specific categories to help customers find you for precise needs. I frequently find profiles missing vital secondary categories like "Oil change service," "Brake shop," or "Tire repair shop." Without these, you're invisible to Halifax drivers specifically searching for these common services, giving business to competitors who have them listed.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    First impressions matter, especially for an auto repair shop where trust is paramount. I often see profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. This means you're not showcasing your clean bays, professional technicians, comfortable waiting area, or the types of vehicles you service. Halifax customers want to see where their car will be worked on before they even call.
