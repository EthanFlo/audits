When the agency model makes sense

Be fair: agencies make sense for certain businesses. If you have:

  • Multiple locations (10+ GBP listings to manage)
  • An enterprise budget where retainers are not a line-item concern
  • A complex vertical with specialized compliance (medical, legal) that needs ongoing oversight
  • A need for broader digital marketing beyond just GBP (paid ads, content, link building)

If you are a single-location SMB or a small multi-location operator with 2-5 spots, the $18k-36k/year retainer is overkill. Flo Marketing's $287 one-time fits better.
