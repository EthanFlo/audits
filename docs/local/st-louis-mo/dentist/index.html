The three fixes on 80% of audited dentist profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many St. Louis dental practices underutilize the Google Business Profile description. Often, I find descriptions that are barely a few sentences long, failing to use the available 750 characters. This is a missed opportunity to showcase your unique services, highlight your practice's philosophy, and include important keywords like "family dentistry" or "implant dentistry" that help patients find you. A comprehensive description helps Google understand exactly what you offer and matches you with relevant local searches.

  • You're Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    While "Dentist" is your primary category, many St. Louis dentists neglect adding vital secondary categories that expand their reach. I frequently discover profiles missing key classifications like "Cosmetic Dentist," "Pediatric Dentist," or "Emergency Dental Service." Without these, you're invisible to patients specifically seeking those specialized services. Adding precise secondary categories tells Google exactly who you serve, improving your visibility for highly specific and valuable patient searches within St. Louis neighborhoods.

  • Your Profile Lacks Sufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A surprising number of St. Louis dental practices have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, relying instead on user contributions or stock images. This means patients aren't seeing your modern treatment rooms, your friendly front desk team, patient testimonials with consent, or before-and-after smile transformations. High-quality, relevant photos build trust and give potential patients a real feel for your practice before they even call. More photos mean higher engagement and a better chance of converting a viewer into an appointment.

Why these matter specifically for dentists in St. Louis, MO

In a city as established as St. Louis, the sheer density of dental practices means competition for new patients is fierce. Being on the first page of Google Maps for "dentist near me" isn't just nice, it's essential. Our unique neighborhood structure, from The Hill to Central West End, means patients often search very locally. If your profile isn't fully optimized, a patient just two blocks away might choose a competitor simply because their profile was more visible and complete in a hyper-local search.

An incomplete Google Business Profile with missing categories means you might be overlooked by someone specifically seeking a "Pediatric Dentist in Kirkwood" or an "Emergency Dentist in Clayton" even if you are perfectly situated and offer those services. You're essentially invisible to a highly motivated patient base who value convenience.

Furthermore, patient search behavior for dental care is increasingly visual and trust-based. Beyond just location, St. Louis patients want to see who you are and what your practice feels like before committing. A profile lacking engaging photos of your actual clinic, staff, or unique technology, especially when competitors are showcasing theirs, creates a perception of an outdated or less transparent practice. In a city where word-of-mouth still holds sway, a polished online first impression through Google Business Profile is the digital equivalent of a strong referral.
