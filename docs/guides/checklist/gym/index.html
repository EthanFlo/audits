30-point Google Business Profile checklist for gyms

This 30-point Google Business Profile checklist isn't just general advice; it's a precise, actionable sequence designed for immediate impact. Think of it as a step-by-step blueprint that cuts through the noise, guiding your gym through specific optimizations rather than vague suggestions. By focusing on concrete tasks, this checklist ensures you address every critical aspect of your online presence, leaving no stone unturned in the quest for local search dominance.

Imagine a slow afternoon at your gym – perhaps between peak hours or during a quiet Sunday. This checklist provides the exact framework for your marketing team, or even yourself, to methodically enhance your Google Business Profile. It’s an investment of focused time that promises significant returns, directly translating into increased visibility, more inquiries, and ultimately, a surge in new members discovering your CrossFit box, yoga studio, or personal training facility right when they need it.

The three fixes on 80% of audited gym profiles

Business Info

  • ☐ Verify your gym's precise street address matches official records for accurate navigation.
  • ☐ Update holiday hours, especially for Christmas and New Year, to avoid visitor confusion.
  • ☐ Ensure your primary website link points directly to your gym's membership page or homepage.
  • ☐ Confirm your phone number is clickable on mobile and rings directly to the front desk.
  • ☐ Craft a compelling 750-character business description highlighting your unique gym offerings.

Categories

  • ☐ Set "Gym" or "Fitness Center" as your primary category for maximum visibility.
  • ☐ Add "CrossFit Gym" if applicable, to attract niche fitness enthusiasts effectively.
  • ☐ Include "Yoga Studio" if classes are a core offering, broadening your potential reach.
  • ☐ Select "Personal Trainer" if one-on-one coaching is a significant service provided.
  • ☐ Review all available categories to find every relevant fit for your diverse gym services.

Photos

  • ☐ Upload a high-resolution logo that is instantly recognizable as your gym's brand.
  • ☐ Choose a vibrant cover photo showcasing your gym's main workout area or a popular class.
  • ☐ Add at least five high-quality interior photos highlighting equipment and cleanliness.
  • ☐ Include exterior photos from various angles to help members easily locate your gym.
  • ☐ Post photos of happy members actively participating in gym classes or personal training sessions.

Services

  • ☐ List all membership tiers, including drop-in rates for new gym visitors.
  • ☐ Detail specific fitness classes like HIIT, spinning, or Zumba with brief descriptions.
  • ☐ Add personal training packages, specifying one-on-one or small group options.
  • ☐ Include any unique amenities such as childcare, sauna, or smoothie bar.
  • ☐ Clearly state pricing or how to obtain pricing for each gym service listed.

Reviews

  • ☐ Respond to every new gym review within 24-48 hours, showing active engagement.
  • ☐ Thank members for positive feedback, specifically mentioning aspects they loved about your gym.
  • ☐ Politely address negative reviews, offering solutions or inviting offline discussion.
  • ☐ Encourage satisfied members to leave reviews by placing a QR code at your gym's front desk.
  • ☐ Use keywords from your gym's services when responding to reinforce search relevance.

Posts

  • ☐ Create a weekly "Offer" post promoting a trial membership or discounted class pack.
  • ☐ Announce new gym classes or trainer introductions using the "Update" post type.
  • ☐ Highlight upcoming fitness challenges or workshops with an "Event" post, including dates.
  • ☐ Share success stories of gym members using a "What's New" post with a compelling photo.
  • ☐ Regularly link "Call to action" buttons in posts directly to relevant gym sign-up pages.

Why these matter specifically for gyms in Checklist

Once all 30 items are meticulously checked off, the real work of measurement begins. Navigate to your Google Business Profile Insights to monitor key metrics like searches, map views, and website clicks. Allow a minimum of 14 days for Google's algorithms to fully index and reflect these updates across its platform. After this period, analyze the impact and then loop back to the start, repeating this checklist quarterly or bi-annually to maintain optimal local search performance and ensure your gym remains highly visible.
