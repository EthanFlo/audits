The three fixes on 80% of audited roofing contractor profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short and Generic

    Many Sacramento roofing contractors use a short, unoptimized description that barely hits 200 characters. Google allows up to 750 characters, a crucial space to highlight your specialized services like asphalt shingle replacement, tile roof repairs, or gutter installation. A detailed description allows you to weave in keywords like 'Sacramento emergency roof repair' and clearly communicate your unique selling propositions, setting you apart from local competition and attracting precise customer needs.

  • Neglecting Critical Secondary Categories

    Beyond "Roofing Contractor," many profiles are missing vital secondary categories. This oversight significantly limits how many relevant searches your business can appear for. I frequently find Sacramento roofing companies are missing "Roof Repair," "Emergency Roofing Service," and "Roof Inspection Service." Adding these accurately broadens your reach, ensuring homeowners searching for specific solutions find *your* business, not just a general contractor.

  • Lack of High-Quality, Relevant Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Google profiles often have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, hindering visual appeal and trust. I often see profiles missing crucial visuals like before-and-after shots of completed roof replacements, photos of your team safely working on Sacramento homes, close-ups of specific material installations (e.g., solar shingles), and pictures of your branded vehicles. These images build credibility and show the quality and scope of your roofing projects.
