The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description (Under 400 Characters)

    Many Newcastle electricians aren't leveraging the full 750 characters Google allows for their business description. This often means missing out on vital keywords and detailed explanations of services like switchboard upgrades, safety inspections, or bespoke commercial fit-outs. A brief description leaves potential customers guessing about your full range of expertise, making it harder for Google to rank you for diverse electrician services in the local area.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Your Google Business Profile should accurately reflect all your specialized services, but many profiles only list "Electrician." We often find missing secondary categories like "Emergency Electrician," "EV Charging Station Installation Service," or "Electrical Panel Upgrade Service." Adding these helps Google understand your specific offerings, connecting you directly with customers searching for these niche yet high-value services across Newcastle suburbs.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Visual content is key, yet most electrician profiles have fewer than 30 unique, owner-uploaded photos. This means you're likely missing images of your team in uniform, branded vehicles, completed jobs (like neat wiring or installed lighting), safety equipment, or before-and-after shots of electrical repairs. High-quality, diverse photos build trust and showcase your professionalism to potential clients in Newcastle.
