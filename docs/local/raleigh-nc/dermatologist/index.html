The three fixes on 80% of audited dermatologist profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Raleigh dermatologist profiles feature a brief, generic description that fails to use the full 750 characters Google allows. This is a missed chance to highlight your specialized services, patient-care philosophy, or unique offerings like advanced laser treatments or specialized pediatric dermatology, directly influencing why a patient chooses your practice over another.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    While "Dermatologist" is a primary category, many local practices neglect secondary categories that help Google understand their full scope. I often find profiles missing "Cosmetic Dermatologist," "Skin Cancer Clinic," or "Pediatric Dermatologist." Without these, you're invisible to patients searching for your specific expertise in the Raleigh area.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Most profiles have very few, or no, high-quality, owner-uploaded photos. Patients want to see your modern waiting area, the friendly front desk, specialized treatment rooms, and your expert team. Neglecting images of your clinic exterior, interior, staff, and cutting-edge equipment leaves a crucial visual gap that local competitors are likely filling.
