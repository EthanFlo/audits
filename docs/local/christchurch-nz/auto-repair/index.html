The three fixes on 80% of audited auto repair shop profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many auto repair shops in Christchurch are missing a massive opportunity by leaving their Google Business Profile description almost empty. Google allows up to 750 characters, but I frequently see profiles with only a couple of sentences. This means you’re not communicating your unique expertise, your specialty services like WOF checks, engine diagnostics, or transmission repairs, or your commitment to local Christchurch drivers. It's prime real estate to tell potential customers why they should choose you over anyone else.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Your primary category might be 'Auto Repair Shop,' but many Christchurch businesses stop there. Google allows multiple secondary categories, yet most profiles I audit are missing these vital additions. By not including specific services like 'Oil Change Service,' 'Brake Shop,' and 'Tire Shop,' you're effectively invisible to customers searching directly for those services. This dramatically limits your reach to local drivers specifically looking for these common repairs in Christchurch, sending them to competitors who have listed them.

  • Scant Photos Failing to Showcase Your Shop

    A visually barren Google Business Profile hurts your credibility. I often find auto repair shops in Christchurch with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, missing out on vital opportunities to show off their business. Customers want to see your clean workshop, your certified mechanics at work, the reception area, specific tools or diagnostic equipment, and even before-and-after shots of repairs. Without these, your profile feels incomplete and untrustworthy, making it harder for potential clients to choose you for their vehicle's needs.
