The three fixes on 80% of audited hair salon profiles

Shortchanged Business Description

Many Jacksonville hair salons use a Google Business Profile description that's far too brief, often under 400 characters, when Google allows up to 750. This means you’re missing a crucial opportunity to showcase your unique services like specialized color techniques, trendy cuts, or your relaxing salon atmosphere. A detailed description helps potential clients understand your brand identity, the client experience you offer, and why they should choose your salon over competitors, influencing their booking decision significantly.

Missing Key Secondary Categories

Beyond "Hair Salon," many profiles are missing vital secondary categories that capture specific client searches. For instance, without "Color Specialist," "Men's Barber," or "Bridal Salon," you're invisible to people specifically seeking those services. Google uses these categories to match user intent with your offerings, so neglecting them means you're not showing up for highly targeted searches, allowing competitors who list them to capture valuable local traffic in Jacksonville.

Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

A surprising number of hair salon profiles feature fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, relying heavily on customer submissions. You’re missing chances to visually tell your story and attract new clients. High-quality images of various hairstyles, fresh cuts, vibrant color work, your clean and inviting salon interior, premium products, and your friendly team are essential. These visuals build trust, showcase your expertise, and give clients a compelling reason to visit your Jacksonville salon.
