The three fixes on 80% of audited dermatologist profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Perth dermatologists are using a Google Business Profile description far shorter than the 750-character limit, often less than 400 characters. This missed opportunity means you aren't showcasing critical specialties like skin cancer detection, advanced acne treatments, or cosmetic dermatology services that patients specifically search for. A detailed description helps Google understand your full service offering and improves relevance for diverse patient queries.

  • Neglecting Secondary Service Categories

    Beyond "Dermatologist," many profiles miss crucial secondary categories. For optimal visibility in Perth, your practice should be accurately categorised for specific services. Common omissions include "Cosmetic Dermatologist," "Skin Cancer Screening Service," and "Paediatric Dermatologist." Adding these significantly broadens your reach, ensuring you appear for more niche yet highly relevant patient searches.

  • Insufficient Visual Storytelling

    Profiles often have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, failing to visually engage potential patients. Missing photos typically include high-quality images of your clinic’s welcoming reception area, modern treatment rooms, the friendly faces of your medical team (with consent), and even tasteful visual explanations of common procedures. These visuals build trust and help patients connect with your practice before they even call.
