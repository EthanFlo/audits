The three fixes on 80% of audited med spa profiles

  • Your Google Business Profile Description is Too Short

    Many Brisbane med spas use a brief, often generic description that barely scratches the surface of their unique offerings. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles I review barely hit 400. This is a missed opportunity to showcase your signature treatments, highlight your advanced technology, or articulate your clinic's specific approach to client care. A well-crafted description packed with relevant keywords is crucial for attracting the right clientele.

  • You're Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Beyond 'Med Spa,' many profiles neglect to utilise vital secondary categories. This severely limits how often you appear in specific local searches. For example, if you offer premium services like Botox, Laser Hair Removal, or Microneedling, but these aren't listed as secondary categories, you're invisible to potential clients searching specifically for those treatments in Brisbane. Optimising these tells Google exactly what services you specialise in.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    First impressions are vital, and for a med spa, visual appeal can make or break a booking. Many Brisbane med spa profiles have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, often lacking key visuals. Clients want to see your modern treatment rooms, your friendly team in action, or high-quality before-and-after results (where permitted). A sparse photo gallery fails to convey the professionalism and luxurious, trustworthy experience you offer, prompting potential clients to look elsewhere.
