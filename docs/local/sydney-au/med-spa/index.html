The three fixes on 80% of audited med spa profiles

  • Business Description Under 400 Characters

    Med spas often overlook the power of their Google Business Profile description. I frequently see profiles with brief, uninspired descriptions that are well under Google's 750-character limit. This means you're missing a critical opportunity to detail your unique treatments like injectables, advanced facials, and body contouring, showcase your luxurious Sydney clinic environment, and convey your expertise, directly impacting how potential clients perceive your brand.

  • Missing Secondary Categories

    Many Sydney med spas only list "Med Spa" as their primary category and stop there. An audit often reveals a complete absence of vital secondary categories. Adding precise categories such as "Botox Clinic," "Laser Hair Removal Service," and "Skin Care Clinic" ensures your business appears in highly specific searches, capturing clients looking for exact treatments rather than just a general med spa.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    It's common to find med spa profiles with a sparse photo gallery, often fewer than 30 owner-uploaded images. This leaves potential clients guessing about your space and services. Critical missing photos typically include high-quality images of treatment rooms, the welcoming reception area, staff in action (with consent), detailed before-and-after results for various treatments, and close-ups of specific equipment used, failing to showcase your Sydney facility's full appeal.
