The three fixes on 80% of audited auto repair shop profiles

  • Your Business Description Isn't Working Hard Enough

    Many auto repair shops in Hamilton only use a fraction of the available 750 characters for their Google Business Profile description. This is a missed opportunity to showcase your expertise, specific services like WOF inspections or fleet maintenance, and what makes your shop unique. A short, generic description fails to capture attention or include important keywords that potential customers are searching for, meaning fewer people click through to learn more about your reliable services.

  • Overlooking Crucial Secondary Categories

    Often, auto repair shops only list "Auto Repair Shop" as their primary category and forget to add secondary ones. I frequently find profiles missing vital categories like 'Oil Change Service', 'Brake Shop', or 'Tire Shop'. Without these, you're invisible to Hamilton drivers specifically searching for those exact services, handing those customers directly to competitors who have fully optimised their profile categories.

  • A Scarcity of Shop-Defining Photos

    Most auto repair shop profiles in Hamilton suffer from a lack of quality owner-uploaded photos. I see profiles with less than 30 images, often missing crucial visual elements. Customers want to see your clean workshop, the friendly faces of your mechanics, diagnostic equipment, comfortable waiting areas, and examples of quality repairs. Without these detailed visuals, your profile feels impersonal and fails to build trust or showcase the professional environment and expertise you offer potential customers.
