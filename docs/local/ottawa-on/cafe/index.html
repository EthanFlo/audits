I frequently hear from Ottawa cafe owners frustrated by empty tables, wondering why their phone isn't ringing for catering orders, or why they keep seeing their competitors dominate local Google Maps searches. The three fixes on 80% of audited cafe profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many Ottawa cafes leave valuable space unused in their Google Business Profile description, often coming in under 400 characters when Google allows up to 750. This means you're missing opportunities to highlight your unique Ethiopian single-origin blend, your seasonal vegan pastry selection, or your cozy, dog-friendly patio perfect for downtown dwellers. A robust description packed with relevant keywords about your offerings and ambiance helps Google understand your business better, connecting you with customers searching for exactly what you offer.

  • Missing Secondary Categories

    Beyond the primary "Cafe" category, Google allows several secondary categories to further define your business. I often find Ottawa cafes missing out on crucial classifications that could capture more specific searches. For instance, if you offer brunch, you should also be listed as a "Brunch Restaurant." If you bake your own goods, "Bakery" is a must. And if coffee is your main draw, "Coffee Shop" is essential to catch those hyper-specific searches. Properly categorizing your business dramatically improves discoverability.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Visuals are paramount for cafes, especially in a city as vibrant as Ottawa. I consistently see profiles with fewer than 30 high-quality photos uploaded directly by the business owner. Customers want to see your expertly crafted latte art, the inviting interior and exterior of your space, your smiling baristas, and the delicious food items like your signature Eggs Benedict or artisan sandwiches. Missing these visuals means missing out on first impressions that convert scrollers into customers walking through your door.

Why these matter specifically for cafes in Ottawa, ON

These seemingly small details in your Google Business Profile aren't just minor oversights; for cafes in Ottawa, ON, they are critical to survival and growth. Ottawa's cafe scene is incredibly competitive, with a high density of excellent establishments from the ByWard Market to Westboro. Standing out isn't just about having great coffee; it's about being visible precisely when a potential customer is looking.

Commute patterns across Ottawa mean many people are searching for "coffee near me" or "brunch Ottawa" on their phones, often while walking or driving to work, or exploring a new neighbourhood. These searches are high-intent, and Google Business Profile is the primary tool they use to make immediate decisions. An incomplete or poorly optimized profile means you’re essentially invisible during these crucial decision-making moments.

Ottawa customers, whether they're Parliament Hill staffers, university students, or local families, often have specific needs: a cafe with fast Wi-Fi, a kid-friendly spot, a quiet place for meetings, or a patio for those beautiful Ottawa summers. If your profile doesn't explicitly showcase these features through detailed descriptions and relevant photos, you're losing out to competitors who do. People aren't going to click through to your website if your Google Business Profile doesn't grab them instantly.
