The three fixes on 80% of audited locksmith profiles

SECTION 2 — THREE FINDINGS

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many Auckland locksmith profiles use a description well under Google’s 750-character limit, often barely reaching 200-300 characters. This means crucial keywords for services like "emergency lockout", "car key replacement", or specific Auckland suburbs you serve are missing. A short description fails to tell Google and potential customers the full scope of your locksmith expertise, limiting your visibility for diverse, high-value searches.

  • Missing Secondary Categories

    A common oversight is only listing "Locksmith" as the primary category, neglecting vital secondary categories. Locksmiths in Auckland should leverage options like "Emergency Locksmith", "Auto Locksmith", and "Safe Opening Service". Without these specific categories, you're invisible to customers searching for specialised services, effectively hiding your diverse skills from people who need a specific type of locksmith urgently.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    I frequently find locksmith profiles with sparse photo galleries, often fewer than 10 owner-uploaded images. This misses a huge opportunity to build trust and showcase professionalism. Customers want to see photos of your team, branded service vehicles, examples of different locks you service, your workshop, and even your technicians at work. High-quality, diverse photos differentiate you from competitors and convey reliability.

Why these matter specifically for locksmiths in Auckland, NZ

A comprehensive Google Business Profile, brimming with relevant photos and a detailed description, builds immediate credibility.
