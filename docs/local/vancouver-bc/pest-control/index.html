The three fixes on 80% of audited pest control profiles

  • 1. Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many Vancouver pest control businesses use a sparse Google Business Profile description, missing out on valuable keywords and space to describe their full range of services. Google allows up to 750 characters, but I often find profiles with less than 400, barely touching on rodent control, ant extermination, or emergency services, which means fewer chances to rank for specific pest issues customers are searching for.

  • 2. You're Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Beyond "Pest Control Service" as your primary category, many profiles neglect crucial secondary categories that help Google understand your specializations. Commonly overlooked ones include "Termite Control," "Rodent Exterminator," and "Bed Bug Exterminator." Without these, your Vancouver pest control business might not appear for highly specific, high-intent searches.

  • 3. Too Few Owner-Uploaded Photos

    First impressions matter, especially for a service like pest control. I frequently see profiles with less than 30 owner-uploaded photos, featuring minimal examples of your work, your team, or your service vehicles. Customers want to see before-and-after photos of infestations, your technicians in action, or clear images of your vans serving Vancouver neighborhoods, building trust and conveying professionalism.
