The three fixes on 80% of audited photographer profiles

  • Too Short, Too Vague: Your Google Business Profile Description

    Many Phoenix photographers miss a huge opportunity by only using a short, generic description on their GBP. Google allows up to 750 characters, but most profiles I audit are well under 400. This is your chance to showcase your unique style, specialties (weddings, portraits, commercial), and what makes you the go-to photographer in the Valley, directly impacting how potential clients perceive and find you.

  • Overlooking Vital Secondary Categories

    Your primary category might be "Photographer," but without relevant secondary categories, you're invisible for more specific searches. For Phoenix photographers, commonly missed but crucial secondary categories include "Wedding Photographer," "Portrait Studio," and "Real Estate Photographer." Adding these precisely tells Google what services you offer, significantly broadening your reach to specific client needs searching across Scottsdale, Tempe, and Mesa.

  • A Scarcity of High-Quality, Relevant Photos

    Photographers understand visuals, yet many Google Business Profiles I review have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. Beyond stunning portfolio shots, you're likely missing crucial images: interior/exterior of your studio (if applicable), behind-the-scenes glimpses, photos of your team, and client interactions. For Phoenix clients, seeing the face behind the lens and the professional setup builds trust and showcases your brand's personality, encouraging engagement over competitors.

Why these matter specifically for photographers in Phoenix, AZ

Phoenix and its surrounding cities (Scottsdale, Tempe, Chandler) boast a highly competitive photography market. With so many talented artists vying for attention, an optimized Google Business Profile isn't just a bonus—it's essential for standing out. Generic or incomplete profiles simply get lost in the shuffle, pushing excellent local photographers further down the search results while competitors with better GBP listings capture valuable leads.

Phoenix is a sprawling metro area, and clients often search for photographers based on proximity or willingness to travel for specific backdrops like the desert landscapes or urban settings. An incomplete GBP misses opportunities to rank for hyper-local searches, or for clients in a specific part of the Valley looking for a photographer near them. Clear categories and a descriptive profile help clients understand your service area and specialties for their specific event or project.

Potential photography clients in Phoenix aren't just looking for "a photographer." They're searching for "wedding photographer Scottsdale," "family photos Phoenix," or "commercial photography Tempe." Your GBP, with its categories, description, and photos, directly impacts your ability to appear for these specific, high-intent searches. Without a fully optimized profile, you're effectively invisible to clients actively looking to book the exact services you offer, leaving bookings on the table.
