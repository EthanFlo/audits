The three fixes on 80% of audited moving company profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many Edinburgh moving companies use generic 200-300 character descriptions, missing the chance to highlight unique services like student moves, antique transport, or specific Edinburgh neighbourhoods served. This limits their ability to capture relevant long-tail searches and convey their full value to local customers.

  • Overlooking Key Secondary Categories

    Many Edinburgh movers only list "Moving Company." I often find profiles missing crucial secondary categories like "Long Distance Movers," "Office Relocation Service," or "Piano Mover." This oversight means you're not appearing in specific searches where your specialist services are exactly what local customers need, effectively hiding your expertise from potential bookings in areas you excel.

  • Lack of High-Quality Owner Photos

    I frequently see Edinburgh moving companies with fewer than 10 owner-uploaded photos. Customers want to see your team in action, well-maintained vans, secure packing examples, and even smiling faces. Missing these visual proofs of professionalism, equipment, and trustworthiness makes it harder for potential clients to choose you over a competitor who clearly showcases their reliable service.
