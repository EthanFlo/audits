The three fixes on 80% of audited roofing contractor profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short and Generic

    Many Edmonton roofing contractors fail to utilize the full 750-character potential of their Google Business Profile description. Often, I find profiles with descriptions under 400 characters that are vague, missing key details about services like shingle replacement, flat roofs, or emergency repairs, and lack local keywords relevant to Edmonton neighbourhoods. This undercuts your ability to rank for specific services and clearly communicate your expertise to potential clients searching for detailed roofing solutions.

  • Crucial Secondary Categories Are Often Overlooked

    A significant blind spot for many Edmonton roofing contractors is neglecting secondary Google Business Profile categories. While "Roofing Contractor" is primary, missing crucial additions like "Roof Repair," "Emergency Roofing Service," and "Roof Inspection Service" limits your visibility. These additional categories act as powerful signals to Google, telling it exactly which specific services you offer and helping your business appear in more targeted local searches beyond just the general "roofing" term.

  • Insufficient Visual Proof of Your Expertise

    Most roofing contractor profiles in Edmonton I review fall well short of the ideal 30+ owner-uploaded photos. Instead, I see profiles lacking comprehensive visual proof of their work. Missing are images of completed projects (before/after), team members in action, vehicle branding, close-ups of specific materials used, and detailed photos of safety practices. These visuals are crucial for building trust and showcasing your professionalism to potential customers browsing for a reliable local roofer.
