Data Subject Rights

Last updated: 2026-05-05

Under Canadian PIPEDA, European GDPR, and Californian CCPA, you have the right to exercise the following controls over personal data we hold about you.

Your rights

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — request we fix inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request we delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — request your data in machine-readable format
  • Object — tell us to stop processing your data
  • Restrict — limit how we use your data (e.g. stop cold email only)

How to exercise these rights

Email: florendo.ethan@gmail.com

Subject line: include one of:

  • Data access request
  • Data correction request
  • Data deletion request
  • Data portability request
  • Object to processing

Include in the body: your email address (so we can look up what we hold), and any specific fields you want addressed.

Response time

We respond to all DSRs within 7 business days. We will not charge you for access or deletion requests.

What we hold (typical)

If you're a cold-email recipient: your business email, business name, category, website, and a timestamp. Nothing else.

If you chatted with us: your email, business name, message history, session ID. Kept for 90 days then auto-deleted.

If you purchased from us: your email, name, invoice history, project deliverable URLs. Kept for 7 years for Canadian tax compliance.

Fast-track opt-out

If you just want to stop receiving cold emails from us, reply STOP to any email. Your address is added to our opt-out ledger within 5 minutes and we will not email you again.
