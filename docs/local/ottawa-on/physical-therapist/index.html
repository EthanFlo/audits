The three fixes on 80% of audited physical therapist profiles

  • Incomplete Business Description

    Your Google Business Profile description is often overlooked, stuck under 400 characters, far short of the 750 Google allows. For Ottawa physical therapists, this is a missed opportunity to highlight your unique specialties like post-concussion therapy, chronic pain management, or specialized sports injury rehab. A detailed description helps both Google and potential patients understand your expertise, setting you apart from other clinics in the Glebe or Barrhaven areas seeking specific treatments.

  • Lack of Specific Service Categories

    Many Ottawa physical therapy practices only list "Physical Therapist" as their primary category, ignoring crucial secondary options. You're missing out on local searches for specific services. Think "Sports Medicine Clinic," "Rehabilitation Center," or "Orthopedic Physical Therapist" as powerful additions. Leveraging these helps your clinic appear for highly targeted searches, connecting you with patients actively looking for your specialized care in areas like Kanata or Orléans.

  • Insufficient Visual Content

    Your Google Business Profile likely has fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, significantly less than what engages potential patients. We often find practices missing visuals of their modern equipment, treatment rooms, welcoming reception area, or candid shots of therapists interacting with patients (with consent). High-quality photos showcasing your Ottawa clinic's environment, team, and the specific services offered build trust and give a clear impression of your professional, patient-focused practice.
