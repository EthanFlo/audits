The three fixes on 80% of audited dentist profiles

  • Your Business Description Isn't Telling Your Full Story

    Many Houston dentists don't realize Google Business Profile allows up to 750 characters for their business description, yet their current one is often under 400. This is a missed opportunity to highlight your practice's unique selling points. Your description should detail your philosophy, specialized services like advanced cosmetic procedures or gentle pediatric care, and your commitment to patient comfort. A truncated description leaves potential patients in Houston guessing, failing to convey why your practice is the best choice.

  • Crucial Secondary Categories Are Often Absent

    Beyond "Dentist," many profiles in Houston fail to utilize additional, highly relevant secondary categories. Google allows several, and optimizing these is vital for local visibility. I frequently find practices missing key categories like "Cosmetic Dentist," "Pediatric Dentist," and "Emergency Dental Service." Adding these helps Google understand the breadth of your services, allowing you to rank for more specific, high-intent searches from patients looking for specialized care, ultimately expanding your reach within the competitive Houston market.

  • Your Practice's Visuals Are Falling Short

    A surprising number of Houston dental practices have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos on their Google Business Profile. While patient reviews are important, visuals significantly enhance trust and appeal. I often see profiles missing essential images like inviting waiting areas, modern treatment rooms, smiling staff, the practice exterior, and clear branding. These visuals offer a vital glimpse into your environment, building confidence and helping patients feel more comfortable choosing you before they even pick up the phone.
