The three fixes on 80% of audited dermatologist profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many Durban dermatologists use a Google Business Profile description that's under 400 characters. Google allows up to 750, giving you crucial space to highlight your specialized services like advanced acne treatments, dermatological surgery, or pediatric dermatology. A short description often fails to convey your unique expertise, leaving potential patients to guess what makes your practice different and better than others in the Durban area.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    While "Dermatologist" is your primary category, neglecting secondary categories severely limits your visibility. I frequently find profiles missing vital additions like "Cosmetic Dermatology", "Skin Cancer Screening Service", and "Pediatric Dermatologist". Adding these precisely tells Google – and patients – the full breadth of services you offer, allowing you to rank for more specific and high-intent searches from Durban residents seeking particular treatments.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A surprising number of dermatologist profiles in Durban have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. Patients want to see your modern clinic interior, the friendly reception area, perhaps your state-of-the-art equipment, and professional team portraits (with consent). Missing are images of your consultation rooms, exterior signage, and even educational visual aids. These photos build trust and professionalism, helping patients feel comfortable before they even step through your door.
