The three fixes on 80% of audited roofing contractor profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many Oklahoma City roofing contractors use only a short, generic blurb for their Google Business Profile description. Google allows 750 characters, but I frequently see profiles under 400. This is a missed opportunity to detail your specific services, experience with OKC weather challenges, and why you’re the best choice for local homeowners. A comprehensive description tells both Google and potential clients exactly what you offer, enhancing relevance for specific searches like "storm damage roof repair OKC."

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    While "Roofing Contractor" is essential, many profiles stop there. I frequently find OKC roofers neglecting crucial secondary categories that significantly impact local search visibility. Optimizing your GBP means adding relevant categories like "Roof Repair," "Emergency Roofing Service," and "Roof Inspection Service." These additions ensure your business appears for a wider range of specific, high-intent local searches, connecting you with customers looking for precise solutions in Oklahoma City.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Google Business Profiles often lack sufficient owner-uploaded photos. For roofing contractors, this means missing vital visuals like before-and-after project comparisons showing storm damage remediation, your team safely at work on an Oklahoma City property, specific material installations (shingles, metal, TPO), and crew photos to build trust. A robust gallery of at least 30 high-quality images helps potential clients visualize your expertise and the quality of your work, making your profile far more compelling and credible.

  • Customer search behavior for roofing is often urgent and location-specific. After seeing a leak or storm damage, homeowners aren't browsing; they're looking for immediate, trustworthy solutions. An audit ensures your profile is meticulously accurate and rich with keywords for service areas across the metro, directly matching those high-intent queries. This precision prevents your business from being overlooked in critical moments when residents need a reliable OKC roofer fast, driving calls directly to you.
