The three fixes on 80% of audited veterinarian profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Calgary veterinarians limit their Google Business Profile description to a few generic sentences. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet I often find practices missing out on space to detail their unique services like advanced diagnostics, fear-free techniques, or specialized surgical procedures. This brevity fails to convey the full scope of your compassionate pet care and specialized offerings to potential clients.

  • Missing Secondary Service Categories

    Veterinary practices often default to just 'Veterinarian' as their primary category. However, I consistently discover that profiles lack crucial secondary categories like 'Emergency veterinarian,' 'Animal hospital,' or 'Pet boarding service.' These specific additions significantly broaden your visibility in targeted searches, helping pet owners in Calgary find you when they need specialized care beyond a routine check-up.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A visually engaging Google Business Profile significantly impacts how new clients perceive your practice. I frequently see veterinary profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. This means pet owners aren't seeing key visuals like your welcoming reception area, friendly veterinary team, state-of-the-art examination rooms, or happy pets interacting with staff. High-quality images build trust and convey the warm environment of your Calgary clinic.
