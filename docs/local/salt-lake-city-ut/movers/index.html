The three fixes on 80% of audited moving company profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Your Google Business Profile description is prime digital real estate, yet I often find Salt Lake City moving companies barely scratch the surface. Many use only a few sentences, missing out on crucial keywords and details. Google allows up to 750 characters to showcase your unique services, areas of expertise, and customer commitment, but most fall significantly short of the 400-character mark.

  • Missing Secondary Categories

    A common oversight for moving companies is only listing "Moving Company" as their primary category. Google allows up to nine additional categories that pinpoint your specialized services. I frequently see profiles missing critical secondary categories like "Long Distance Movers," "Office Relocation Service," or "Piano Movers," which limits specific search visibility in Salt Lake City.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A picture truly speaks a thousand words, especially for visually-driven services like moving. I often see profiles with only a handful of generic photos, leaving potential clients in Salt Lake City with little confidence. Images of your clean, branded trucks, professional movers in uniform, carefully packed items, before-and-after shots of packed homes, your office facade, and happy customers are essential to building trust.
