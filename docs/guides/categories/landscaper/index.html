Best Google Business categories for a landscaper

To truly dominate local search results, understanding and correctly utilizing Google Business categories is paramount for any landscaper. Your chosen categories act as "ranking lanes," telling Google exactly what services your business offers and which searches it should appear for. This dual system of a single primary category and up to nine secondary categories is your most powerful tool for local SEO.

Unfortunately, many landscapers make a critical error: they only assign a primary category to their Google Business Profile. This leaves a staggering nine potential "ranking lanes" empty, causing them to miss out on countless targeted customer searches. By strategically filling these secondary category slots, you can significantly broaden your visibility and capture a wider array of high-intent local queries.

The three fixes on 80% of audited landscaper profiles

Landscaper

Choosing "Landscaper" as your primary Google Business category is the most effective strategy for a business offering a comprehensive range of outdoor services. This broad, encompassing term signals to Google that your business is a general provider of landscaping services, allowing it to rank for a wide array of related searches. While more specific options like "Lawn care service" or "Hardscape contractor" exist, they are best reserved for secondary categories unless your business specializes exclusively in one area. "Landscaper" ensures you cast the widest net from the outset.

Why these matter specifically for landscapers in Categories

  1. Lawn care service: Unlocks searches related to mowing, fertilization, aeration, dethatching, and other routine grass maintenance.
  2. Hardscape contractor: Unlocks searches for designing and installing patios, walkways, retaining walls, fire pits, and other stone or concrete features.
  3. Tree service: Unlocks searches for tree trimming, pruning, removal, stump grinding, and general tree health maintenance.
  4. Landscape designer: Unlocks searches for professional planning, conceptualization, and aesthetic layout of outdoor spaces and gardens.
  5. Irrigation system contractor: Unlocks searches for installing, repairing, and maintaining sprinkler systems and drip irrigation.
  6. Pond contractor: Unlocks searches for building, maintaining, or repairing water features like koi ponds, waterfalls, and decorative fountains.
  7. Paver installer: Unlocks searches specifically for the installation of interlocking concrete pavers for driveways, patios, and paths.
  8. Garden designer: Unlocks searches for specialized design and planting plans for flower beds, vegetable gardens, and perennial borders.
  9. Landscape lighting designer: Unlocks searches for the design and installation of outdoor lighting systems, including pathway lights, uplighting, and accent lighting.
