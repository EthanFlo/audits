The three fixes on 80% of audited auto repair shop profiles

  • Your Google Business Profile Description is Too Short

    Many auto repair shops in Seattle miss a key opportunity to fully explain their expertise and services. Google allows up to 750 characters, yet most profiles use less than half. This means you are not detailing your specializations like classic car restoration, hybrid vehicle maintenance, or European import service, or highlighting your unique value proposition to potential customers actively looking for specific solutions in the Seattle area.

  • Neglecting Essential Secondary Categories

    Your primary category might be "Auto Repair Shop," but without specific secondary categories, you limit your visibility for crucial services. Many Seattle shops overlook options like "Oil Change Service," "Brake Shop," and "Tire Shop." These specific categories are exactly what local customers search for, directly impacting how often your business appears in relevant Google Maps and search results, especially for urgent needs.

  • An Underutilized Photo Gallery

    Google Business Profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos often miss out on significant customer engagement. For Seattle auto repair shops, this means not showcasing your clean service bays, specialized diagnostic equipment, skilled technicians at work, comfortable waiting area, or even examples of complex repairs completed. High-quality images build trust and help customers visualize bringing their vehicle to your shop before they even pick up the phone.
