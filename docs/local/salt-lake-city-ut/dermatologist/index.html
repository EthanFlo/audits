The three fixes on 80% of audited dermatologist profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Salt Lake City dermatologists condense their business description to a few generic sentences, barely scratching 400 characters when Google allows 750. This means you’re missing a vital opportunity to detail your specialized services, advanced treatments, or unique patient care philosophy. A robust description helps Google understand your full scope and attracts patients searching for specific dermatological needs beyond just 'dermatologist near me'.

  • Incomplete Service Categories

    A common issue I discover is dermatologists only using the primary 'Dermatologist' category. To truly dominate Salt Lake City searches, you need to leverage secondary categories. Examples include 'Cosmetic Dermatology,' 'Skin Cancer Screening Service,' and 'Pediatric Dermatology.' These specific tags help Google match your practice with highly targeted patient searches, ensuring you appear for critical services beyond general dermatology and capture a broader patient base.

  • Insufficient Visual Portfolio

    Your Google Business Profile should showcase your practice visually, yet many dermatologists in Salt Lake City have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. Patients want to see your modern clinic interior, welcoming reception, expert staff, and even the specialized equipment you use. High-quality images of treatment rooms, the exterior of your building, and patient education visuals build trust, convey professionalism, and significantly increase engagement with your profile before a patient even calls.
