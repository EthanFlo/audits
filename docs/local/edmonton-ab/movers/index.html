The three fixes on 80% of audited moving company profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Edmonton moving companies fail to maximize their Google Business Profile description, often leaving it under 400 characters when Google allows 750. This means missing crucial opportunities to highlight your specific services like local residential moves, packing assistance, or specialty item transport within the Edmonton area, letting potential customers skip over your profile before they even call.

  • Missing Secondary Categories

    Your main category might be "Moving Company," but without secondary categories, you’re invisible for specific searches. I frequently find profiles missing vital additions like "Long Distance Movers," "Office Relocation," and "Piano Movers." This oversight means customers searching for these exact services in Edmonton won't find you, even if you offer them.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A surprising number of Edmonton moving businesses have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, often missing key visuals. Customers want to see your clean, modern trucks, your professional, uniformed team in action, or images of your secure packing methods. Generic photos or relying solely on customer uploads fails to build trust and show off your unique service quality.
