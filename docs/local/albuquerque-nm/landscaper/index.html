The three fixes on 80% of audited landscaper profiles

  • Missing Your Full Story: Underutilized Business Description

    Google allows up to 750 characters in your business description, yet many Albuquerque landscapers are barely scratching 300. This prime real estate is where you tell prospective clients about your specialized services, your commitment to xeriscaping, or your passion for native New Mexico plants. A short description leaves vital details unsaid, making it harder for customers to choose you over a competitor.

  • Overlooking Key Service Categories

    Many profiles stop at just "Landscaper" as their primary category. This is a huge missed opportunity. Customers in Albuquerque specifically search for "Tree Service," "Hardscaping," or "Lawn Care" for their distinct needs. Without these crucial secondary categories, your profile won't appear in those highly targeted searches, directly impacting your visibility for valuable projects across the city.

  • Scarce Visual Proof of Your Craft

    Your Google Business Profile should be a dynamic portfolio, yet many landscapers have fewer than 10 owner-uploaded photos. We consistently find profiles lacking images of completed projects showcasing intricate flagstone patios, vibrant drought-tolerant gardens, or irrigation system installations. High-quality visuals are essential for capturing attention and demonstrating your expertise to homeowners actively seeking landscaping solutions in Albuquerque.
