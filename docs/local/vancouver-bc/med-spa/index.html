The three fixes on 80% of audited med spa profiles

  1. Underutilized Business Description

    Many Vancouver med spa profiles leave their Google Business Profile description severely underwritten, often using fewer than 400 of the available 750 characters. This means you're missing a crucial opportunity to weave in important keywords like "Botox Yaletown," "laser hair removal Kitsilano," or "dermal fillers Gastown." A robust, keyword-rich description helps Google understand precisely what services you offer and where, making it far easier for local clients to discover your clinic when they search for specific treatments in their neighbourhood.

  2. Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Your Google Business Profile allows for a primary category and multiple secondary categories, yet many med spas only list "Medical Spa." This is a huge oversight in a competitive market like Vancouver. Optimizing your secondary categories with specific services like "Laser Hair Removal Service," "Botox Clinic," and "Skin Care Clinic" ensures you appear in searches for those exact treatments. Without them, you're invisible to potential clients who know precisely what they're looking for.

  3. Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A surprising number of Vancouver med spa profiles lack a comprehensive visual presence, often with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. You’re missing chances to showcase your luxurious treatment rooms, state-of-the-art equipment, welcoming reception area, and especially your skilled team in action (with client permission, of course). High-quality photos build trust and set expectations. Patients want to see where they'll be treated and who will treat them, long before they book their first consultation.
