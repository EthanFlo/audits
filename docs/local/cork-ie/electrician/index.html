The three fixes on 80% of audited electrician profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short and Generic

    Many Cork electricians only use a few lines to describe their services, leaving valuable space unused. Google allows up to 750 characters, but I often see profiles with less than 400, missing opportunities to detail specialized services like fault finding, full rewires, or electrical safety inspections. A longer, keyword-rich description helps Google understand exactly what you offer, matching you with the right customers searching for specific electrical work across Cork city and county, instead of just general electrician queries.

  • Neglecting Crucial Secondary Categories

    Google Business Profile lets you add multiple categories beyond just "Electrician," yet many Cork businesses only list one. This oversight severely limits visibility for specialized searches. I frequently find profiles missing vital secondary categories like "Emergency Electrician," "EV Charging Station Installer," or "Electrical Panel Upgrade Service." Adding these specific classifications ensures you appear in niche searches, directly connecting you with Cork residents and businesses needing these specialized, high-value electrical services, rather than being overlooked.

  • Insufficient or Outdated Visual Content

    A vibrant photo gallery builds trust and showcases professionalism, but many Cork electrician profiles fall short with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded images. This means potential clients don't see your team in action, examples of recent wiring jobs, before-and-after fuse board upgrades, or photos of your branded vans. High-quality, diverse photos of completed projects, your workshop, and smiling staff can significantly influence a customer's decision to call your Cork electrical service over a competitor who appears less transparent or professional.

Their searches are often highly specific: 'emergency electrician Cork,' 'socket repair Douglas,' or 'EV charger installer Ballincollig.'
