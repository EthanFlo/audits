What the score means

  • 0-400 chars: Poor. You are leaving significant keyword space unused. Most small businesses hit this range.
  • 400-600 chars: OK. Decent coverage but could expand.
  • 600-749 chars: Good. Near-full coverage of keyword space.
  • 750 chars: Perfect. Maxed out.

Tips for writing to the 750-char limit

  1. Open with your primary service + city (e.g. "Emergency plumber serving Vancouver, BC since 2008")
  2. List 3-5 secondary services by name (Google uses these for long-tail queries)
  3. Include your service area radius or specific neighborhoods
  4. Mention years of experience or operational history
  5. Close with a specific differentiator (same-day turnaround, licensed, insured, etc.)
