The three fixes on 80% of audited auto repair shop profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many Austin auto repair shops miss a golden opportunity by leaving their Google Business Profile description too short. With up to 750 characters available, a brief description under 400 characters fails to fully articulate your specialized services, unique selling propositions, or the specific vehicle makes you service. This crucial text should detail everything from diagnostics and engine repair to AC service, giving potential customers a comprehensive overview before they even click away.

  • Overlooking Crucial Secondary Categories

    Your primary category is important, but neglecting secondary categories severely limits how many specific searches your Austin auto repair shop appears for. Many profiles I audit are missing vital classifications that Google uses to match search queries. Adding specific service categories like "Oil Change Service," "Brake Shop," and "Tire Shop" ensures you capture niche searches, making your business visible to customers with precise needs beyond just "auto repair."

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    In the visually driven world of online search, an auto repair shop with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos simply doesn't stand out. This often means missing shots of your clean, modern bays, certified technicians at work, welcoming waiting areas, or examples of completed repairs. High-quality images build trust and help customers visualize your shop's professionalism and capability, which is vital for attracting new business in Austin's competitive market.
