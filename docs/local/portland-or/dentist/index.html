The three fixes on 80% of audited dentist profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description (Often Under 400 Characters)

    Many Portland dental practices miss a huge opportunity by only using a fraction of the 750 characters available for their Google Business Profile description. Instead of just basic services, this space should detail your unique patient experience, advanced technology like digital X-rays, or specialization in family dentistry. A robust description helps Google understand exactly who you are, making your practice more relevant to specific patient searches in our community.

  • Overlooking Critical Secondary Categories

    Simply listing "Dentist" as your sole category severely limits your reach. An audit often reveals missing secondary categories that pinpoint your specialties. For Portland dentists, this means you might be missing patients searching specifically for a Cosmetic Dentist, Pediatric Dentist, or Emergency Dental Service. Properly assigning these categories connects you with precise patient needs, broadening your visibility significantly within the local search results.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos (Often Fewer Than 30)

    A strong visual presence builds trust and engagement, yet many dental profiles have fewer than 30 photos uploaded by the owner. We often find practices lacking crucial images like high-quality interior shots of reception and operatories, team photos showing friendly faces, or even before-and-after examples (where appropriate). These missing visuals help local patients visualize your practice and feel comfortable before their first visit.
