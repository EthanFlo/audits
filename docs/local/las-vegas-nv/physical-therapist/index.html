The three fixes on 80% of audited physical therapist profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many physical therapy profiles in Las Vegas fail to leverage the full 750 characters available for their business description, often settling for less than 400. This is a missed opportunity to include vital keywords related to specific conditions treated, unique therapeutic approaches, or specialized equipment. A concise description limits your ability to communicate your full value, preventing Google from understanding the depth of your services and matching you with patients actively searching for those precise solutions.

  • Missing Critical Secondary Categories

    Google Business Profile allows for multiple categories, yet many Las Vegas physical therapists only list "Physical Therapy Clinic." This oversight means you’re not appearing for highly specific patient searches. Key secondary categories like "Sports Physical Therapy," "Post-Surgical Rehabilitation," and "Neurological Physical Therapy" are often overlooked. Without these, your clinic won't surface when patients search for specialized care, directly hindering your ability to capture niche markets and attract a broader range of patients.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    We frequently find physical therapy profiles with fewer than 30 high-quality, owner-uploaded images. Patients want to see your modern equipment, the welcoming clinic interior, and friendly staff. Missing are photos showcasing specialized treatment areas, patient success stories (with consent), or the clinic exterior. A lack of visual content reduces trust and appeal, leaving potential patients with an incomplete picture of your practice and its unique services.
