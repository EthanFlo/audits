The three fixes on 80% of audited chiropractor profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many Cork chiropractors leave their Google Business Profile description significantly underwritten, often using fewer than 400 characters when Google permits up to 750. This missed opportunity means you're not fully communicating your specialisations like scoliosis treatment, sciatica relief, or pediatric chiropractic care. A complete, keyword-rich description helps Google understand the breadth of your services, making you more visible for specific patient searches beyond just "chiropractor near me."

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Limiting your profile to just "Chiropractor" severely restricts your reach. Many Cork clinics are missing crucial secondary categories that attract diverse patient groups. I often find profiles lacking categories like "Sports Chiropractor," essential for athletes recovering from injuries; "Auto Accident Chiropractor," for those needing post-collision care; or "Family Chiropractor," appealing to parents seeking care for children and themselves. Adding these broadens your appeal significantly.

  • Lack of Engaging Visuals

    A surprising number of chiropractor profiles in Cork have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, far below the optimal number. Patients want to see your clinic before they visit. Common missing visuals include photos of your welcoming reception area, clean treatment rooms, advanced equipment like adjustment tables or X-ray machines, and professional headshots of your team. High-quality images build trust and give potential patients a better sense of your practice's atmosphere and capabilities.
