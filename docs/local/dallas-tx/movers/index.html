The three fixes on 80% of audited moving company profiles

*

Your Google Business Profile Description is Too Short

Many Dallas moving companies leave valuable space blank in their Google Business Profile description. Google allows 750 characters, yet most use less than 400. This is a missed opportunity to include essential keywords like "apartment movers Dallas," "commercial relocation," or "long distance moving from Dallas." Your description should detail your unique services, highlight your insured status, and list the specific DFW areas you serve, telling both customers and Google exactly what you offer. *

You're Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

Beyond the primary "Moving Company" category, many Dallas movers fail to utilize secondary categories. These are vital for capturing niche searches. I often find profiles that could benefit from adding specific categories like 'Long-Distance Moving Service,' 'Office Relocation Service,' and 'Piano Moving Service.' Without these, you're invisible to potential clients specifically seeking those specialized moving solutions across the Dallas area. *

A Lack of High-Quality, Owner-Uploaded Photos

Your Google Business Profile should act as a visual portfolio, yet many Dallas moving companies upload fewer than 30 owner-provided photos. We commonly see profiles missing clear images of their modern moving fleet, their uniformed and friendly crew in action, safely packed boxes, or even testimonials displayed visually. These visuals build trust, showcase professionalism, and tell a powerful story to Dallas residents considering your moving services.
