The three fixes on 80% of audited law firm profiles

  • Incomplete Business Description

    Many Melbourne law firms use less than half of the allowed 750 characters for their Google Business Profile description. This is a significant missed opportunity to showcase your specific legal expertise, such as commercial litigation, property law, or criminal defence, and communicate what differentiates your practice. A comprehensive description allows potential clients to quickly assess if your services align with their urgent legal needs.

  • Overlooking Crucial Secondary Categories

    I frequently find law firms listing only "Law Firm" as their primary category. Google allows up to nine secondary categories, and you're missing out on vital search visibility by not using them. For Melbourne practices, I recommend adding specific categories like Personal Injury Lawyer, Family Law Attorney, and Estate Planning Attorney to precisely target clients searching for specialised legal assistance.

  • Scarcity of High-Quality Photos

    Your Google Business Profile often features fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, if any. Potential clients want to see your professional office space in Melbourne, your welcoming reception area, and even team photos that convey trust and approachability. Showcase your dedicated meeting rooms, a well-stocked library, and the professional environment where clients will discuss sensitive legal matters.
