The three fixes on 80% of audited plumber profiles

*

Incomplete Business Description

Many Columbus plumbing profiles underutilize the Google Business Profile description, often leaving it well under the 750-character limit. You might only have a brief 200-character blurb, missing a crucial opportunity to detail your specialized services like water heater repair, drain cleaning, or emergency plumbing across areas like Dublin or Gahanna. This oversight means Google has less text to match your business with specific, high-intent customer searches.

*

Missing Secondary Categories

A surprising number of local plumbers only list "Plumber" as their single category. This is a missed chance to tell Google exactly what specific services you excel at. For Columbus plumbers, I often find profiles missing vital secondary categories such as "Emergency Plumber," "Drain Cleaning Service," and "Water Heater Installation Service." Without these, you're less likely to appear for those targeted, high-value searches.

*

Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

Your Google Business Profile should be a visual showcase of your professionalism and work. I frequently see Columbus plumber profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, often missing key visuals. Customers want to see photos of your clean, branded vans, your licensed technicians in uniform, images of completed jobs (before/after), and perhaps even your professional tools or office space. Visual trust signals are incredibly important.
