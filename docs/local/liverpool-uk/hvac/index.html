The three fixes on 80% of audited hvac contractor profiles

Your Google Business Profile Description is Too Short

Many HVAC contractors in Liverpool leave their description under 400 characters, missing a huge opportunity. Google allows up to 750 characters to detail your expertise in boiler servicing, heat pump installations, and emergency repairs. This crucial text helps both Google and potential customers understand your full range of services, boosting your relevance for specific searches across Merseyside.

You're Missing Crucial Secondary Service Categories

Most profiles correctly list "HVAC contractor" as primary, but fail to add secondary categories. This severely limits who sees your business. We often find profiles in Liverpool could add "Air conditioning repair service", "Furnace repair service", and "Duct cleaning service" to capture more specific searches. These specific categories directly impact your visibility for essential customer needs.

Not Enough High-Quality, Relevant Photos

Customers want to see who they're hiring and your quality of work. Many HVAC profiles in Liverpool have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, often missing key visuals. We look for photos of your team on jobs, branded vans, completed installations like a new boiler or AC unit, and even your workshop. These visuals build trust and showcase professionalism.
