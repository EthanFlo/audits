The three fixes on 80% of audited veterinarian profiles

  • Limited Business Description

    Many Jacksonville veterinarian profiles I review only use a fraction of the available space for their business description, often falling below 400 characters. Google allows up to 750 characters, a crucial opportunity to detail your specialized services like advanced diagnostics, preventative care, or specific pet surgeries that set your practice apart from others in the area.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    Beyond the primary 'Veterinarian' category, many practices overlook vital secondary options that broaden their reach. I frequently find profiles missing categories like 'Animal Hospital,' 'Emergency Veterinarian,' or 'Pet Surgery.' Adding these ensures you appear in more targeted searches, connecting you with pet owners seeking specific services in Jacksonville.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Most profiles I audit have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, often missing crucial visual elements. We need more than just a logo. Pet owners in Jacksonville want to see your clean examination rooms, friendly staff interacting with animals, state-of-the-art equipment, and perhaps even 'behind-the-scenes' shots to build trust and familiarity before their first visit.
