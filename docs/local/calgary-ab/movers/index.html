The three fixes on 80% of audited moving company profiles

  • Your business description is a missed opportunity.

    Many moving companies in Calgary leave their Google Business Profile description short and unoptimized. Google allows up to 750 characters, but I often find profiles with less than 400. This is valuable space to highlight your unique selling points, services like packing and storage, or specialized equipment, and include keywords that Calgary residents use when searching for movers. A robust description helps Google understand your full service offering better.

  • You're missing crucial secondary categories.

    Your primary category might be 'Moving Company,' but many Calgary movers aren't leveraging secondary categories. These tell Google exactly what other services you offer, expanding your reach. I frequently see profiles that could benefit from adding 'Long Distance Movers,' 'Office Relocation Service,' or 'Piano Mover,' depending on their specialties. These specific categories help you appear in more targeted local searches, attracting customers looking for precise moving solutions.

  • Your photo gallery is underperforming.

    A visually appealing Google Business Profile attracts more clicks, yet many moving companies in Calgary have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos. Customers want to see your team in action, clean and well-maintained trucks, professional packing materials, and even your storage facilities. Missing are images of your crew safely handling furniture, testimonials with faces, or your uniformed team on a job. More high-quality, relevant photos build trust and showcase professionalism.

Customer search behavior for moving companies in Calgary is highly intent-driven. People searching for movers are typically close to making a booking decision. A profile rich with specific categories, detailed descriptions, and plenty of visual proof directly addresses these needs, converting high-intent searchers into actual leads.

Moreover, Calgary's spread-out nature means trust and local reputation are paramount. Homeowners and businesses alike want to feel confident in their choice, knowing their belongings are safe. A comprehensive and active Google Business Profile with abundant, high-quality photos and detailed information acts as a strong signal of trustworthiness and legitimacy, helping your Calgary moving company build that crucial local confidence before they even pick up the phone.
