The three fixes on 80% of audited physical therapist profiles

  • Undersized Business Description

    Many physical therapist profiles in Liverpool miss a crucial opportunity by using a business description well under Google's 750-character limit. Instead of a detailed narrative highlighting your specialist treatments like manual therapy, injury rehabilitation, or post-operative care, many descriptions are brief and uninformative. This oversight means you’re failing to communicate your full value proposition and unique selling points to potential patients actively searching for specific pain relief or recovery services in the area.

  • Neglecting Secondary Categories

    A common finding is physical therapy profiles only listing "Physical Therapist" as their primary category. This seriously limits visibility. Google allows for multiple secondary categories that can dramatically expand your reach. I often see practices in Liverpool missing vital additions like "Sports Medicine Clinic," "Rehabilitation Centre," or "Pain Management Clinic." These specific categories help you appear in more nuanced local searches, connecting you with patients looking for very particular types of treatment.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Most Liverpool physical therapist profiles fall short of the recommended 30+ owner-uploaded photos, often featuring just a few generic shots. Patients want to see your actual clinic, treatment rooms, equipment, and your friendly team. Missing are engaging photos demonstrating specific exercises, your reception area, client testimonials (with consent), and interior shots that convey a professional, welcoming environment. These visuals build trust and help potential patients envision their experience with you.
