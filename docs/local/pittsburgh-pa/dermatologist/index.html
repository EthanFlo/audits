The three fixes on 80% of audited dermatologist profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many dermatologist profiles in Pittsburgh fail to maximize their Google Business Profile description, often coming in under 400 characters when Google allows up to 750. This means crucial details about your specific services – like advanced mole mapping, specialized acne treatments, or aesthetic procedures such as injectables and laser therapy – are often left unmentioned. A detailed description helps Pittsburgh patients understand your unique offerings, making your practice stand out from general dermatology clinics.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    A common oversight is not including relevant secondary categories on your profile. While "Dermatologist" is essential, many practices miss opportunities to be found for specific services. For dermatologists in Pittsburgh, adding categories like "Cosmetic Dermatologist," "Skin Cancer Treatment," or "Pediatric Dermatology" can significantly broaden your reach. These specific keywords help you appear in more targeted searches, connecting you with patients looking for very particular types of care.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Profiles often have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, which is a missed opportunity to visually showcase your practice. Patients in Pittsburgh want to see more than just a logo. Missing images typically include professional shots of your clinic's inviting waiting area, modern treatment rooms, specialized equipment, and friendly staff. High-quality photos build trust and give potential patients a clear sense of your environment and the professionalism of your team before they even visit.
