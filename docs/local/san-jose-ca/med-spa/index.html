The three fixes on 80% of audited med spa profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many San Jose med spas use a brief, often generic business description under 400 characters, missing a vital opportunity. Google allows up to 750 characters. This expanded space is crucial for detailing your unique services like advanced laser treatments, custom facial protocols, or body contouring, helping potential clients understand your specialties beyond a simple service list and improving keyword relevance for local searches.

  • Missing Critical Secondary Categories

    It's common for med spa profiles to only list "Medical Spa" as their primary category, overlooking valuable secondary categories. Google uses these to match highly specific searches. Failing to include categories such as "Botox Clinic," "Laser Hair Removal Service," or "Microneedling Service" means your San Jose med spa misses out on direct visibility when customers search for those exact treatments, funneling potential clients to competitors.

  • Insufficient Owner-Uploaded Photos

    A striking number of med spa profiles have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, relying instead on generic stock images or client contributions. In a visual industry, this is a significant drawback. We often find profiles missing high-quality images of inviting treatment rooms, professional staff, detailed before-and-after results, or product displays, failing to showcase your San Jose facility and expertise effectively to browsing clients.
