The three fixes on 80% of audited moving company profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Google allows for a detailed 750-character business description, but many moving companies in Halifax leave theirs woefully short. This is a missed opportunity to showcase your specific expertise, whether it's local Halifax moves, long-distance services across Nova Scotia or the Maritimes, specialized packing, or secure storage solutions. A robust description rich with keywords helps Google understand your full range of services and match you with more relevant customer searches, directly impacting your visibility.

  • Missing Key Secondary Categories

    While "Moving Company" is your primary category, neglecting secondary categories severely limits your reach. For Halifax movers, categories like "Long Distance Movers," "Office Relocation Service," or "Piano Mover" are crucial. These specialized categories ensure you appear for specific, high-intent searches from customers needing those exact services, rather than just generic moving queries. Correctly assigning these tells Google you're a perfect match for those precise needs.

  • Not Enough High-Quality Owner Photos

    Most moving company profiles in Halifax lack a sufficient number of owner-uploaded photos – ideally, you should have over 30. Customers want to see your professional team in branded uniforms, your well-maintained trucks, and specialized equipment. Missing are images of wrapped furniture, safe loading practices, or even happy customers. These visual proofs build trust and showcase your professionalism, making your business more appealing and trustworthy at a glance.
