The three fixes on 80% of audited chiropractor profiles

  • Missing Opportunities in Your Business Description

    Many Christchurch chiropractors only use a few lines to describe their practice, missing out on crucial keywords and selling points. Google allows up to 750 characters, giving you ample space to highlight your unique approach, specializations like sports injury rehab or family wellness, and what sets your practice apart for patients seeking relief in our city. This underutilized space is vital for search ranking and attracting the right patients.

  • Overlooking Essential Secondary Categories

    Your primary category might be 'Chiropractor,' but many Christchurch practices stop there. This is a significant oversight. Google allows several secondary categories that precisely tell Google and potential patients what else you offer. Key categories like 'Sports Chiropractor,' 'Family Chiropractor,' or 'Auto Accident Chiropractor' help you appear in more targeted searches, ensuring you reach residents specifically looking for these services across Christchurch.

  • Insufficient Visual Storytelling Through Photos

    Google Business Profiles with fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos often signal a missed opportunity for Christchurch chiropractors. You’re likely missing crucial images: a welcoming reception area, treatment rooms, your team, patient testimonials (with permission), and exterior shots showing accessibility and parking. High-quality photos build trust and give prospective patients a vital peek into your practice before they even call, making them more likely to choose you.
