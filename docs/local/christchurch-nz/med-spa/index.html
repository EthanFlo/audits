The three fixes on 80% of audited med spa profiles

  • Underutilised Business Description

    Many Christchurch med spas miss out by having a Google Business Profile description under 400 characters, when Google allows 750. This means you're not fully utilising a key space to explain your unique selling points, specialist treatments like advanced facials or body sculpting, and the luxurious client experience you offer. A detailed description helps Google and potential clients understand precisely what makes your clinic stand out.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Without specific secondary categories, your med spa won't appear for many targeted searches. Common omissions include categories like "Botox clinic," "Laser hair removal service," or "Microneedling service." If these aren't explicitly listed, Google won’t connect your business with Christchurch residents actively searching for these high-demand treatments, leading to missed client opportunities and reduced local visibility.

  • Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Christchurch clients want to see results and feel trust before booking, yet many med spa profiles lack sufficient imagery. This often means missing crucial before-and-after galleries, detailed shots of your state-of-the-art equipment, welcoming treatment rooms, or your friendly, expert staff in action. These visual cues are essential for demonstrating professionalism, building confidence, and attracting local med spa clientele.
