The three fixes on 80% of audited real estate agent profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many real estate agents in Auckland leave valuable Google Business Profile description space blank, often using under 400 characters when Google allows up to 750. This means you’re missing a crucial opportunity to include relevant keywords like "Auckland property valuation," "commercial real estate," or "first-home buyer assistance." A robust description helps Google understand your specific services and target audience, improving your chances of ranking for diverse and specific local searches across Auckland's competitive market.

  • You're Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Beyond simply "Real Estate Agent," your profile likely lacks specific secondary categories that tell Google (and potential clients) exactly what you specialise in. During an audit, I often find agents have overlooked vital options like "Buyer's Agent," "Seller's Agent," or "Property Manager." Adding these precise categories is essential for matching your services directly with highly specific customer searches, especially within Auckland's diverse property segments, from central apartments to suburban family homes.

  • Not Enough Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Google recommends a minimum of 30 owner-uploaded photos, yet many real estate agents in Auckland fall far short. You're likely missing professional headshots, team photos, images of your office interior/exterior, or compelling "sold" signs in prominent Auckland neighbourhoods. Crucially, images of happy clients (with permission) and local landmarks around your serviced areas build trust and showcase local expertise. These visuals are vital for creating a strong first impression and reassuring potential clients.
