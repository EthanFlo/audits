The three fixes on 80% of audited dermatologist profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short

    Many Gold Coast dermatologists only use a few sentences in their Google Business Profile description, leaving valuable space on the table. Google allows up to 750 characters, but I often see profiles using less than 400. This is a missed opportunity to detail your specialized services like advanced skin cancer screenings, cosmetic injectables, or pediatric dermatology, and to highlight your unique patient approach or clinic amenities. A comprehensive description helps both Google and potential patients understand your full scope of practice.

  • Missing Crucial Secondary Categories

    Your primary category might be "Dermatologist," but Google allows you to add up to nine additional categories to accurately represent your practice. I frequently find Gold Coast dermatology profiles missing vital secondary categories like "Cosmetic Dermatology," "Skin Cancer Screening Service," or "Pediatric Dermatology." Without these, you're invisible for highly specific, high-intent searches. Properly categorizing your services ensures you appear for the precise conditions and treatments potential patients are actively seeking, significantly boosting your local visibility.

  • Too Few Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Dermatology is a visual field, yet many Gold Coast clinics upload fewer than 30 photos when ideally you should have many more. I often find profiles missing essential images of the clinic's inviting waiting area, treatment rooms, your friendly team, or even specific equipment. High-quality photos of your reception, consulting rooms, and medical staff build crucial trust and give patients a clear visual expectation before their visit. More engaging and informative visuals significantly enhance your profile's appeal.

Gold Coast boasts a vibrant, growing population, which unfortunately also means a high density of competing dermatologists. When searching for a dermatologist, they're typically looking for "dermatologist near me" or "skin cancer clinic [suburb name]". A fully optimized profile, complete with accurate categories and a detailed description, ensures you appear for these hyper-local searches, capturing patients specifically looking within their immediate commute zone, rather than losing them to a clinic across town.

Gold Coast patients are looking for clinics that exude professionalism, trust, and a welcoming environment. The photos on your Google Business Profile are crucial here; they provide a vital first impression. Missing images of your team and clinic interior can make a practice feel less approachable or established, potentially deterring patients who are already feeling vulnerable and seeking reassurance before their first visit.
