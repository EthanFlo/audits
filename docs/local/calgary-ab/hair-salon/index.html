The three fixes on 80% of audited hair salon profiles

  • Your Business Description is Too Short and Vague

    Many Calgary hair salons use fewer than 400 characters for their Google Business Profile description, wasting valuable space. Google allows up to 750 characters, giving you ample room to detail your unique styling philosophy, signature services like balayage or precision cuts, and your salon's inviting atmosphere. A short description misses crucial keywords and opportunities to connect with potential clients seeking specific styles or experiences, leaving them to scroll past.

  • Untapped Potential: Missing Secondary Categories

    Overlooking secondary categories is a common audit finding. While "Hair Salon" is essential, Google offers specific categories that precisely describe your expertise. Many Calgary salons miss opportunities by not adding categories like "Hair Colorist," "Barber Shop" for men's services, or "Bridal Shop" if you offer wedding styling. Adding these helps your salon appear in more nuanced searches, attracting clients looking for specialized services you already provide.

  • Scant Visuals: Fewer Than 30 Owner-Uploaded Photos

    Your Google Business Profile should be a visual showcase, yet many Calgary hair salons have under 30 owner-uploaded photos. Missing are high-quality images of recent client transformations, your stylish interior, skilled stylists in action, and even product displays. Clients want to see your work and the salon vibe before booking. A profile lacking vibrant, updated visuals fails to inspire confidence or demonstrate the quality and range of your services.

Why these matter specifically for hair salons in Calgary, AB

Calgary's hair salon scene is vibrant and competitive, spanning bustling downtown districts to busy suburban communities. With numerous excellent salons vying for attention, your Google Business Profile isn't just a listing; it's a vital differentiator. A fully optimized profile helps you cut through the noise, ensuring that when a potential client searches for 'hair salons Calgary,' your business is among the first they see, not lost in the endless scroll.

Calgarians often value convenience, but will also travel for specialized services. For daily cuts, clients typically search by proximity, like 'haircut near me SE Calgary'. However, for a major color correction or bridal styling, they might broaden their search but still rely on strong local signals to find a true specialist. An optimized profile with rich descriptions and specific secondary categories directly addresses both these search behaviors, guiding clients seeking either convenience or expert skill straight to your door.

Customer search behavior for hair salons is highly visual. Before booking, Calgarians want to see the quality of your work, the cleanliness of your space, and the general ambiance. A robust gallery of owner-uploaded photos on your Google Business Profile builds immediate trust and showcases your unique style, acting as a powerful online portfolio. Without compelling visuals, potential clients are more likely to choose a competitor whose profile vividly demonstrates their expertise and inviting atmosphere.
