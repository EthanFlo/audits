The three fixes on 80% of audited dermatologist profiles

  • Your Google Business Profile Description is Underutilized

    Many Sydney dermatologists only scratch the surface with their Google Business Profile description. Often, it's a brief 200-300 character blurb, missing the chance to inform and attract. Google allows up to 750 characters, giving you ample space to detail your expertise in cosmetic procedures, skin cancer detection, or specialized pediatric dermatology, directly appealing to patients searching for your specific skills.

    Beyond 'Dermatologist', many profiles fail to include secondary categories crucial for discovery. Without listing specific services like 'Cosmetic Dermatology', 'Skin Cancer Screening Service', or 'Pediatric Dermatologist', your practice misses out on highly relevant local searches. These categories are vital signals to Google, helping connect your expertise with the exact needs of patients across Sydney suburbs.

    In a visual world, profiles with under 30 owner-uploaded photos often underperform. Patients want to see your modern clinic, the friendly reception area, perhaps even anonymized 'before and after' results, or your team. Generic stock images or just a logo don't build trust. Missing photos of your sterile examination rooms or advanced equipment leaves a void that discourages potential patients from choosing your practice.
