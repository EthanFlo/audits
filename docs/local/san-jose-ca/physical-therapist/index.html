The three fixes on 80% of audited physical therapist profiles

  • Underutilized Business Description

    Many San Jose physical therapists underutilize their Google Business Profile description. I often find profiles with less than 400 characters, missing opportunities to highlight specialized rehabilitation programs, advanced pain management techniques, or unique sports injury recovery expertise. Google allows 750 characters. Expanding this space lets you thoroughly explain what makes your practice ideal for patients seeking relief and recovery in the local area, improving visibility for specific services.

  • Incomplete Service Categorization

    Many San Jose physical therapists miss key secondary categories. Beyond "Physical Therapist," profiles often lack specific service categories like "Sports Medicine Clinic," "Rehabilitation Center," or "Pediatric Physical Therapy." These are vital for attracting patients searching for specialized care. Adding them ensures your practice appears in more targeted local searches, reaching the exact audience needing your unique services and boosting local search relevance.

  • Lack of Engaging Visuals

    Many San Jose physical therapy profiles have fewer than 30 owner-uploaded photos, significantly limiting patient engagement. Practices often miss opportunities to showcase modern treatment rooms, specialized equipment like anti-gravity treadmills, and staff interactions. High-quality images of your clinic's atmosphere, therapy sessions (with consent), and patient success stories build trust. They give prospective patients a vital visual understanding of your practice, encouraging them to book appointments.
