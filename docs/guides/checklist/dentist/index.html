30-point Google Business Profile checklist for dentists

This 30-point Google Business Profile checklist offers dentists a precise, actionable roadmap to optimize their online presence. Forget vague advice; this guide provides the exact steps needed to enhance visibility and attract more patients directly through Google Search and Maps. Each item is a specific task, designed to be ticked off systematically, transforming a slow afternoon into a powerful SEO session.

Unlike general marketing tips, a checklist provides clarity and accountability, ensuring no critical element is overlooked. Think of this as your practice's blueprint for digital success, guiding you through the essential configurations and ongoing management tasks for your Google Business Profile. By following this sequence, dentists can significantly improve their local search rankings, making it easier for new patients to discover and choose their services.

The three fixes on 80% of audited dentist profiles

Business Info

  • ☐ Verify your business name matches your legal registration exactly, avoiding unnecessary keywords.
  • ☐ Ensure your address and phone number are consistent across your website and all directories.
  • ☐ Add your primary website URL and a direct link to your online appointment booking system.
  • ☐ Accurately set your clinic's operating hours, including any special holiday schedules or extended evening hours.
  • ☐ Confirm your service area, especially if offering mobile services or Emergency Dental home visits.

Categories

  • ☐ Select "Dentist" as your primary business category to rank for core dental searches.
  • ☐ Add "Cosmetic Dentist" if a significant portion of your practice focuses on aesthetic procedures like veneers.
  • ☐ Include "Pediatric Dentist" as a secondary category if you specialize in treating children's oral health.
  • ☐ Select "Emergency Dental Service" if you provide urgent care for dental trauma or severe pain.
  • ☐ Review and add other relevant secondary categories such as "Orthodontist" or "Periodontist" as applicable.

Photos

  • ☐ Upload a professional, well-lit exterior photo showing your clinic's street view for easy recognition.
  • ☐ Add high-quality interior photos of your waiting room, front desk, and clean treatment rooms.
  • ☐ Include engaging team photos to introduce your staff and build trust and approachability with potential patients.
  • ☐ Showcase before-and-after photos for Cosmetic Dentist procedures, with explicit patient consent, demonstrating results.
  • ☐ Upload a short video tour of your practice to give patients a dynamic sense of your environment.

Services

  • ☐ List all core dental services clearly, such as cleanings, fillings, extractions, and routine check-ups.
  • ☐ Detail specialized services like dental implants, Invisalign, and teeth whitening, emphasizing benefits.
  • ☐ Create a specific service entry for "Emergency Dental Care," outlining common issues you address urgently.
  • ☐ Add a "Pediatric Dentistry" service, detailing child-friendly procedures and your approach to young patients.
  • ☐ Describe payment options, accepted insurance plans, or financing solutions within your service descriptions.

Reviews

  • ☐ Respond promptly and professionally to every new patient review, both positive and constructive feedback.
  • ☐ Actively encourage satisfied patients to leave Google reviews, perhaps with a gentle follow-up email.
  • ☐ Create and prominently display a direct Google review link in your clinic and on your website for convenience.
  • ☐ Monitor for and report any spam, fake, or clearly malicious reviews to Google for investigation and removal.
  • ☐ Highlight positive testimonials on your clinic's website or social media channels to reinforce your reputation.

Posts

  • ☐ Create a "What's New" post announcing a new service, like advanced laser dentistry or new cosmetic options.
  • ☐ Use an "Offer" post to promote seasonal specials on teeth whitening or new patient examinations.
  • ☐ Share valuable dental health tips or educational content via an "Update" post, e.g., "The Importance of Flossing."
  • ☐ Announce upcoming clinic events, such as a free dental health seminar or a community outreach day, using an "Event" post.
  • ☐ Add a clear call-to-action button, like "Book Appointment" or "Learn More," to every relevant Google Post.

Why these matter specifically for dentists in Checklist

After successfully checking off all 30 points, the next crucial step is ongoing monitoring and refinement. Utilize the "Insights" section within your Google Business Profile dashboard to track key metrics like views, searches, and actions (calls, website visits, direction requests). Allow at least 14 days for Google's algorithms to fully process and reflect these changes in search rankings. Following this period, review your performance data and repeat the checklist process annually, or whenever significant changes occur in your practice, to ensure continuous optimization and competitive edge.
